### Prompt 10 – WhatsApp Broadcast Script: “Daily Gratitude Practice”

Backstory: WhatsApp and Telegram groups are widely used in Singapore for community updates. A short, daily gratitude prompt delivered on WhatsApp helps maintain engagement and personal connection between the coach and followers.

Goal: Create a 30-day gratitude message plan for WhatsApp broadcast.

Prompt:

 “You are a community manager for a spiritual coach. Create a 30-day WhatsApp gratitude series.

  - Each message under 60 words.

  - Include 1 gratitude question and 1 affirmation daily.

  - Use simple, warm tone.”

Inputs:

  - Duration of series

  - Tone style (casual/spiritual)

  - Engagement type (poll, reply, reflection)
