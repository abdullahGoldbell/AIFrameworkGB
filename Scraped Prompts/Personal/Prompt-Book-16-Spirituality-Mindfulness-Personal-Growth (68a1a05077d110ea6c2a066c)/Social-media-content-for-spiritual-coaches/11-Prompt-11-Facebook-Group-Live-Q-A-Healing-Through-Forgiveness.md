### Prompt 11 – Facebook Group Live Q&A: “Healing Through Forgiveness”

Backstory: Many followers in Singapore’s spiritual communities hold onto resentment due to family or workplace conflicts. A live Q&A offers them a safe space to ask questions directly to the coach, fostering deeper connection and trust.

Goal: Plan a 45-minute Facebook Live Q&A session on forgiveness techniques.

Prompt:

 “You are an event planner for a spiritual coach. Create a 45-minute Facebook Live Q&A plan on the theme ‘Healing Through Forgiveness’.

  - Include opening story, 5 guiding questions, interactive polls, and a closing meditation.

  - Suggest 3 promotional posts for the group before the event.”

Inputs:

  - Audience demographics
