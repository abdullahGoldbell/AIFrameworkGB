**Prompt 21 – Visualization for Overcoming Fear of Change Using Koi Fish & River Symbolism**

Backstory: Amelia, 29, from Tampines, is about to relocate overseas for a postgraduate programme. She feels anxious about the unknown. She recalls the koi pond at the Chinese Garden where the fish always swam calmly despite shifting currents and wants this symbolism in her visualization.

Goal: Write a visualization exercise that builds courage to embrace life changes using river and koi fish imagery.

Prompt:

 "You are a life transition coach. Write a 15-minute guided visualization using koi fish and flowing river symbolism to inspire courage during major life changes."

Inputs:

  - Type of change happening

  - Preferred pace of narration

  - Sensory imagery preference
