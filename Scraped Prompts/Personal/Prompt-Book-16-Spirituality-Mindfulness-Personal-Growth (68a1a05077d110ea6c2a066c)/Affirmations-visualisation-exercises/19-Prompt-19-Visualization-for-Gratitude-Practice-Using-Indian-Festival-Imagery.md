**Prompt 19 – Visualization for Gratitude Practice Using Indian Festival Imagery**

Backstory: Meera, 32, living in Singapore, often focuses on what’s missing in her life. Her therapist suggested daily gratitude practice. Meera feels most alive during Deepavali when the house is lit and Little India glows with festive lights, and she wants a visualization that brings that warmth and abundance into her everyday mindset.

Goal: Write a gratitude visualization using imagery from Indian festivals to inspire joy and contentment.

Prompt:

 "You are a gratitude coach. Create a 12-minute guided visualization for daily gratitude practice using imagery from Indian festivals like Deepavali or Pongal."

Inputs:

  - Favourite festival

  - Number of gratitude points to focus on

  - Preferred tone (festive / calm)
