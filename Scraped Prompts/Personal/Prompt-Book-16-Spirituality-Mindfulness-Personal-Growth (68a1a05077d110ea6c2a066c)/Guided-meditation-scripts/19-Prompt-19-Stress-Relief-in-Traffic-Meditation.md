### Prompt 19 – Stress Relief in Traffic Meditation

Backstory: Peak-hour congestion on the PIE, CTE, or AYE — and crowded MRT carriages on the East-West Line — are a daily reality for many commuters in Singapore, causing frustration and stress. This short meditation can be done in a stationary vehicle or on the train to release tension and maintain calm during unavoidable delays.

Goal: Create a commute-specific stress relief meditation.

Prompt:

 “You are a mindfulness trainer. Write a 5-minute guided meditation for stress relief during traffic jams or crowded commutes.

  - Focus on breath awareness and body relaxation.

  - Add visualizations of open roads or calming scenery.

  - End with a positive intention for the day.”

Inputs:

  - Duration

  - Imagery choice

  - Tone (humorous, calming)
