### Prompt 15 – Meditation for Resilience During Challenges

Backstory: Life in Singapore, especially in highly competitive academic and corporate environments, often involves setbacks. Building resilience is key to bouncing back without losing motivation. A guided resilience meditation can provide mental strength, adaptability, and emotional balance during tough phases.

Goal: Write a meditation that boosts resilience and mental strength.

Prompt:

 “You are a resilience coach. Create a 12-minute guided meditation for overcoming challenges.

  - Use breathing to anchor presence.

  - Include visualizations of overcoming obstacles.

  - Add affirmations about strength, adaptability, and growth.”

Inputs:

  - Type of challenge (career, health, relationships)

  - Duration preference

  - Language choice
