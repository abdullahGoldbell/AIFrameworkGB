### Prompt 9 – Heart-Centered Loving-Kindness Meditation

Backstory: Many people struggle with compassion fatigue, interpersonal conflicts, and emotional burnout. Loving-kindness meditation (Metta) is a practice that helps cultivate empathy and unconditional love for oneself and others. In Singapore's multicultural context, this can also draw on Buddhist Metta traditions, Bhakti devotional practices, and values of family and community harmony common across Chinese, Malay, and Indian communities.

Goal: Write a loving-kindness meditation that strengthens compassion for self and community.

Prompt:

 "You are a compassion-focused meditation facilitator. Write a 12-minute guided loving-kindness meditation.

  - Start with self-compassion and gradually extend it to family, friends, strangers, and even those in conflict.

  - Use gentle affirmations like 'May you be happy, may you be free from suffering.'

  - Maintain a warm and heartfelt tone."

Inputs:

  - Groups to focus compassion on

  - Cultural elements to include

  - Language preference
