### Prompt 14 – Mindful Eating Meditation

Backstory: With Singapore's fast-paced lifestyle, many people eat at hawker centres or food courts while scrolling on their phones, leading to poor digestion and overeating. Mindful eating meditations help reconnect with the sensory experience of food and the gratitude for nourishment. This practice can be particularly helpful for those managing lifestyle conditions like diabetes or obesity.

Goal: Write a meditation that encourages mindful eating practices.

Prompt:

 “You are a wellness coach integrating mindfulness into daily routines. Write a 10-minute mindful eating meditation.

  - Begin before the meal with gratitude for the food and those who prepared it.

  - Guide slow, conscious chewing and sensory awareness.

  - End with reflection on nourishment and satiety.”

Inputs:

  - Meal type (breakfast, lunch, dinner)

  - Religious or cultural food context

  - Tone (educational, calming)
