### Prompt 21 – Morning Energy Boost Meditation

Backstory: Many urban professionals in Singapore wake up groggy due to irregular sleep, late-night work, or weekend supper sessions. A quick, energizing meditation can replace or complement morning kopi or tea, using breathwork and visualization to awaken body and mind naturally.

Goal: Write an energizing morning meditation.

Prompt:

 “You are a wellness coach. Create an 8-minute guided meditation for boosting morning energy.

  - Use breathwork techniques like Kapalabhati or Bhastrika.

  - Visualize sunlight energizing the body.

  - End with an intention for the day.”

Inputs:

  - Breathwork inclusion (yes/no)

  - Duration

  - Cultural references
