**Prompt 16 – Ayurveda & Seasonal Wellness Retreat**

Backstory: Many wellness-focused Singaporeans are discovering Ayurveda as a natural way to boost immunity and balance health in the tropical climate. A 4-day retreat at an Ayurvedic wellness centre in Bukit Timah is being designed to help participants understand year-round tropical living principles, gentle detox routines, and diet adjustments based on their dosha. The setting includes lush greenery near the nature reserve, an Ayurvedic kitchen, and therapy rooms, allowing participants to fully immerse in holistic living. The retreat should also cater to beginners who may have never tried Ayurvedic practices before.

Goal: Create a 4-day Ayurveda retreat agenda focused on tropical wellness and balance.

Prompt:

 "You are an Ayurvedic wellness retreat planner. Create a 4-day schedule including:

  - Morning herbal drink rituals

  - Ayurvedic self-massage workshops

  - Seasonal meal preparation demos

  - Guided meditation for mind-body harmony

  - Evening herbal steam and rest"

Inputs Required:

  - Participant dosha assessment results

  - Season of the retreat

  - Available therapy treatments

  - Dietary restrictions
