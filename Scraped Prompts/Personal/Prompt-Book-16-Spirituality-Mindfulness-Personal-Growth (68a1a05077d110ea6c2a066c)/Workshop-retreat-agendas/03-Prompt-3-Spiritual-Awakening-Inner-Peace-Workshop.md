**Prompt 3 – Spiritual Awakening & Inner Peace Workshop**

Backstory: Young adults in Singapore are increasingly seeking spiritual growth beyond traditional religion, exploring meditation, energy healing, and consciousness practices. A one-day workshop in a quiet studio space in Tiong Bahru or a wellness centre at Dempsey Hill aims to introduce these seekers to practical spiritual tools they can use daily. The event should balance ancient wisdom drawn from Buddhist, Taoist, Hindu, and yogic traditions with modern mindfulness techniques — reflecting Singapore’s multi-faith landscape. The agenda must be paced to avoid overwhelm and should include moments for self-reflection, partner exercises, and group discussions.

Goal: Create a one-day workshop flow for young adults seeking spiritual awakening, blending traditional Asian wisdom with modern techniques.

Prompt:

 "You are a spiritual workshop designer. Create a 1-day agenda that includes:

  - Welcome & intention setting

  - Guided meditations

  - Teachings drawn from Asian spiritual texts and traditions

  - Partner/group reflection activities

  - Closing gratitude ritual"

Inputs Required:

  - Group size

  - Indoor/outdoor setting availability

  - Preferred spiritual themes

  - Participant familiarity with meditation
