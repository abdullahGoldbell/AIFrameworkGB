**Prompt 7 – Yoga & Ayurveda Wellness Retreat**

Backstory: Many Singaporeans are turning to traditional healing systems like Yoga and Ayurveda for holistic health, alongside TCM and other Asian wellness traditions. A five-day retreat is being organized at a wellness resort in Bintan or a boutique retreat venue in Sentosa for wellness seekers, focusing on yoga asanas, pranayama, meditation, and personalized Ayurvedic consultations. The retreat should include daily detox meals, herbal treatments, and educational sessions on maintaining balance according to Ayurvedic principles. This program must be suitable for both beginners and seasoned yoga practitioners.

Goal: Create a 5-day retreat plan integrating yoga, meditation, and Ayurveda-based healing practices.

Prompt:

 "You are a wellness retreat planner. Design a 5-day agenda for a Singapore-region Yoga & Ayurveda retreat (Sentosa, Bintan, or similar venue), including:

  - Morning yoga sequences

  - Midday Ayurvedic cooking classes

  - Afternoon meditation and pranayama sessions

  - Evening cultural programs

  - Daily Ayurvedic therapies"

Inputs Required:

  - Number of participants

  - Facilities available for therapy and classes

  - Dietary needs

  - Yoga proficiency levels
