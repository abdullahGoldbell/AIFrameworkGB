**Prompt 20 – Sacred Masculinity Retreat**

Backstory: Conversations around conscious masculinity are slowly gaining traction in Singapore. A 2-day retreat at a coastal venue in Changi aims to guide men in exploring emotional intelligence, vulnerability, and healthy leadership through mindfulness, movement, and sharing circles. The program should challenge societal stereotypes while offering a supportive brotherhood space. It will draw on Singapore’s multicultural traditions — incorporating elements such as silat-inspired movement, tai chi, or dharma-based teachings — to give the work cultural depth.

Goal: Create a men’s retreat program fostering self-awareness and emotional openness.

Prompt:

 "You are a men’s personal growth coach. Create a 2-day retreat schedule including:

  - Morning movement and breathwork

  - Emotional intelligence workshops

  - Group dialogues on healthy masculinity

  - Traditional cultural activities

  - Evening reflection circles"

Inputs Required:

  - Participant age range

  - Cultural backgrounds

  - Preferred physical activity levels

  - Retreat setting
