**Prompt 4 – Mindful Parenting Retreat**

Backstory: Parents in Singapore are struggling to balance demanding work commitments with mindful, conscious parenting — navigating PSLE pressure, packed enrichment schedules, and dual-income realities. A two-day retreat at a quiet resort in Bintan or a wellness venue in Sentosa aims to help parents learn emotional regulation, communication, and mindfulness techniques to use with their children. The agenda should provide both parent-only sessions and family-inclusive activities. It should include experiential exercises, discussions on Singapore family values across Chinese, Malay, Indian, and Eurasian backgrounds, and practical take-home tools.

Goal: Create a two-day retreat agenda that supports parents in cultivating mindful and conscious parenting skills.

Prompt:

 "You are a parenting and mindfulness expert. Design a 2-day retreat plan including:

  - Parent-only learning sessions

  - Family bonding activities

  - Mindfulness exercises suitable for children

  - Evening reflection circles"

Inputs Required:

  - Age range of children attending

  - Number of families

  - Venue facilities (indoor, outdoor, childcare support)

  - Languages spoken by participants (English, Mandarin, Malay, Tamil)
