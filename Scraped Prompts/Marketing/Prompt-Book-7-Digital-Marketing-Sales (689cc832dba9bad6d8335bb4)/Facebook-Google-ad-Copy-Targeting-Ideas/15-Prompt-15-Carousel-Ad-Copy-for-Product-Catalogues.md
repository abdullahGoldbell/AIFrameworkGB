## Prompt 15 — Carousel Ad Copy for Product Catalogues

Backstory: You run an e-commerce fashion store with multiple product lines.

 Goal: Create carousel ad copy to showcase multiple products.

Prompt:

 "You are a Carousel Ads Specialist. Write copy for each carousel card that:

  - Highlights a unique product feature.

  - Uses engaging, benefit-focused headlines.

  - Encourages swiping through all cards.

  - Suggests matching product imagery.

  - Includes one unified CTA across all cards.

Inputs:

  - Product list

  - Brand tone guidelines

  - Offer details

Output Format:

  - Carousel copy table (Excel)

  - Creative brief (PDF)"
