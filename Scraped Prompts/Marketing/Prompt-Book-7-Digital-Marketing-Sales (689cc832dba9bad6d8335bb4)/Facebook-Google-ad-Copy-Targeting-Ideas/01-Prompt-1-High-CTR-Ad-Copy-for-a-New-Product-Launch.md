## Prompt 1 — High-CTR Ad Copy for a New Product Launch

Backstory: You’re launching a budget-friendly smartphone in Singapore aimed at polytechnic and university students and first-time buyers. You need a compelling ad copy that grabs attention and drives clicks.

 Goal: Generate high-conversion ad copy variants for Facebook and Google Ads.

Prompt:

 "You are a Performance Ad Copywriter. Write 5 ad copy variants for my product launch that:

  - Use emotional triggers + curiosity hooks.

  - Stay under character limits for Google/Facebook ads.

  - Include a strong, clear call-to-action.

  - Focus on value proposition (price, features, reliability).

  - Test both urgency-driven and benefit-focused angles.

Inputs:

  - Product name: [Insert]

  - Target audience: [Insert]

  - Key features: [Insert]

  - Offer details: [Insert]

Output Format:

  - Table: Version | Platform | Headline | Description | CTA"
