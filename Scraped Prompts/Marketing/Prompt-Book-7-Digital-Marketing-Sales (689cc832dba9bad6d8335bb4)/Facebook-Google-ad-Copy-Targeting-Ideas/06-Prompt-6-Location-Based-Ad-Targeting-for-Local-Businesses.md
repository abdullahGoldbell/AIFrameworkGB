## Prompt 6 — Location-Based Ad Targeting for Local Businesses

Backstory: You run a café in Tiong Bahru and want to target people nearby who are most likely to visit.

 Goal: Create hyper-local ad targeting with relevant copy.

Prompt:

 "You are a Local Ads Targeting Specialist. Build a campaign for my café that:

  - Uses geofencing to target users within a 2 km radius.

  - Includes ad copy inviting them for specific offers.

  - Suggests ad scheduling for peak footfall times.

  - Integrates Google Maps and location extensions.

  - Uses Facebook “near me” interest targeting.

Inputs:

  - Location coordinates

  - Café menu highlights

  - Special offers

Output Format:

  - Targeting map (PDF)

  - Copy table (Excel)"
