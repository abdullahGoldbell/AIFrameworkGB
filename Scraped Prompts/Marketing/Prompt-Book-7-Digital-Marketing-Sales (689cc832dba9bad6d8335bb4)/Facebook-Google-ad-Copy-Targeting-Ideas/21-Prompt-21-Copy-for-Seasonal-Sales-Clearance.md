## Prompt 21 — Copy for Seasonal Sales Clearance

Backstory: You run an apparel store and need to clear end-of-season stock.

 Goal: Create ad copy that drives bulk sales quickly.

Prompt:

 "You are a Clearance Campaign Expert. Write ad copy that:

  - Emphasises urgency (limited stock, final days).

  - Targets bargain hunters.

  - Suggests bundle purchase messaging.

  - Includes visual style guide for creatives.

  - Adapts to Facebook and Google formats.

Inputs:

  - Product types

  - Discount percentage

  - Clearance duration

Output Format:

  - Clearance copy list (Excel)

  - Visual style brief (PDF)"
