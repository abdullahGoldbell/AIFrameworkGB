## Prompt 2 — Targeting Strategy for First-Time Home Buyers

Backstory: You’re a property agent in Singapore promoting affordable BTO and resale HDB flats in heartland neighbourhoods like Tampines, Punggol and Woodlands. You want to find laser-focused Facebook/Google ad targeting to reach likely buyers.

 Goal: Create a targeting strategy with layered audience filters.

Prompt:

 "You are a Paid Ads Targeting Expert. Design a targeting strategy for first-time home buyers that:

  - Uses demographic, interest, and behavioural targeting on Facebook.

  - Suggests Google keyword targeting for high-intent searches.

  - Includes remarketing and lookalike audience ideas.

  - Recommends ad schedule for maximum ROI.

  - Adjusts targeting for HDB heartland audience behaviour.

Inputs:

  - Location: [Insert]

  - Price range: [Insert]

  - Buyer profile: [Insert]

Output Format:

  - Targeting plan table (Excel)

  - Sample ad audience breakdown (PDF)"
