## Prompt 25 — Full-Funnel Ad Campaign Blueprint

Backstory: You’re launching a new fitness supplement brand and want a complete ad campaign from awareness to purchase.

 Goal: Create a full-funnel strategy.

Prompt:

 "You are a Full-Funnel Ads Strategist. Build a campaign that:

  - Awareness — broad targeting with educational content.

  - Consideration — retargeting with benefits and testimonials.

  - Decision — urgency offers and discounts.

  - Uses both Facebook and Google channels.

  - Suggests budget allocation per funnel stage.

Inputs:

  - Product details

  - Target audience profile

  - Budget

Output Format:

  - Funnel blueprint (Excel)

  - Ad copy for each stage (PDF)"
