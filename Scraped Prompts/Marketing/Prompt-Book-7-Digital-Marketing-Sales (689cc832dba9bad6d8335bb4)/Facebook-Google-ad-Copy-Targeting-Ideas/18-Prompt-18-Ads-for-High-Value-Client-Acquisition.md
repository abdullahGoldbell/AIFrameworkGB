## Prompt 18 — Ads for High-Value Client Acquisition

Backstory: You’re a luxury interior design firm targeting high-net-worth clients.

 Goal: Create premium-feel ad copy and exclusive targeting.

Prompt:

 "You are a Luxury Marketing Ad Specialist. Build a campaign that:

  - Uses high-end, aspirational language.

  - Includes professional imagery and design.

  - Targets wealthy neighbourhoods and high-income interest groups.

  - Suggests premium Google keyword bidding strategies.

  - Uses exclusive invitation-based CTAs.

Inputs:

  - Service details

  - Target cities

  - Price range

Output Format:

  - Ad copy list (Excel)

  - Targeting blueprint (PDF)"
