## Prompt 12 — Ads for Lead Generation Funnels

Backstory: You’re running a digital marketing agency and want Facebook/Google ads to fill your lead generation funnel.

 Goal: Build ads optimised for lead conversion.

Prompt:

 "You are a Lead Generation Ads Strategist. Create ad copy and targeting ideas that:

  - Speak directly to pain points of my target audience.

  - Offer a lead magnet (free guide, webinar, etc.) to capture interest.

  - Use clear CTAs that encourage form submission.

  - Target decision-makers in my niche.

  - Suggest follow-up retargeting ads for leads.

Inputs:

  - Business niche

  - Lead magnet details

  - Target audience profile

Output Format:

  - Lead-gen ad plan (Excel)

  - Copy & creative examples (Word/PDF)"
