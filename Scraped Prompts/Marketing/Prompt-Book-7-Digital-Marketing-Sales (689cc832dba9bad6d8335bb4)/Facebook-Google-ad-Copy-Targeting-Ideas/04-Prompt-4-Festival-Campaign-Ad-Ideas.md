## Prompt 4 — Festival Campaign Ad Ideas

Backstory: You own an online jewellery store in Singapore and want to run special ads for Chinese New Year and Deepavali.

 Goal: Create culturally relevant, high-engagement ad ideas.

Prompt:

 "You are a Festival Campaign Specialist. For my jewellery store, create ad copy and targeting ideas that:

  - Reflect Singapore’s multicultural festive aesthetics for Chinese New Year/Deepavali.

  - Suggest festive offer positioning.

  - Include copy variants for emotional storytelling.

  - Target people searching for gifts.

  - Suggest ad creatives (image/video) that resonate with the festivals.

Inputs:

  - Product details

  - Festival offers

  - Target age group

Output Format:

  - Festival campaign plan (Excel)

  - Sample copy & creative brief (PDF)"
