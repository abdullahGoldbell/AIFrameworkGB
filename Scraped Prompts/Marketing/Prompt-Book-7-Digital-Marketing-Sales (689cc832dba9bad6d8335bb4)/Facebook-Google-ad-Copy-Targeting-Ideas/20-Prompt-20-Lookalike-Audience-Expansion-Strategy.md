## Prompt 20 — Lookalike Audience Expansion Strategy

Backstory: Your digital course platform has a small audience but high engagement. You want to expand reach using lookalikes.

 Goal: Build lookalike audiences on Facebook and Google.

Prompt:

 "You are a Lookalike Audience Specialist. Create a plan that:

  - Defines seed audience size and source.

  - Suggests lookalike % splits (1%, 2%, 5%).

  - Combines with interest/behaviour filters.

  - Recommends ad creatives for new audiences.

  - Provides scaling roadmap without losing quality.

Inputs:

  - Current audience data

  - Course topics

  - Target countries

Output Format:

  - Lookalike plan (Excel)

Scaling roadmap (PDF)"
