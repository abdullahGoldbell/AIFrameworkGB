## Prompt 13 — Crisis or Urgent Sales Campaign Ads

Backstory: You run an electronics store and need to clear stock urgently due to upcoming GST changes.

 Goal: Create urgency-driven ad copy and targeting ideas.

Prompt:

 "You are an Urgency Ad Specialist. Build ads that:

  - Emphasise limited-time availability.

  - Use urgency words without appearing spammy.

  - Target past buyers and lookalikes.

  - Suggest countdown timer creatives.

  - Provide retargeting strategy for cart abandoners.

Inputs:

  - Product details

  - Deadline date

  - Offer percentage

Output Format:

  - Ad copy table (Excel)

  - Urgency campaign blueprint (PDF)"
