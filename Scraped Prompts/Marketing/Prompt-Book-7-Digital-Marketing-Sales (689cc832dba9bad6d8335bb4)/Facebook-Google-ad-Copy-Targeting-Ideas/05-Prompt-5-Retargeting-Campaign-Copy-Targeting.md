## Prompt 5 — Retargeting Campaign Copy & Targeting

Backstory: Your online course website gets a lot of traffic, but many visitors don’t enrol. You want retargeting ads to bring them back.

 Goal: Build a retargeting ad plan with copy ideas.

Prompt:

 "You are a Retargeting Campaign Strategist. Design a retargeting campaign that:

  - Segments visitors by behaviour (visited course page, abandoned checkout).

  - Creates ad copy tailored to each segment.

  - Uses urgency and scarcity messaging.

  - Suggests Google Display and Facebook retargeting audiences.

  - Recommends ad frequency caps to avoid fatigue.

Inputs:

  - Website analytics data

  - Course details

  - Offer for returning visitors

Output Format:

  - Retargeting campaign matrix (Excel)

Ad copy versions (Word/PDF)"
