## Prompt 19 — Festival-Specific Ad Targeting Playbook

Backstory: You own a Singapore gifting business and want to maximise sales during Chinese New Year, Hari Raya Puasa, Deepavali, and Christmas.

 Goal: Build targeting plans for each festival.

Prompt:

 "You are a Festival Ads Planner. For my business, create:

  - Audience targeting ideas for each festival relevant to Singapore’s multicultural market.

  - Seasonal ad copy variants.

  - Cultural imagery suggestions.

  - Special offer positioning per festival.

  - Recommended campaign timelines.

Inputs:

  - Product categories

  - Festival dates

  - Budget allocation per festival

Output Format:

  - Festival targeting chart (Excel)

  - Creative brief (PDF)"
