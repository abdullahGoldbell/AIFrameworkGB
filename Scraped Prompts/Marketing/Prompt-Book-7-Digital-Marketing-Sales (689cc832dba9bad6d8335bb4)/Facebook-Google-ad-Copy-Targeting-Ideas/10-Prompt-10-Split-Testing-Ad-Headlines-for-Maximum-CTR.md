## Prompt 10 — Split Testing Ad Headlines for Maximum CTR

Backstory: You run a travel booking website and want to improve CTR by testing different headlines.

 Goal: Generate headline variations and a test plan.

Prompt:

 "You are a Headline Testing Specialist. Create 10 ad headline variations for my campaign that:

  - Use power words for emotional impact.

  - Stay within platform character limits.

  - Mix benefit-focused and curiosity-driven styles.

  - Are designed for A/B testing.

  - Suggest test duration and KPIs.

Inputs:

  - Campaign goal

  - Destination details

  - Offer specifics

Output Format:

  - Headline variations table (Excel)

  - A/B test plan (Word/PDF)"
