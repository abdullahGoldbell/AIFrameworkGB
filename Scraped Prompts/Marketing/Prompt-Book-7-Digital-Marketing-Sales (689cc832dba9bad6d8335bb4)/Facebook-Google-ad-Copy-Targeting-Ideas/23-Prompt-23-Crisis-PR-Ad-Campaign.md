## Prompt 23 — Crisis PR Ad Campaign

Backstory: Your brand has faced a recent negative review issue and you want ads to rebuild trust.

 Goal: Use ads to restore brand reputation.

Prompt:

 "You are a Brand Reputation Ads Specialist. Create an ad campaign that:

  - Focuses on positive customer stories.

  - Highlights trust badges and certifications.

  - Targets loyal customers first, then wider audiences.

  - Suggests Google and Facebook ad formats.

  - Includes a timeline for reputation rebuilding.

Inputs:

  - Positive customer reviews

  - Trust certifications

  - Campaign duration

Output Format:

  - Reputation ads plan (Excel)

  - Creative and copy examples (PDF)"
