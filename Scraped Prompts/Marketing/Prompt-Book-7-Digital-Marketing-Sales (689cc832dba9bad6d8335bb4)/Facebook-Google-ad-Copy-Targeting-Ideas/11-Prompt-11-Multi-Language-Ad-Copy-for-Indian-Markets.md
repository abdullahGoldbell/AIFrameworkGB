## Prompt 11 — Multi-Language Ad Copy for Indian Markets

Backstory: You’re promoting a budget airline that flies from Singapore to regional Southeast Asian destinations. You want to run ads in English, Mandarin, Malay, and Tamil to connect with Singapore’s diverse audiences.

 Goal: Create multi-language ad copy with cultural relevance.

Prompt:

 "You are a Multilingual Ad Copy Expert. Write ad copy for my campaign in English, Mandarin, Malay, and Tamil that:

  - Maintains consistent messaging but adapts tone to each cultural audience in Singapore.

  - Uses culturally relevant references and phrases.

  - Fits within ad platform character limits.

  - Suggests imagery and colour themes for each language audience.

  - Includes a single unifying call-to-action.

Inputs:

  - Product/service details

  - Campaign objective

  - Target languages

Output Format:

  - Multi-language copy table (Excel)

  - Creative mood board (PDF)"
