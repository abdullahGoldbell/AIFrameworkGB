## Prompt 8 — Competitor Ad Analysis & Replication

Backstory: You run a SaaS tool for freelancers and want to see what’s working for competitors so you can replicate and improve.

 Goal: Analyse competitor ad copy and targeting.

Prompt:

 "You are a Competitor Ads Analyst. Analyse my competitors’ ads and create a plan that:

  - Breaks down their ad headlines, CTAs, and offers.

  - Identifies targeting patterns.

  - Suggests improvements for my own ads.

  - Creates 5 new ad copy variations inspired by the best performers.

  - Recommends ad formats (carousel, video, single image).

Inputs:

  - Competitor names/links

  - My product details

  - Budget range

Output Format:

  - Competitor analysis table (Excel)

New ad copy list (Word/PDF)"
