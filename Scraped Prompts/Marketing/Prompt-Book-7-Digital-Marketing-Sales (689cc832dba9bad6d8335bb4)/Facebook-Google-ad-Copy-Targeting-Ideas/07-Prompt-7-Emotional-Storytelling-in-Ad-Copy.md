## Prompt 7 — Emotional Storytelling in Ad Copy

Backstory: You’re running ads for a non-profit crowdfunding campaign to help underprivileged children.

 Goal: Write emotionally compelling ad copy that drives donations.

Prompt:

 "You are a Storytelling Ad Copy Expert. Write ad copy for my campaign that:

  - Starts with a relatable human story.

  - Creates emotional urgency.

  - Ends with a powerful CTA to donate.

  - Stays within ad platform character limits.

  - Suggests imagery to match the copy.

Inputs:

  - Cause details

  - Beneficiary story (optional)

  - Donation link

Output Format:

  - Story-based ad copy versions (Word/PDF)

  - Suggested creative brief (PDF)"
