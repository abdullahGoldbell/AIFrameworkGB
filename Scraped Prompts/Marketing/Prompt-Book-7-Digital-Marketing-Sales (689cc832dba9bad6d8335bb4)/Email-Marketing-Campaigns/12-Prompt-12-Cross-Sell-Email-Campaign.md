## **Prompt 12 — Cross-Sell Email Campaign**

Backstory: Your bookstore’s online store wants to recommend books related to past purchases.

 Goal: Increase average order value via cross-sells.

Prompt:

 "You are a Cross-Sell Email Specialist. Build a 2-email campaign that:

  - Uses customer purchase data to recommend related books.

  - Includes personalised product images and blurbs.

  - Offers bundle pricing.

  - Keeps copy under 250 words.

  - Has strong subject lines with curiosity hooks.

Inputs:

  - Past purchase history

  - Recommended products

  - Bundle discount details

Output Format:

  - Cross-sell copy table (Excel)

Personalisation setup guide (PDF)"
