## **Prompt 21 — Milestone Celebration Email**

Backstory: Your fitness app wants to celebrate customer achievements like “100 workouts completed” to build engagement.

 Goal: Strengthen the emotional connection with the brand.

Prompt:

 "You are a Customer Milestone Email Specialist. Write a celebratory email that:

  - Congratulates the user on their milestone.

  - Shows their progress visually (charts/images).

  - Offers a small gift (free month, badge).

  - Encourages sharing on social media.

  - Teases the next milestone to keep them engaged.

Inputs:

  - Milestone type

  - Reward details

  - Social sharing link

Output Format:

  - Email copy with graphic placement notes (PDF)"
