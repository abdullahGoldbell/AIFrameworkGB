## **Prompt 15 — Refill Reminder Email for Consumable Products**

Backstory: You sell organic supplements and want to remind customers when it’s time to reorder.

 Goal: Automate refill reminders to increase repeat purchases.

Prompt:

 "You are a Product Refill Email Specialist. Build a 2-email reminder sequence that:

  - Sends 5 days before the estimated refill date.

  - Highlights benefits of continuous usage.

  - Offers a small reorder discount.

  - Includes “One-click reorder” CTA.

  - Uses urgency without being pushy.

Inputs:

  - Product refill cycle

  - Discount details

  - Customer purchase history

Output Format:

  - Reminder sequence chart (Excel)

  - Full email copy (Word/PDF)"
