## **Prompt 13 — Loyalty Program Onboarding Series**

Backstory: You’re launching a loyalty rewards program for your café chain.

 Goal: Encourage sign-ups and educate members on benefits.

Prompt:

 "You are a Loyalty Program Email Specialist. Create a 3-email onboarding series that:

  - Welcomes members and explains how points work.

  - Shares ways to earn and redeem points.

  - Offers a bonus for first redemption.

  - Uses branded imagery and friendly tone.

  - Suggests seasonal promotions for members.

Inputs:

  - Loyalty program details

  - Bonus offer

  - Visual assets

Output Format:

  - Onboarding flowchart (Excel)

Email copy + design cues (PDF)"
