## **Prompt 19 — Early Access VIP Sale**

Backstory: You have a cosmetics store with a loyalty club and want to give members first access to a flash sale.

 Goal: Make VIPs feel special and drive high-value purchases.

Prompt:

 "You are a VIP Campaign Email Strategist. Build a 2-email early access campaign that:

  - Announces the VIP-only early access sale.

  - Shares exclusive discount codes.

  - Creates urgency with limited stock messages.

  - Includes attractive product images.

  - Ends with a thank-you note for being a VIP member.

Inputs:

  - Discount percentage

  - Product categories

  - VIP member list

Output Format:

  - VIP email templates (PDF)

  - Campaign timeline (Excel)"
