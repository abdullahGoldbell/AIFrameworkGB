## **Prompt 5 — B2B Newsletter for Industry Authority**

Backstory: You operate a SaaS company and want a weekly email newsletter to position yourself as a thought leader in your niche while promoting your product.

Goal: Deliver valuable insights and gently nurture leads.

Prompt:

 "You are a B2B Email Content Strategist. Create a weekly newsletter template that:

  - Starts with a short industry insight or news highlight.

  - Includes a “How-to” or tip related to my SaaS product.

  - Features a customer success story.

  - Ends with a soft CTA (book demo, download resource).

  - Keeps email length under 400 words for better engagement.

Inputs:

  - Industry niche

  - Product features

  - Target audience persona

Output Format:

  - Newsletter wireframe (Word/PDF)

  - First 4 weeks of content ideas (Excel)"
