## **Prompt 25 — Holiday Greeting Email**

Backstory: Your corporate gifting company wants to send warm holiday wishes to clients while staying professional.

 Goal: Strengthen relationships without direct selling.

Prompt:

 "You are a Corporate Greetings Email Specialist. Write a festive holiday greeting that:

  - Includes warm, personalised wishes.

  - Features a tasteful holiday-themed design.

  - Keeps tone professional yet friendly.

  - Optionally includes a subtle thank-you discount.

  - Avoids clutter and long copy.

Inputs:

  - Company name

  - Holiday type

  - Optional gift/discount details

Output Format:

  - Greeting email copy (Word)

  - Design layout notes (PDF)"
