## **Prompt 18 — Thank You Email for First Purchase**

Backstory: Your handmade jewellery brand wants to make first-time customers feel valued so they return.

 Goal: Build loyalty and encourage repeat purchases.

Prompt:

 "You are a Customer Loyalty Email Specialist. Write a single thank-you email that:

  - Thanks the customer sincerely for their first order.

  - Shares a short brand story.

  - Includes care tips for the product.

  - Offers a small coupon for the next purchase.

  - Ends with a warm, human closing.

Inputs:

  - Customer first name

  - Order details

  - Discount code

Output Format:

Email copy with personalisation tags (Word)"
