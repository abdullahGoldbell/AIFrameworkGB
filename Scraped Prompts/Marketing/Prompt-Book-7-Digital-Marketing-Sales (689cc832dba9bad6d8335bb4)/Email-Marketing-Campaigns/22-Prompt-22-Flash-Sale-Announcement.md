## **Prompt 22 — Flash Sale Announcement**

Backstory: Your electronics store wants to run a 24-hour flash sale.

 Goal: Drive maximum traffic and purchases in a short time.

Prompt:

 "You are a Flash Sale Email Specialist. Build a 2-email flash sale sequence that:

  - Announces the sale with big, bold visuals.

  - Shares top-selling items in the email body.

  - Uses countdown timers.

  - Includes strong CTAs (“Shop Now”).

  - Sends a last-hour reminder.

Inputs:

  - Sale start/end time

  - Discount details

  - Product highlights

Output Format:

  - Flash sale sequence table (Excel)

  - Email design mockups (PDF)"
