## **Prompt 7 — Product Launch Email Sequence**

Backstory: Your tech gadget store is launching a new smart home device and you want to build hype before release.

 Goal: Use email to create anticipation and maximise launch day sales.

Prompt:

 "You are a Product Launch Email Strategist. Build a 5-email sequence that:

  - Starts with a teaser announcement.

  - Shares behind-the-scenes or development stories.

  - Includes a pre-order offer.

  - Sends a launch day “Now Live” email.

  - Follows up with early reviews/testimonials.

Inputs:

  - Product features & USP

  - Launch date

  - Pre-order details

Output Format:

  - Launch email timeline (Excel)

  - Full copy with design suggestions (PDF)"
