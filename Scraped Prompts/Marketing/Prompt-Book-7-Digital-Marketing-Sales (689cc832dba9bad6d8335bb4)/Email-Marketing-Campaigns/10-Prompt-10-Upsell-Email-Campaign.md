## **Prompt 10 — Upsell Email Campaign**

Backstory: You sell online fitness programs and want to upsell premium plans to customers who bought basic packages.

 Goal: Move customers to higher-value purchases.

Prompt:

 "You are an Upsell Email Strategist. Build a 3-email upsell sequence that:

  - Highlights the benefits of the premium plan.

  - Shares success stories from premium customers.

  - Offers a limited-time upgrade discount.

  - Includes side-by-side feature comparison.

  - Ends with a strong CTA to upgrade now.

Inputs:

  - Basic vs premium features

  - Upgrade price

  - Deadline for offer

Output Format:

  - Upsell email chart (Excel)

  - Full email copy (PDF)"
