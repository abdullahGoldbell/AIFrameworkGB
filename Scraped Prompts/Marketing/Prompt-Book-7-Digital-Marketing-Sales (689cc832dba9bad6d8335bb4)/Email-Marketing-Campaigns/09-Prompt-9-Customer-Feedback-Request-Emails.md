## **Prompt 9 — Customer Feedback Request Emails**

Backstory: You run a meal subscription service and want to collect feedback after the first delivery to improve service and boost reviews.

 Goal: Encourage customers to share experiences and rate your service.

Prompt:

 "You are a Customer Feedback Email Specialist. Write a 2-email feedback request campaign that:

  - Sends a thank-you email after the first order.

  - Requests a short review or feedback form completion.

  - Offers an incentive (discount on next purchase).

  - Keeps the email under 150 words for quick reading.

  - Includes a direct review form link.

Inputs:

  - Service details

  - Incentive type

  - Feedback form link

Output Format:

  - Feedback email copy (Word)

  - Review collection plan (PDF)"
