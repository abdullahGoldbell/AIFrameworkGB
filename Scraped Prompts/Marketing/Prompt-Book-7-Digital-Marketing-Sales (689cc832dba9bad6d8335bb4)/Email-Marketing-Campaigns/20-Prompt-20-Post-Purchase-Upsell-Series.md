## **Prompt 20 — Post-Purchase Upsell Series**

Backstory: Your online furniture store wants to offer matching items after a customer buys a major product.

 Goal: Increase post-purchase revenue with relevant suggestions.

Prompt:

 "You are a Post-Purchase Email Strategist. Create a 3-email upsell sequence that:

  - Suggests matching or complementary items.

  - Includes lifestyle images.

  - Offers a bundle discount.

  - Sends the first email within 3 days of purchase.

  - Personalises recommendations based on purchase history.

Inputs:

  - Purchased product details

  - Recommended items

  - Bundle offer

Output Format:

  - Upsell sequence plan (Excel)

  - Email copy (Word/PDF)"
