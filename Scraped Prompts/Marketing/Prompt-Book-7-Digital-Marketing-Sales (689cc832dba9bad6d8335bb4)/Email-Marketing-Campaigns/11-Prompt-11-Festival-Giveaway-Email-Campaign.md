## **Prompt 11 — Festival Giveaway Email Campaign**

Backstory: Your Singapore-based fashion accessories brand wants to run a Hari Raya giveaway to engage subscribers.

 Goal: Drive excitement and participation.

Prompt:

 "You are a Giveaway Email Campaign Specialist. Create a 3-email giveaway sequence that:

  - Announces the giveaway and how to enter.

  - Sends a mid-campaign reminder with social proof.

  - Reveals winners and promotes products.

  - Keeps tone festive and celebratory.

  - Encourages sharing on social media.

Inputs:

  - Giveaway prize details

  - Entry rules

  - Campaign dates

Output Format:

  - Giveaway campaign table (Excel)

  - Email + social share templates (PDF)"
