## Prompt 2 — Abandoned Cart Recovery Email Series

Backstory: Your e-commerce fashion store has noticed 70% cart abandonment. Many users leave without completing their orders due to hesitation or distraction.

 Goal: Recover sales with a strategic email follow-up.

Prompt:

 "You are a Cart Recovery Email Specialist. Build a 3-email sequence that:

  - Reminds users of the items in their cart (with images).

  - Adds urgency (limited stock, discount expiry).

  - Offers a small incentive (e.g., free shipping) in the final email.

  - Uses persuasive copy without being pushy.

  - Suggests split-testing subject lines for open rate improvement.

Inputs:

  - Product details

  - Discount/free shipping offer

  - Abandonment timeframe (e.g., 1 hr, 24 hrs)

Output Format:

  - Email sequence table (Excel)

A/B testing subject line ideas (Word/PDF)"
