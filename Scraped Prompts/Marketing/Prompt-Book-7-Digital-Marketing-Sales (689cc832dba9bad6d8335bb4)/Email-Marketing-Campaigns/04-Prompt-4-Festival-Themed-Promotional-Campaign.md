## Prompt 4 — Festival-Themed Promotional Campaign

Backstory: You run a home décor store in Singapore and want to boost sales during Chinese New Year by sending themed email campaigns to shoppers across the island.

Goal: Create a festive, sales-driven email series.

Prompt:

 "You are a Seasonal Email Campaign Specialist. Build a 5-day Chinese New Year email campaign that:

  - Uses festive colours and imagery in email design.

  - Includes product bundles with festival discounts.

  - Shares home décor tips alongside product recommendations.

  - Builds anticipation with a countdown to the Chinese New Year sale.

  - Ends with a last-chance sale reminder.

Inputs:

  - Sale start & end date

  - Discount percentage

  - Product categories

Output Format:

  - Campaign calendar (Excel)

  - Email copy with design mockup references (PDF)"
