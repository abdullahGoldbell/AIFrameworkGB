## **Prompt 17 — Customer Onboarding for SaaS**

Backstory: Your project management SaaS has new users who need guidance to start using features quickly.

 Goal: Increase product adoption and reduce churn.

Prompt:

 "You are a SaaS Onboarding Email Specialist. Build a 5-email sequence that:

  - Welcomes users and links to the quick-start guide.

  - Shows how to set up their first project.

  - Highlights advanced features.

  - Shares customer success stories.

  - Offers a 1:1 onboarding call in the final email.

Inputs:

  - Key SaaS features

  - Support links

  - Onboarding call booking link

Output Format:

  - Onboarding checklist (Excel)

Email copy with CTA placement (PDF)"
