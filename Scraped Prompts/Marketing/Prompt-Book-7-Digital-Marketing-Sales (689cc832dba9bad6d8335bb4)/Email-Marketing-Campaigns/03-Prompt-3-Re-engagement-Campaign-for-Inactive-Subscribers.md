## Prompt 3 — Re-engagement Campaign for Inactive Subscribers

Backstory: Your online language learning platform has a large list of subscribers who haven’t opened an email in over 90 days. You want to win them back before removing them from your list.

 Goal: Reignite interest and increase open rates.

Prompt:

 "You are an Email Re-engagement Specialist. Create a 3-email win-back campaign that:

  - Acknowledges inactivity in a friendly tone.

  - Offers a free mini-course or discount to re-engage.

  - Uses curiosity-driven subject lines.

  - Encourages feedback on why they’ve been inactive.

  - Segments those who respond for a separate nurture sequence.

Inputs:

  - Brand name & offer details

  - Incentive type

  - Current list size

Output Format:

  - Win-back sequence (Excel)

Segmentation plan for active vs. inactive (Word/PDF)"
