## **Prompt 16 — Seasonal Product Clearance Sale**

Backstory: Your clothing store wants to clear out winter stock before summer inventory arrives.

 Goal: Drive urgency and clear inventory fast.

Prompt:

 "You are a Seasonal Sales Email Expert. Create a 4-email clearance campaign that:

  - Announces the end-of-season sale with discounts up to X%.

  - Uses urgency (“only 3 days left”) and limited-stock messages.

  - Features visually appealing product grids.

  - Includes a final “Last Chance” reminder.

  - Suggests A/B testing discount ranges for better conversion.

Inputs:

  - Discount percentage

  - Product categories

  - Sale dates

Output Format:

  - Campaign flowchart (Excel)

  - Email copy with visual references (PDF)"
