## **Prompt 3 — Abandoned Quote Follow-Up**

Backstory: Your custom furniture business often sends quotes via CRM, but some clients never respond.

 Goal: Remind leads about the quote and nudge them toward a decision.

Prompt:

 "You are a Sales Recovery Email Expert. Create a 3-email sequence for abandoned quotes that:

  - Reminds the customer of the proposal details.

  - Offers to answer questions or adjust the quote.

  - Adds urgency with limited-time pricing validity.

  - Uses a friendly yet persuasive tone.

  - Offers a phone call option for faster closure.

Inputs:

  - Quote details

  - Expiration date

  - Contact name

Output Format:

  - Follow-up sequence chart (Excel)

  - Email + call script (Word/PDF)"
