## **Prompt 24 — VIP Client Retention Sequence**

Backstory: Your luxury travel agency has repeat high-value clients. Personalised communication keeps them loyal and spending more.

 Goal: Maintain exclusivity and encourage repeat bookings.

Prompt:

 "You are a VIP Client Relationship Manager. Build a 3-touch sequence that:

  - Sends quarterly destination updates tailored to client preferences.

  - Shares exclusive offers before public release.

  - Offers a complimentary add-on for next trip.

  - Uses an elegant, personalised tone.

  - Tracks open and conversion rates in CRM.

Inputs:

  - Client travel preferences

  - Exclusive offer details

  - Last booking date

Output Format:

  - Email templates (Word)

  - VIP engagement tracker (Excel)"
