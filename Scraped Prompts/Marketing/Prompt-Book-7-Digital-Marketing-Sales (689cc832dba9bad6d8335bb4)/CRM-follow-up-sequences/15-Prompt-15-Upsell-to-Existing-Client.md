## **Prompt 15 — Upsell to Existing Client**

Backstory: Your digital agency has clients who use only one service (SEO), but could benefit from others (social media, PPC).

 Goal: Increase average client value.

Prompt:

 "You are a CRM Upsell Specialist. Build a 2-email sequence that:

  - Thanks them for their ongoing partnership.

  - Shows how additional services can enhance results.

  - Offers a free audit or trial.

  - Shares a case study showing success with multiple services.

  - Encourages booking a consultation call.

Inputs:

  - Existing service details

  - Additional services

  - Case study link

Output Format:

  - Upsell plan (Excel)

  - Email drafts (Word/PDF)"
