## **Prompt 10 — Lost Deal Follow-Up**

Backstory: Your IT consulting firm loses some deals to competitors, but wants to stay top of mind for future opportunities.

 Goal: Keep the relationship warm without being intrusive.

Prompt:

 "You are a Lost Deal Recovery Specialist. Create a 2-touch follow-up that:

  - Acknowledges their choice politely.

  - Offers to provide free resources relevant to their needs.

  - Shares a quarterly update with new solutions.

  - Keeps tone professional and helpful.

  - Tags them for future campaigns.

Inputs:

  - Prospect name

  - Service discussed

  - Relevant resource link

Output Format:

  - Recovery follow-up schedule (Excel)

  - Email drafts (Word)"
