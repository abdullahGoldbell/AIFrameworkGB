## **Prompt 6 — Payment Reminder Sequence**

Backstory: You run a digital marketing agency where some clients delay payments after invoice generation.

 Goal: Politely remind them and secure payment without straining the relationship.

Prompt:

 "You are a Payment Reminder Specialist. Build a 3-touch reminder sequence that:

  - First email — gentle reminder with invoice details.

  - Second email — reiterate due date, offer payment methods.

  - Third email — clear final notice with polite urgency.

  - Keep tone professional yet approachable.

  - Integrate payment link buttons in every email.

Inputs:

  - Client name

  - Invoice number & amount

  - Payment link

Output Format:

  - Payment sequence table (Excel)

Email templates (Word/PDF)"
