## **Prompt 13 — Client Feedback Collection**

Backstory: Your interior design agency wants to improve services by collecting structured feedback after every project.

 Goal: Get actionable insights and maintain goodwill.

Prompt:

 "You are a Client Experience Specialist. Write a single follow-up email that:

  - Thanks the client for working with you.

  - Shares a simple feedback form link.

  - Explains how their input will help.

  - Keeps tone warm and appreciative.

  - Optionally offers a small thank-you gift.

Inputs:

  - Client name

  - Project type

  - Feedback form link

Output Format:

  - Feedback email template (Word)

  - Tracking spreadsheet (Excel)"
