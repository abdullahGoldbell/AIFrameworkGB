## **Prompt 2 — Post-Event Follow-Up**

Backstory: You just attended a trade show and collected 150 business cards from potential clients.

 Goal: Reconnect while the event is fresh and book meetings.

Prompt:

 "You are an Event Lead Follow-Up Specialist. Write a 3-email follow-up sequence that:

  - References the event and your meeting.

  - Provides additional information on your service.

  - Shares a personalised suggestion based on the conversation.

  - Includes a CTA for scheduling a call.

  - Keeps each email under 200 words for quick reading.

Inputs:

  - Event name & date

  - Meeting notes

  - Scheduling link

Output Format:

  - CRM follow-up script (Word)

Contact tagging plan (Excel)"
