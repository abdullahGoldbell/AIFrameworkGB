## **Prompt 11 — High-Value Lead Priority Sequence**

Backstory: Your real estate firm has a small list of high-value buyers who need quick follow-up to close deals.

 Goal: Give VIP treatment to boost conversion probability.

Prompt:

 "You are a High-Ticket CRM Specialist. Design a 5-step sequence that:

  - Responds within 1 hour of inquiry.

  - Sends personalised property recommendations.

  - Offers virtual tour booking.

  - Sends testimonials from similar buyers.

  - Ends with a clear call to action for viewing or booking.

Inputs:

  - Buyer budget range

  - Property types

  - Contact preference (call/email)

Output Format:

  - Lead handling SOP (PDF)

  - Email + SMS scripts (Word)"
