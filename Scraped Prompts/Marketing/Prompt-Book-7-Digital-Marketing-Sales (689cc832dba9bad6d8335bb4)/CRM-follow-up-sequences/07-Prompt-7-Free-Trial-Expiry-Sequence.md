## **Prompt 7 — Free Trial Expiry Sequence**

Backstory: Your SaaS project management tool offers a 14-day free trial, but most users don’t convert without reminders.

 Goal: Prompt users to subscribe before trial ends.

Prompt:

 "You are a Trial Conversion Specialist. Design a 4-email countdown sequence that:

  - Alerts user when 7 days remain.

  - Highlights unused premium features.

  - Shares testimonials or case studies.

  - Gives final 24-hour subscription reminder.

  - Offers an exclusive discount for immediate upgrade.

Inputs:

  - Trial length

  - Subscription pricing

  - Key premium features

Output Format:

  - Email copy sequence (Word)

  - Conversion tracking sheet (Excel)"
