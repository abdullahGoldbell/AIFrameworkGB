## **Prompt 9 — Webinar Attendee Follow-Up**

Backstory: Your digital coaching business hosts free webinars to generate leads. Attendees often drop off without taking action.

 Goal: Convert attendees into paying clients.

Prompt:

 "You are a Webinar Conversion Specialist. Build a 3-email sequence that:

  - Thanks them for attending and sends replay link.

  - Shares additional resources to deepen trust.

  - Offers a limited-time discount for related paid course.

  - Uses urgency without sounding pushy.

  - Tracks open and click rates for optimisation.

Inputs:

  - Webinar topic

  - Replay link

  - Course details

Output Format:

  - Sequence in CRM import format (CSV)

  - Email copy (PDF)"
