## **Prompt 14 — Seasonal Greeting + Soft Offer**

Backstory: Your corporate gifting business wants to wish clients on festivals and subtly promote services.

 Goal: Stay memorable without hard selling.

Prompt:

 "You are a Festive CRM Outreach Specialist. Write a greeting email that:

  - Personalises with client name and past interactions.

  - Includes a short festive wish.

  - Softly introduces a relevant seasonal offer.

  - Keeps email under 150 words.

  - Includes one clean call-to-action link.

Inputs:

  - Festival name

  - Offer details

  - Client list

Output Format:

  - Greeting email design guide (PDF)

  - Copy (Word)"
