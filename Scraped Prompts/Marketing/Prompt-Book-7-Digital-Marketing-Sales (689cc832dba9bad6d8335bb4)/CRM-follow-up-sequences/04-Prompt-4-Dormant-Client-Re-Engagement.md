## **Prompt 4 — Dormant Client Re-Engagement**

Backstory: Your corporate training company has past clients who haven’t booked sessions in over 12 months.

 Goal: Reignite the relationship and bring them back.

Prompt:

 "You are a Client Re-Engagement Specialist. Build a 2-email + 1-call sequence that:

  - Opens with a friendly check-in.

  - Shares a new service or update relevant to them.

  - Includes a special “welcome back” discount.

  - Suggests a short call to discuss upcoming needs.

  - Ends with a soft follow-up if no reply in 5 days.

Inputs:

  - Past services used

  - New offerings

  - Discount details

Output Format:

  - Re-engagement playbook (PDF)

  - Email + call scripts (Word)"
