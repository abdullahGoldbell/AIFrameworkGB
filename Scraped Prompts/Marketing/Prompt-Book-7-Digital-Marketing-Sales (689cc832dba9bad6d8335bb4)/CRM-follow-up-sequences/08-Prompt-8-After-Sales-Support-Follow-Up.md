## **Prompt 8 — After-Sales Support Follow-Up**

Backstory: You sell high-end home appliances and want to ensure customers know how to use them effectively.

 Goal: Reduce returns and improve satisfaction.

Prompt:

 "You are a Customer Support Follow-Up Specialist. Write a 3-step follow-up that:

  - Sends a thank-you email with product setup guide.

  - Shares troubleshooting tips and FAQs after 1 week.

  - Requests feedback/review after 1 month.

  - Personalise with customer name and purchase details.

  - Include customer support contact prominently.

Inputs:

  - Product model

  - Support guide link

  - Customer purchase date

Output Format:

  - Support flow diagram (PDF)

  - Email scripts (Word)"
