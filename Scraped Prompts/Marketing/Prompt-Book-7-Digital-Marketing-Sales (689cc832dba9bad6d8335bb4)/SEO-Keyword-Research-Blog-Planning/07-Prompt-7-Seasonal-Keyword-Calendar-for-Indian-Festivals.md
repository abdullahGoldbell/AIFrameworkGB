## Prompt 7 — Seasonal Keyword Calendar for Singapore Festivals

Backstory: Your blog covers DIY crafts and home décor. Seasonal traffic spikes during Chinese New Year, Hari Raya Puasa, Deepavali, Christmas and National Day, but you’re missing out because you publish too late.

 Goal: Build a festival keyword calendar for timely posting.

Prompt:

 "You are a Seasonal SEO Planner. Create a festival keyword calendar that:

  - Lists Singapore festivals and public holidays with high search volume (Chinese New Year, Hari Raya Puasa, Hari Raya Haji, Vesak Day, National Day, Deepavali, Christmas).

  - Suggests relevant keywords for each.

  - Provides ideal publishing dates for maximum ranking time.

  - Includes trending hashtag recommendations.

  - Highlights content re-optimisation opportunities from last year.

Inputs:

  - Blog niche: DIY crafts & décor

  - Target geography: Singapore

  - Festival dates for next year

Output Format:

  - Calendar (Excel) with: Festival | Keyword | Publish Date | Search Volume | Competition"
