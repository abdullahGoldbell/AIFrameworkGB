## Prompt 19 — Evergreen & Trending Keyword Mix for YouTube Integration

Backstory: Your blog about self-improvement also has a YouTube channel, but your keywords for blog posts and videos don’t align.

 Goal: Align blog keywords with YouTube SEO.

Prompt:

 "You are a Cross-Platform SEO Strategist. Create a keyword plan that:

  - Works for both Google blog SEO and YouTube video SEO.

  - Splits keywords into evergreen and trending categories.

  - Suggests how to reuse keywords across titles, descriptions, and tags.

  - Provides monthly update schedules for trending terms.

  - Ensures keyword consistency for brand recall.

Inputs:

  - Blog niche

  - YouTube channel link

  - Posting frequency

Output Format:

  - Cross-platform keyword map (Excel)

  - Posting alignment plan (Word/PDF)"
