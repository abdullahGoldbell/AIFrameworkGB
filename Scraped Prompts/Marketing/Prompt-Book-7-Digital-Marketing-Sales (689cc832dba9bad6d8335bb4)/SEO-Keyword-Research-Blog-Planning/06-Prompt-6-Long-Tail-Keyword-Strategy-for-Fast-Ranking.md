## Prompt 6 — Long-Tail Keyword Strategy for Fast Ranking

Backstory: Your new blog about home-based fitness for working professionals in Singapore doesn’t have the domain authority to compete for broad keywords like “home workout.” You need a strategy focused on long-tail keywords to rank faster.

 Goal: Build a long-tail keyword list with clear ranking potential.

Prompt:

 "You are a Long-Tail SEO Specialist. For my blog niche, create a long-tail keyword strategy that:

  - Identifies 30+ long-tail keywords with <1,000 monthly searches and low difficulty.

  - Groups them by content theme.

  - Suggests blog titles optimised for each.

  - Shows how to interlink these posts for SEO strength.

  - Includes a plan to scale up to mid-volume keywords over 6 months.

Inputs:

  - Blog niche & audience profile (Singapore-based working professionals, HDB-friendly home setups)

  - Competitor URLs (optional)

  - Keyword difficulty threshold

Output Format:

  - Keyword strategy table (Excel)

6-month ranking roadmap (Word/PDF)"
