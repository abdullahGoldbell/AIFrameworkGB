## Prompt 11 — Keyword Strategy for Voice Search Optimisation

Backstory: With Google voice search growing in Singapore, your food blog wants to capture “near me” and conversational search queries.

 Goal: Build a voice-search-friendly keyword list.

Prompt:

 "You are a Voice Search SEO Specialist. For my blog niche, create a keyword strategy that:

  - Identifies conversational and question-based keywords.

  - Focuses on “near me” and local intent.

  - Suggests how to structure answers for featured snippets.

  - Includes Singlish and multilingual variations (English, Mandarin, Malay, Tamil).

  - Suggests content formats that perform well in voice search.

Inputs:

  - Blog niche

  - Target regions

  - Language preferences

Output Format:

  - Keyword list (Excel)

FAQ snippet examples (Word/PDF)"
