## Prompt 16 — Backlink Keyword Strategy for Blog Growth

Backstory: You’re running a finance advice blog but struggling to get backlinks. You know that high-quality backlinks require targeting keywords that naturally attract link-worthy content.

 Goal: Identify keywords ideal for generating backlinks.

Prompt:

 "You are a Backlink SEO Strategist. Based on my niche, identify:

  - Keywords that naturally attract links (statistics, research-based terms, “ultimate guide” style).

  - Competitors’ top-linked pages and their target keywords.

  - Content ideas using these keywords that other sites would reference.

  - Outreach opportunities based on keyword targets.

  - A plan for integrating these keywords into my blog strategy.

Inputs:

  - Blog niche

  - Competitor URLs

  - Target regions

Output Format:

  - Keyword + backlink potential table (Excel)

Outreach list (Word/PDF)"
