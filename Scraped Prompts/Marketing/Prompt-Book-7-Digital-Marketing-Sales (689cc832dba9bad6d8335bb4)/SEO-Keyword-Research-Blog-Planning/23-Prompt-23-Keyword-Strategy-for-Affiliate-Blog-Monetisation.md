## Prompt 23 — Keyword Strategy for Affiliate Blog Monetisation

Backstory: You’re monetising via affiliate marketing but your keywords don’t convert well.

 Goal: Create a keyword plan optimised for affiliate sales.

Prompt:

 "You are an Affiliate SEO Strategist. For my blog, identify:

  - Keywords with strong purchase intent.

  - Product-focused blog topic ideas.

  - Optimised title formats for higher CTR.

  - Recommended affiliate link placement strategies.

  - Conversion optimisation tips based on keywords.

Inputs:

  - Affiliate products/services list

  - Target audience profile

  - Competitor URLs

Output Format:

  - Affiliate keyword list (Excel)

  - Post template for affiliate content (Word/PDF)"
