## Prompt 25 — Evergreen SEO Growth Plan for 12 Months

Backstory: You want a full-year SEO roadmap so your blog grows steadily and rankings compound over time.

 Goal: Create a 12-month SEO keyword plan.

Prompt:

 "You are a Long-Term SEO Planner. Build a keyword-based SEO roadmap that:

  - Assigns keyword clusters for each month.

  - Balances evergreen and seasonal keywords.

  - Includes backlink-building milestones.

  - Tracks progress with monthly KPIs.

  - Suggests quarterly content audits.

Inputs:

  - Blog niche

  - Posting capacity (posts/month)

  - Current keyword list

Output Format:

  - 12-month roadmap (Excel)

  - KPI tracking template (Word/PDF)"
