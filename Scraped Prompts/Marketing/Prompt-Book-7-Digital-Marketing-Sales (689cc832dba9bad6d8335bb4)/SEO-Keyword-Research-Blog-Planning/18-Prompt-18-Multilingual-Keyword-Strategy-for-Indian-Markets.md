## Prompt 18 — Multilingual Keyword Strategy for Singapore Markets

Backstory: Your recipe blog wants to expand reach by targeting Mandarin, Malay, and Tamil search terms along with English to engage Singapore’s multicultural audience.

 Goal: Create a multilingual keyword plan.

Prompt:

 "You are a Multilingual SEO Planner. For my blog, create a keyword plan that:

  - Translates core keywords into Mandarin, Malay, and Tamil.

  - Identifies localised search intent differences across Singapore’s language communities.

  - Suggests culturally relevant content ideas.

  - Includes keyword difficulty and volume data for each language.

  - Recommends content localisation strategies.

Inputs:

  - Blog niche

  - Target languages

  - Competitor URLs (if available)

Output Format:

  - Multilingual keyword table (Excel)

  - Language-specific posting guide (Word/PDF)"
