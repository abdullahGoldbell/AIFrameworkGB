## Prompt 2 — Blog Content Calendar Based on Keyword Clusters

Backstory: You’ve already gathered 50–60 SEO keywords but have no structured posting plan. Without a content calendar, you risk random posting and missed ranking opportunities.

 Goal: Create a 3-month blog posting schedule based on keyword clusters.

Prompt:

 "You are a Keyword Clustering & Content Planning Expert. Based on my keyword list, create a blog content calendar that:

  - Groups keywords into 5–6 topical clusters.

  - Assigns one blog topic per week for 3 months.

  - Includes recommended post titles optimised for CTR.

  - Suggests internal linking opportunities between posts.

  - Highlights seasonal posting opportunities.

Inputs:

  - Keyword list (Excel/CSV)

  - Blog posting frequency (1/week, 2/week, etc.)

  - Seasonal or event-based topics to prioritise

Output Format:

Content calendar (Excel) with: Date | Blog Title | Target Keyword | Secondary Keywords | Internal Links"
