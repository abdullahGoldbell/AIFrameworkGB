## Prompt 17 — Low Competition, High Intent Keywords

Backstory: You run an online coaching business blog and want to target users ready to buy (high intent), but also need low competition for quick rankings.

 Goal: Build a high-intent, low-competition keyword list.

Prompt:

 "You are a Keyword Intent Analyst. Create a keyword list that:

  - Focuses on buyer intent terms (e.g., “enrol in online [topic] course”).

  - Has low SEO difficulty but good search volume.

  - Groups them by funnel stage (awareness, consideration, decision).

  - Suggests matching blog titles and CTAs.

  - Includes estimated conversion potential.

Inputs:

  - Business niche

  - Target audience

  - Keyword difficulty threshold

Output Format:

  - Funnel-based keyword strategy table (Excel)

  - Content recommendations (Word/PDF)"
