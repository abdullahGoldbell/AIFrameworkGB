## Prompt 3 — Competitor Gap Analysis for SEO Advantage

Backstory: Your blog’s niche is healthy local Singapore recipes (hawker favourites made lighter, Peranakan, Malay, Chinese, and Indian home cooking), but your main competitors dominate Google’s first page. You need to find keywords and topics they’ve missed.

 Goal: Identify competitor content gaps and untapped keyword opportunities.

Prompt:

 "You are an SEO Gap Analysis Specialist. Analyse the top 5 competitors in my niche and:

  - List keywords they rank for but I don’t.

  - Identify keywords with high traffic potential but low competition.

  - Suggest unique blog topics targeting these gaps.

  - Highlight missed internal linking opportunities.

  - Provide ranking difficulty scores for each keyword.

Inputs:

  - Competitor URLs (list)

  - My blog URL

  - Geographic focus: Singapore

Output Format:

  - Gap analysis table (Excel): Keyword | Competitor Rank | My Rank | Volume | Difficulty | Suggested Topic"
