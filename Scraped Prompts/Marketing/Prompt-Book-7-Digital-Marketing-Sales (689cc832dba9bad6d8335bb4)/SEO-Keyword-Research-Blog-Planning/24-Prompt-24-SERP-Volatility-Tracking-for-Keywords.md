## Prompt 24 — SERP Volatility Tracking for Keywords

Backstory: Some of your keywords jump between positions in Google SERPs and you want to understand why.

 Goal: Track keyword SERP volatility.

Prompt:

 "You are a SERP Volatility Analyst. For my keyword list, create a tracking plan that:

  - Monitors daily position changes.

  - Identifies algorithm update impacts.

  - Suggests stabilising strategies.

  - Flags high-risk keywords.

  - Provides monthly trend reports.

Inputs:

  - Keyword list (Excel)

  - Google Search Console access (optional)

Output Format:

  - SERP volatility chart (Excel)

Stabilisation strategy guide (Word/PDF)"
