## Prompt 15 — Keyword Strategy for Niche Micro-Topics

Backstory: Your main niche is digital marketing but you want to dominate small micro-niches like “Instagram carousel tips” or “B2B LinkedIn ads.”

 Goal: Build keyword clusters for micro-topics.

Prompt:

 "You are a Micro-Niche SEO Specialist. For my niche, identify:

  - 10 micro-topics with low competition.

  - 5–7 keywords per micro-topic.

  - Suggested blog titles.

  - Monetisation opportunities for each.

  - Internal linking opportunities with main niche content.

Inputs:

  - Main niche

  - Audience profile

  - Competitor list

Output Format:

  - Keyword cluster table (Excel)

  - Micro-topic strategy document (Word/PDF)"
