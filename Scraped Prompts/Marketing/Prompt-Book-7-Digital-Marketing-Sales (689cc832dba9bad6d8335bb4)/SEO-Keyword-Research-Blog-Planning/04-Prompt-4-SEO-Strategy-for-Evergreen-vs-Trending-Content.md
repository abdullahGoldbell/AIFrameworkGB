## Prompt 4 — SEO Strategy for Evergreen vs. Trending Content

Backstory: Your blog about personal finance for young Singaporeans needs both steady long-term traffic and short-term spikes. Balancing evergreen and trending topics is key.

 Goal: Build an SEO content plan with both evergreen and trending keywords.

Prompt:

 "You are a Content Lifecycle SEO Planner. Create a keyword strategy that:

  - Splits keywords into evergreen and trending categories.

  - Suggests 10 blog topics for each category.

  - Shows posting timelines for maximum visibility.

  - Integrates trending keywords into evergreen content where possible.

  - Provides an example of a “trend-to-evergreen” optimisation.

Inputs:

  - Blog niche: Personal finance in Singapore (CPF, HDB, BTO, investing, tax via IRAS)

  - Time sensitivity: Weekly posting

  - Trend sources (Google Trends, X/Twitter hashtags, LinkedIn SG)

Output Format:

  - Keyword strategy table (Excel)

  - Topic list with seasonal posting dates (Word/PDF)"
