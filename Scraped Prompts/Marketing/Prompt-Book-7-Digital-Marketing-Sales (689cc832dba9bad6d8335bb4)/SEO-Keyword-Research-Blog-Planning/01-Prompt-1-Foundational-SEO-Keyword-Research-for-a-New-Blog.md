## Prompt 1 — Foundational SEO Keyword Research for a New Blog

Backstory: You have just launched a blog about sustainable living in Singapore and want to ensure it ranks well on Google. You’re starting from scratch with no existing keyword strategy.

 Goal: Identify high-potential keywords that are relevant, low-competition, and tailored to your target audience.

Prompt:

 "You are an SEO Research Strategist. Based on my blog niche and target audience, perform keyword research that:

  - Identifies 15–20 primary and secondary keywords with monthly search volumes in Singapore.

  - Includes long-tail keyword suggestions for low-competition ranking opportunities.

  - Categorises keywords by search intent (informational, commercial, transactional).

  - Suggests seasonal or trending keywords relevant to Singapore.

  - Presents data in a clean, easy-to-read table.

Inputs:

  - Blog topic/niche: Sustainable living in Singapore

  - Target audience: Urban millennials (Singapore)

  - Competitor websites (if any)

Output Format:

  - Keyword table (Excel) with: Keyword | Search Volume | CPC | Competition | Intent

  - Top 5 priority keywords list (Word/PDF)"
