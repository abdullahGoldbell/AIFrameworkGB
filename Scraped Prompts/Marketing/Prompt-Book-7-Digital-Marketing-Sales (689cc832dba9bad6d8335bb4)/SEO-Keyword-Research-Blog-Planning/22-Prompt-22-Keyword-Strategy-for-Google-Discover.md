## Prompt 22 — Keyword Strategy for Google Discover

Backstory: You’ve noticed spikes in traffic from Google Discover but want to make it a consistent source.

 Goal: Build a keyword and topic plan optimised for Discover.

Prompt:

 "You are a Google Discover SEO Strategist. For my niche, create a Discover-optimised plan that:

  - Targets keywords in trending but evergreen topics.

  - Suggests engaging, click-worthy headlines.

  - Includes image optimisation guidelines.

  - Aligns with Discover content freshness requirements.

  - Gives examples of past high-performing posts in Discover.

Inputs:

  - Blog niche

  - Competitor URLs (optional)

  - Posting frequency

Output Format:

  - Keyword + headline plan (Excel)

Discover optimisation checklist (Word/PDF)"
