### Prompt 7 — Writing Compliance & Policy Documentation

Backstory: You’ve launched a fintech app in Singapore, but regulatory requirements demand that you publish clear compliance policies for users.

Goal: Draft customer-facing compliance documentation that is accurate but easy to understand.

Prompt:

 "You are a Regulatory Documentation Specialist. I want you to create compliance and privacy policies for our fintech app.

Your task:

  - Review applicable regulations (MAS guidelines, PDPA, PCI DSS).

  - Write privacy policy, data handling policy, and terms of service.

  - Ensure language is plain and free of legal jargon where possible.

  - Include diagrams showing data flow and storage locations.

  - Format for both web and PDF publication.

Output format: Policy documents in DOCX, PDF, and HTML formats.

Input Files & Code Section:

  - Current draft compliance notes.

  - Legal team’s checklist.

Data flow diagrams."
