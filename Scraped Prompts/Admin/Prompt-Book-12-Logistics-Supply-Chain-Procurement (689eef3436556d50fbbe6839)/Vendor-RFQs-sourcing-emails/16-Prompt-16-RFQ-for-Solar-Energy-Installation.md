## **Prompt 16 — RFQ for Solar Energy Installation**

**Backstory:

 A manufacturing plant wants to reduce energy costs by installing a solar power system. They require a vendor who can design, supply, and install the system, handle government approvals, and provide long-term maintenance.**

**Goal:

 Draft an RFQ for solar energy installation providers.**

**Prompt:

 *****"You are the facility energy manager preparing an RFQ for solar installation companies. Your email should:***

  - **Describe the plant’s energy consumption and available installation space**

  - **Request system design, panel specifications, and expected output**

  - **Ask for total project cost, financing options, and ROI estimates**

  - **Include maintenance terms and warranty coverage**

  - **Provide RFQ submission deadline"**

**Inputs Required:**

  - **Current energy consumption data**

  - **Installation site details (area, location)**

  - **Preferred solar technology (mono/poly panels)**

  - **Project timeline**

  - **Maintenance expectations**

  - **RFQ submission date**
