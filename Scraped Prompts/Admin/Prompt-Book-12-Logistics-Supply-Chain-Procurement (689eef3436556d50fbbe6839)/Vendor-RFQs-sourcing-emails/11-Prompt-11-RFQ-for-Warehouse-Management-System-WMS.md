## **Prompt 11 — RFQ for Warehouse Management System (WMS)**

Backstory:

 A logistics company is upgrading its warehouse operations and needs a software system to handle inventory management, shipment tracking, and integration with their ERP. The system must be scalable for future expansion and offer robust customer support.

Goal:

 Prepare a detailed RFQ for a WMS vendor.

Prompt:

 *"You are the IT head drafting an RFQ email for WMS providers. Your email should:*

  - Describe your current warehouse operations and challenges

  - List required software features (inventory tracking, order management, analytics)

  - Specify integration requirements with existing systems

  - Ask for licensing, training, and support details

  - Request pricing for setup and subscription plans

  - Provide RFQ submission deadline"

Inputs Required:

  - Warehouse details (size, operations)

  - List of desired features/modules

  - Integration requirements

  - User licenses needed

  - Go-live timeline

  - RFQ submission deadline
