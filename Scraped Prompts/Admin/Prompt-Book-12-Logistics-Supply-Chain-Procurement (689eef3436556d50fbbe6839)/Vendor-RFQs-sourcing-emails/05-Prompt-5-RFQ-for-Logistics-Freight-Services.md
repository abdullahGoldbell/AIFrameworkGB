## **Prompt 5 — RFQ for Logistics & Freight Services**

Backstory:

 A Singapore-based electronics distributor is expanding regionally across Southeast Asia and requires a reliable logistics partner for both inbound and outbound shipments. The company needs multi-modal transport options (sea + air + land) and must ensure strict adherence to delivery schedules to avoid delays in fulfilling customer orders. The RFQ should solicit pricing, delivery guarantees, and damage liability clauses from multiple logistics firms.

Goal:

 Prepare an RFQ email for domestic and regional logistics and freight services.

Prompt:

 *"You are the supply chain manager preparing an RFQ email to logistics companies. Your email should:*

  - Describe the nature and volume of goods shipped monthly

  - Outline pickup and delivery locations, including domestic Singapore routes and Southeast Asia cross-border requirements

  - Request mode-wise pricing (sea, air, land/trucking)

  - Ask for delivery SLAs, tracking capabilities, and damage liability terms

  - Specify insurance coverage requirements

  - Provide a deadline for submitting the quotation"

Inputs Required:

  - Type of goods shipped

  - Average monthly shipment volume

  - Pickup and delivery locations

  - Preferred modes of transport

  - Delivery timelines required

  - Insurance requirements

  - RFQ submission deadline
