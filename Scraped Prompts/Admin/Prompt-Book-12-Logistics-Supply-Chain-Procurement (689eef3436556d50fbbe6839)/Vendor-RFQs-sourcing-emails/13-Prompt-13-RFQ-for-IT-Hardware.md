## **Prompt 13 — RFQ for IT Hardware**

Backstory:

 A corporate office is upgrading its IT infrastructure to accommodate new hires. They require laptops, monitors, docking stations, and peripherals from reliable vendors who can offer volume discounts and post-purchase technical support.

Goal:

 Draft an RFQ email for IT hardware procurement.

Prompt:

 *"You are the IT procurement lead preparing an RFQ for hardware suppliers. Your email should:*

  - Specify the type and configuration of each hardware item

  - Include brand/model preferences, if any

  - Ask for pricing with warranty terms and support packages

  - Request delivery schedule and installation/setup services

  - Include payment terms and submission deadline"

Inputs Required:

  - Hardware types and specifications

  - Quantity for each item

  - Brand/model preference

  - Delivery location and timeline

  - Warranty/support expectations

  - RFQ submission date
