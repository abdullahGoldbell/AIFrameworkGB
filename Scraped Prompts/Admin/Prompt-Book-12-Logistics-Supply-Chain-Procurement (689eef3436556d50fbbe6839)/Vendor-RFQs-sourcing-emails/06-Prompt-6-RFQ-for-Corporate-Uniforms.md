## **Prompt 6 — RFQ for Corporate Uniforms**

Backstory:

 A regional hospitality chain headquartered in Singapore is rebranding and wants to roll out new uniforms for all front-line and back-office staff. They need a vendor who can handle custom designs, provide multiple size fittings, and ensure durable fabric quality for long-term use. The procurement team plans to distribute the uniforms across 20 properties spanning Singapore and Southeast Asia.

Goal:

 Draft an RFQ for sourcing custom corporate uniforms.

Prompt:

 *"You are the brand operations manager preparing an RFQ for uniform suppliers. Your email should:*

  - Introduce your brand and outline the rebranding initiative

  - Specify types of uniforms required (front desk, housekeeping, F&B, admin)

  - Detail material, stitching quality, and customization (logo embroidery, colors)

  - Ask for size charts, trial samples, and bulk pricing

  - Include delivery timelines for each property location

  - State warranty/replacement policies and submission deadline"

Inputs Required:

  - List of uniform types

  - Quantity per location

  - Material and design preferences

  - Branding requirements (logo placement, color codes)

  - Delivery schedule per location

  - RFQ submission deadline
