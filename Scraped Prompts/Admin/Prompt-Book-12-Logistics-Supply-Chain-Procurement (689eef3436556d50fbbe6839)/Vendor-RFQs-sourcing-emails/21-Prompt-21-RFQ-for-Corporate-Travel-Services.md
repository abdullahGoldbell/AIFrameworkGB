## **Prompt 21 — RFQ for Corporate Travel Services**

Backstory:

 A consultancy firm requires a travel management partner to handle domestic and international bookings for 100+ employees. They want negotiated rates, 24/7 support, and expense reporting.

Goal:

 Draft an RFQ for corporate travel agencies.

Prompt:

 *"You are the admin coordinator preparing an RFQ for corporate travel providers. Your email should:*

  - Describe travel requirements (domestic/international) and volume

  - Request details on booking processes, discounts, and partnerships

  - Ask for technology integration for booking and expense tracking

  - Include customer service expectations and reporting needs

  - Provide RFQ submission deadline"

Inputs Required:

  - Annual travel volume estimates

  - Destinations and frequency

  - Special service requirements (visas, insurance)

  - Preferred travel class and budget range

  - RFQ submission date
