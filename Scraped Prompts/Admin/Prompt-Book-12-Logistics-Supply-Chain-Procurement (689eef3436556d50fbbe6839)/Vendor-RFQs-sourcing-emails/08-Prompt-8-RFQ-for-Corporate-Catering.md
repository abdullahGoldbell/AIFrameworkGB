## **Prompt 8 — RFQ for Corporate Catering**

Backstory:

 A Singapore company with 500 employees wants to introduce a daily lunch service to improve employee satisfaction and health. They require vendors who can provide balanced meals, accommodate Singapore's diverse dietary needs (halal, vegetarian, no pork/no lard), and ensure timely delivery to their CBD office. The RFQ should request menus, cost per meal, and SFA hygiene compliance certifications.

Goal:

 Draft a vendor RFQ for daily corporate catering services.

Prompt:

 *"You are the office admin preparing an RFQ email for corporate catering vendors. Your email should:*

  - State the total headcount and meal type requirements

  - Ask for sample menus with halal, vegetarian, and non-halal options reflecting local cuisine variety (Chinese, Malay, Indian, Western)

  - Request details on meal rotation frequency

  - Include delivery timelines and serving method preferences

  - Ask for pricing per meal and monthly contracts

  - Require SFA hygiene certifications and submission deadline"

Inputs Required:

  - Number of employees to serve

  - Meal preferences/dietary restrictions (halal, vegetarian, allergens)

  - Delivery location and timing

  - Serving format (buffet, packed bento meals)

  - RFQ submission deadline
