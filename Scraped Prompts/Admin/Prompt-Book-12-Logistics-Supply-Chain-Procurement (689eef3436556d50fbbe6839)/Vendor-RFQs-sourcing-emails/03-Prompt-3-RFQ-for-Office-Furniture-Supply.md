## **Prompt 3 — RFQ for Office Furniture Supply**

Backstory:

 A new IT firm is setting up a 200-seat office space and requires ergonomic chairs, modular desks, and storage units. The HR and Admin teams want furniture that meets ergonomic standards, offers durability, and comes with a warranty. They will be sending this RFQ to multiple vendors to compare prices and delivery timelines.

Goal:

 Draft a detailed RFQ for procuring ergonomic office furniture.

Prompt:

 *"You are the office admin preparing an RFQ email for furniture vendors. Your email should:*

  - Briefly describe your company’s setup and need for ergonomic furniture

  - Provide a list of required items with quantity and specifications

  - Request multiple color/finish options with pricing

  - Ask for warranty, after-sales service, and delivery details

  - Specify the expected delivery date and installation requirements

  - State the RFQ submission deadline"

Inputs Required:

  - List of furniture items with specifications

  - Total quantity for each item

  - Preferred colors/finishes

  - Delivery and installation address

  - Warranty expectations

  - RFQ submission deadline
