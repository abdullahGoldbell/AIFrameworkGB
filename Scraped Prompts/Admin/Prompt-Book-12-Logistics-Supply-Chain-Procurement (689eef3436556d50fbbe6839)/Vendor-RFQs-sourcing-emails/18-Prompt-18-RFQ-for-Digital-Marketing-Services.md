## **Prompt 18 — RFQ for Digital Marketing Services**

Backstory:

 A mid-sized retail brand wants to expand its online presence through SEO, social media campaigns, and influencer collaborations. They need a digital marketing agency that can create and execute a 6-month strategy with measurable KPIs.

Goal:

 Prepare an RFQ for digital marketing agencies.

Prompt:

 *"You are the brand manager preparing an RFQ for digital marketing vendors. Your email should:*

  - Outline your brand’s current online presence and objectives

  - Request a proposed marketing strategy with deliverables

  - Ask for case studies of past campaigns

  - Include detailed pricing for each service offered

  - Request timeline, performance metrics, and reporting structure

  - Provide RFQ submission deadline"

Inputs Required:

  - Brand overview and objectives

  - Duration of campaign (e.g., 6 months)

  - Desired marketing channels

  - KPIs and success metrics

  - Budget range

  - RFQ submission date
