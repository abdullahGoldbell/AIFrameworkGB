## **Prompt 9 — RFQ for Safety Equipment**

Backstory:

 A new infrastructure project in Singapore requires personal protective equipment (PPE) for all site workers. Items include helmets, gloves, safety shoes, and high-visibility jackets. Vendors must supply SS (Singapore Standard) / ISO-certified gear that meets MOM Workplace Safety and Health (WSH) requirements, and ensure timely delivery before project commencement.

Goal:

 Draft a detailed RFQ for PPE procurement.

Prompt:

 *"You are the site safety officer preparing an RFQ email for PPE vendors. Your email should:*

  - List all PPE items required with relevant certifications (SS/ISO, MOM WSH compliance)

  - Mention size variations and customization (logo printing) if needed

  - Request bulk pricing, warranty, and replacement terms

  - Include delivery timelines and compliance with safety standards

  - Specify insurance coverage and RFQ submission deadline"

Inputs Required:

  - List of PPE items and specifications

  - Quantity per item

  - Certification requirements

  - Delivery location and deadline

  - RFQ submission date
