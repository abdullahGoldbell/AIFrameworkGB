## **Prompt 17 — RFQ for Corporate Catering Services**

Backstory:

 A multinational company with its regional headquarters in Singapore is looking for a reliable catering partner to serve daily meals to 300 employees at its corporate office. The menu must include halal, vegetarian, and non-halal options reflecting Singapore's multicultural workforce, with strict SFA hygiene standards. The company is seeking a vendor who can deliver variety, nutritional balance, and competitive pricing.

Goal:

 Draft an RFQ for corporate meal catering vendors.

Prompt:

 *"You are the admin lead preparing an RFQ for catering vendors. Your email should:*

  - Describe the meal service requirement (breakfast/lunch/dinner) and frequency

  - Include dietary variety (halal, vegetarian, non-halal) and nutritional standards

  - Request sample menus and cost per head in SGD

  - Ask for SFA hygiene certifications and compliance proof

  - Include delivery logistics and setup requirements

  - Provide RFQ submission deadline"

Inputs Required:

  - Number of employees to be served

  - Meal frequency (daily/weekly)

  - Dietary requirements (halal/vegetarian/non-halal)

  - Meal service timings

  - Budget per head (SGD)

  - RFQ submission date
