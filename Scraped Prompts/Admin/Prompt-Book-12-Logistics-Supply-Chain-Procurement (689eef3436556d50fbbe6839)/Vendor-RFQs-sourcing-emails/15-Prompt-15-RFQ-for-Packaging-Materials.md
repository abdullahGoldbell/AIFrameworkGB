## **Prompt 15 — RFQ for Packaging Materials**

Backstory:

 A Singapore-based e-commerce brand needs sustainable and durable packaging for shipping fragile items across Singapore and the Southeast Asia region. They want eco-friendly materials, custom branding on packaging, and bulk supply capacity. Vendors must comply with NEA (National Environment Agency) environmental regulations and Singapore's mandatory packaging reporting requirements.

Goal:

 Prepare an RFQ for eco-friendly packaging material suppliers.

Prompt:

 *"You are the operations head creating an RFQ for packaging vendors. Your email should:*

  - List packaging types (boxes, fillers, tapes, labels) with sizes

  - Specify material preferences (recycled, biodegradable)

  - Request branding and customization details

  - Ask for pricing with volume-based discounts

  - Require delivery timelines and quality guarantees

  - Provide RFQ submission deadline"

Inputs Required:

  - Packaging types and sizes

  - Material preferences

  - Branding requirements

  - Delivery schedule

  - Order quantity

  - RFQ submission date
