## **Prompt 10 — RFQ for Printing & Branding Services**

Backstory:

 A retail chain is launching a seasonal sale and needs banners, posters, and brochures printed in bulk. The vendor must deliver high-quality prints with color accuracy and meet strict deadlines to align with the campaign launch.

Goal:

 Create an RFQ for printing and branding services.

Prompt:

 *"You are the marketing manager preparing an RFQ for printing vendors. Your email should:*

  - Specify the types of materials to be printed (banners, flyers, posters)

  - Mention size, paper quality, and finish requirements

  - State quantities for each item

  - Request proofing process details and turnaround time

  - Ask for pricing with bulk discounts and delivery terms

  - Provide RFQ submission deadline"

Inputs Required:

  - List of print materials with specifications

  - Quantity per item

  - Preferred paper/material type

  - Delivery date

  - RFQ submission deadline
