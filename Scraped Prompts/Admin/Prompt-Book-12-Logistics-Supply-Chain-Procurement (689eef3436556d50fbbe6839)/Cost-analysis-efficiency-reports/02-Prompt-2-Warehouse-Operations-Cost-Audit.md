### **Prompt 2 — Warehouse Operations Cost Audit**

Backstory:

 A retail chain operates 4 warehouses across Singapore (Tuas, Jurong, Changi, and Woodlands). Over the past year, warehouse operational costs have increased by 25%, with electricity, labour, and storage being the biggest contributors. Management wants to understand the cost structure and find areas for automation or process improvement without disrupting operations.

Goal:

 Identify warehouse cost inefficiencies and propose automation-driven savings.

Prompt:

 "You are a Logistics Efficiency Consultant. Conduct a warehouse operations cost audit for 4 locations across Singapore.

  - Break down costs into labour, utilities, storage, and maintenance.

  - Benchmark against industry averages for similar-sized facilities in Singapore and the wider region.

  - Highlight cost overruns and their root causes.

  - Recommend automation tools or workflow changes.

  - Provide a projected ROI in S$ for implementing changes."

Inputs Required:

  - Monthly warehouse cost breakdown (S$)

  - Staff headcount and shift details

  - Utility bills and maintenance logs

  - Equipment and automation usage data

  - Industry benchmark data
