### **Prompt 7 — Cold Chain Delivery Scheduling for Perishables**

Backstory:

 You operate a cold chain logistics business in Singapore, delivering perishable goods like dairy, seafood, and fresh produce to supermarkets (FairPrice, Cold Storage), restaurants, and hotels across the island. Your refrigerated trucks must maintain specific temperature ranges, especially given Singapore’s year-round tropical heat, and every hour of delay risks spoilage. Current routes are planned manually, leading to inefficiencies when there’s unexpected congestion on the AYE/PIE or a last-minute high-priority delivery. You need an optimised plan that considers delivery deadlines, product sensitivity, and shortest travel paths while maintaining temperature control.

Goal:

 Develop an optimised cold chain delivery schedule that ensures timely deliveries while preserving product quality.

Prompt:

 "You are a Cold Chain Logistics Expert. Create a delivery plan for refrigerated trucks operating across Singapore:

  - Prioritise perishable goods based on shelf life and urgency.

  - Group deliveries geographically (CBD, Jurong, Tampines, Woodlands) to minimise travel time.

  - Factor in expressway congestion and peak-hour avoidance.

  - Schedule temperature checks at every delivery point in line with SFA cold chain guidelines.

  - Include contingency plans for vehicle breakdowns or route closures."

Inputs Required:

  - List of delivery addresses with deadlines

  - Product category & temperature requirements

  - Number and capacity of refrigerated trucks

  - Real-time traffic data access

  - Priority customer list
