### **Prompt 1 — Optimised Route Plan for Multiple Deliveries**

Backstory:

 You are the operations head for a mid-sized courier company based in Singapore. Your team manages 15 delivery vans covering over 120 daily orders across the island, and you’ve been facing late deliveries due to unplanned routing. Your drivers sometimes take overlapping paths or face unexpected congestion along the PIE/CTE expressways. You need a systematic way to optimise routes so that each driver covers the shortest possible distance while ensuring timely delivery for high-priority packages. You also want to factor in vehicle capacity and driver working hours under MOM regulations.

Goal:

 Create an optimised delivery route plan that reduces fuel usage and delivery times while improving driver productivity.

Prompt:

 "You are an experienced Logistics Planner with expertise in traffic prediction and route optimisation. Design a delivery schedule and route map for 15 vans covering 120 orders across Singapore.

  - Use real-time traffic patterns and historical data for route planning.

  - Assign orders to drivers based on geographical clusters (CBD, Jurong, Tampines, Woodlands, etc.) and delivery urgency.

  - Ensure no route exceeds vehicle capacity or MOM-compliant driver working hours.

  - Include estimated delivery times for each stop.

  - Provide contingency routes in case of road closures, ERP gantry detours, or delays.

  - Present output in both tabular form (order sequence, time, distance) and a visual route map format."

Inputs Required:

  - Number of delivery vehicles & their capacity

  - Number of orders and delivery addresses

  - Time window for deliveries

  - Priority packages list (if any)

  - Traffic data availability (real-time or historical)
