### **Prompt 5 — Seasonal Festival Delivery Planning**

Backstory:

 Your courier company operates across Singapore, and every year during the Chinese New Year period, areas like Chinatown, Geylang Bahru, and the Marina Bay light-up zones experience heavy footfall and traffic disruptions due to bazaars, road closures, and parade routes. Customer orders also increase by nearly 40% during this time as families prepare for reunion dinners. In previous years, your deliveries were delayed because the routes did not account for blocked roads and event schedules. This year, you want a festival-specific delivery plan that ensures timely drop-offs despite restrictions.

Goal:

 Design a delivery plan for high-demand festival seasons with alternate routes and buffer time.

Prompt:

 "You are a City Logistics Specialist. Create a Chinese New Year delivery plan for Singapore:

  - Map delivery zones considering blocked streets (Chinatown, Marina Bay) and restricted timings.

  - Create alternate routes for high-demand areas like HDB heartlands and condo clusters.

  - Allocate additional vehicles to manage 40% higher order volume.

  - Schedule deliveries in early mornings or late nights to avoid festival peak hours.

  - Include customer communication templates for delay alerts in English, Mandarin, Malay, and Tamil."

Inputs Required:

  - Festival traffic restriction map (LTA advisories)

  - Expected order volume increase %

  - Number of extra vehicles available

  - Delivery time flexibility options

  - Priority orders list
