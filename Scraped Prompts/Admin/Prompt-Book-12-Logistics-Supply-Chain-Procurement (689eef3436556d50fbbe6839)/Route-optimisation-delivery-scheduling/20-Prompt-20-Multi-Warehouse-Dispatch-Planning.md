### **Prompt 20 — Multi-Warehouse Dispatch Planning**

Backstory:

 You operate an online furniture store with warehouses in Tuas, Jurong, and Changi serving Singapore and selected Southeast Asia markets. Orders are sometimes shipped from farther warehouses due to inventory mismatches, increasing delivery costs. You want a dispatch logic that selects the best warehouse for each order while still meeting delivery time commitments.

Goal:

 Create a warehouse-aware routing and dispatch plan to reduce cost and delivery time.

Prompt:

 "You are a Warehouse Dispatch Strategist. Build a multi-warehouse delivery plan for Singapore operations:

  - Assign orders to the nearest warehouse with available stock.

  - Optimise truck loading to combine multiple area deliveries.

  - Avoid sending partial loads unless urgent.

  - Track inter-warehouse transfer requirements.

  - Provide daily cost and efficiency reports."

Inputs Required:

  - Warehouse locations and stock levels

  - Order delivery addresses and deadlines

  - Truck capacities

  - Transfer timelines between warehouses

  - Cost per km for transport
