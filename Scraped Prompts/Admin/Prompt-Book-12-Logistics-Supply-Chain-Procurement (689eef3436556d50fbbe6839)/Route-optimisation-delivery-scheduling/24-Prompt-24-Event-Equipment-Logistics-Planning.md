### **Prompt 24 — Event Equipment Logistics Planning**

Backstory:

 You operate a logistics firm that supplies staging, lighting, and audio equipment for events across Singapore — from concerts at the National Stadium and Singapore Indoor Stadium to MICE events at Marina Bay Sands and Suntec. Events often require equipment to be delivered at specific times for setup, sometimes late at night. Current routing causes delays, and drivers sometimes arrive before venues are ready. You need a time-sensitive delivery plan that aligns with event schedules.

Goal:

 Synchronise event equipment delivery with venue readiness.

Prompt:

 "You are an Event Logistics Coordinator. Create a delivery schedule for event equipment in Singapore:

  - Match delivery arrival with venue setup readiness.

  - Factor in multiple event deliveries in a single night.

  - Assign backup vehicles for urgent changes.

  - Minimise overtime driver hours.

  - Track delivery punctuality for each client."

Inputs Required:

  - Event locations and setup timelines

  - Equipment size and load requirements

  - Number of delivery vehicles

  - Venue access restrictions

  - Traffic patterns for event hours
