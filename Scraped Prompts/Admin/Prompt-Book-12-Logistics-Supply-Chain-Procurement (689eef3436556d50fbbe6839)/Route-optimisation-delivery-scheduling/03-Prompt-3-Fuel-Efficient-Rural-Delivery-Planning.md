### **Prompt 3 — Fuel-Efficient Regional Delivery Planning**

Backstory:

 You operate a regional courier service from Singapore covering cross-border deliveries into Johor and selected towns across Peninsular Malaysia, where customer drop-off points are far apart and road conditions vary. Fuel and toll costs are a major concern, and deliveries must be made twice a week to multiple townships. Your drivers often take routes that result in wasted fuel due to poor planning. You want to design a schedule that covers all destinations in the shortest distance possible, while ensuring urgent parcels reach within 24 hours.

Goal:

 Create a regional delivery schedule that minimises fuel consumption and covers all townships efficiently.

Prompt:

 "You are a Regional Logistics Planner with expertise in fuel efficiency. Prepare a delivery plan covering 30 townships across Johor and southern Peninsular Malaysia from a Singapore hub, using 5 vans.

  - Map routes that minimise total fuel and toll consumption.

  - Allocate urgent packages to the fastest available route.

  - Include a bi-weekly schedule with designated driver assignments.

  - Suggest buffer time for Causeway/Second Link border clearance and seasonal monsoon conditions.

  - Provide a contingency plan for vehicle breakdowns."

Inputs Required:

  - List of townships with GPS coordinates

  - Number of delivery vehicles & fuel type

  - Frequency of deliveries (per week)

  - Urgent vs. non-urgent package details

  - Road and border crossing condition data
