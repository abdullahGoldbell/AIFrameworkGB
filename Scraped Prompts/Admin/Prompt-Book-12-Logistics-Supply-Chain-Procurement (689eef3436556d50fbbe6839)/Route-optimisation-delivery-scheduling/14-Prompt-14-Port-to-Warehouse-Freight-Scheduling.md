### **Prompt 14 — Port-to-Warehouse Freight Scheduling**

**Backstory:

** Your company handles freight logistics from PSA Tuas Port and Pasir Panjang Terminal to warehouses across Singapore (Tuas, Jurong, Changi, and Pioneer). Ship arrivals are often delayed, causing trucks to wait idly and increasing demurrage costs. You need a scheduling system that reacts dynamically to ship ETA changes and reassigns trucks to avoid idle time while still meeting delivery commitments.

Goal:

 Develop a dynamic freight pickup and delivery schedule that reduces waiting time and costs.

Prompt:

 "You are a Port Logistics Scheduler. Build a port-to-warehouse delivery plan for Singapore’s PSA terminals:

  - Sync truck arrivals with updated ship ETAs.

  - Allocate trucks based on cargo priority and destination.

  - Include buffer trucks for last-minute shipment surges.

  - Provide real-time updates to warehouse managers.

  - Analyse cost savings from reduced idle time."

Inputs Required:

  - Ship arrival schedules & updates

  - Cargo priority classification

  - Warehouse addresses and capacities

  - Number of trucks available

  - Loading/unloading time per truck
