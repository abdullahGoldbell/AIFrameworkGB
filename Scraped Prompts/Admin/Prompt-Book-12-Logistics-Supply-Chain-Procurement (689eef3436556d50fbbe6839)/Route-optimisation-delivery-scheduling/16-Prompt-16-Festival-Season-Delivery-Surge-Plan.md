### **Prompt 16 — Festival Season Delivery Surge Plan**

Backstory:

 Your courier company in Singapore experiences a 3× spike in orders during Chinese New Year and the year-end festive period (Christmas and Hari Raya), with a high concentration of deliveries in HDB estates and condo developments. Previous years saw missed deadlines, rider fatigue, and excessive overtime costs. You want a surge-specific routing strategy that balances workload, maintains promised delivery times, and factors in lift-access constraints and pedestrian-heavy void decks during festive hours.

Goal:

 Design a festive season delivery plan that ensures timely completion despite high order volume.

Prompt:

 "You are a Seasonal Logistics Strategist. Create a Chinese New Year delivery surge plan for Singapore:

  - Predict delivery clusters based on historical order data.

  - Use motorbikes/vans for HDB estates and condo deliveries.

  - Implement time-window-based deliveries to avoid peak congestion.

  - Distribute workload evenly among riders.

  - Include overtime cost projections and efficiency improvements."

Inputs Required:

  - Historical order volume per area during Chinese New Year

  - Rider availability and vehicle type mix

  - Street and estate congestion data by hour

  - Delivery time promises per order

  - Cost per overtime hour
