### **Prompt 25 — Cross-Border E-Commerce Delivery**

Backstory:

 You manage cross-border deliveries from Singapore into Malaysia (Johor and beyond) for an e-commerce marketplace. Customs clearance delays at the Tuas and Woodlands checkpoints, plus varying road conditions on the North-South Expressway, cause unpredictable delivery times. You want a route and scheduling system that accounts for border wait times and provides accurate ETAs to customers.

Goal:

 Plan cross-border e-commerce delivery routes with accurate ETAs.

Prompt:

 "You are a Cross-Border Logistics Specialist. Develop a delivery plan from Singapore to Malaysia:

  - Include average customs clearance times at Tuas/Woodlands checkpoints in ETA calculations.

  - Select routes with best road conditions for heavy loads.

  - Pre-schedule documentation to reduce border delays.

  - Provide real-time updates to customers on delays.

  - Track performance against promised ETAs."

Inputs Required:

  - Origin and destination cities

  - Customs processing times

  - Road quality reports

  - Vehicle type and load

  - Delivery priority list
