### **Prompt 2 — Peak Hour Delivery Route Adjustment**

Backstory:

 You manage the dispatch team for an online grocery delivery service in Singapore. Every evening between 6 PM–9 PM, orders spike as working professionals return home to HDB estates and condo developments. Your current delivery pattern is fixed throughout the day, causing delays during peak traffic hours along the CTE, AYE, and PIE. Drivers often miss their ETA targets, and customers complain about late grocery drop-offs. You want a dynamic route adjustment system that automatically reshuffles deliveries when traffic increases or when a driver is ahead/behind schedule.

Goal:

 Design a peak-hour delivery plan that adjusts in real time to ensure all deliveries meet promised timelines.

Prompt:

 "You are a Last-Mile Delivery Optimisation Expert. Create a peak-hour routing strategy for 20 grocery delivery riders across Singapore.

  - Integrate real-time traffic data and GPS tracking.

  - Adjust delivery priority based on customer time sensitivity (e.g., perishable items first).

  - Ensure no driver travels more than 5 km extra due to re-routing.

  - Suggest an algorithm for automatic reassignment of orders.

  - Include a notification plan to inform customers about updated delivery times."

Inputs Required:

  - List of delivery addresses with time windows

  - Real-time traffic API availability

  - Number of delivery vehicles

  - Product categories (perishable/non-perishable)

  - Driver GPS tracking capability
