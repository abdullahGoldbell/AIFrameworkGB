### **Prompt 22 — Postal Service Route Digitisation**

Backstory:

 SingPost wants to modernise its daily route assignments for postmen across Singapore. Current paper-based route planning does not adapt to changing parcel volumes, leading to overburdened postmen in some zones (especially as e-commerce parcel volumes grow). You need to create a digital route assignment tool that automatically adjusts workloads in real time.

Goal:

 Digitise and optimise postal delivery route allocation.

Prompt:

 "You are a Postal Route Optimisation Expert. Design a digital route planning tool for SingPost’s delivery operations:

  - Map zones and delivery points digitally (HDB blocks, condos, landed estates).

  - Auto-adjust assignments based on daily volume.

  - Provide real-time rerouting for absent staff coverage.

  - Allow mobile app access for postmen to track routes.

  - Maintain historical route efficiency data."

Inputs Required:

  - Delivery addresses and volume per zone

  - Staff availability

  - Historical delivery time per route

  - GPS mapping data

  - Device availability for staff
