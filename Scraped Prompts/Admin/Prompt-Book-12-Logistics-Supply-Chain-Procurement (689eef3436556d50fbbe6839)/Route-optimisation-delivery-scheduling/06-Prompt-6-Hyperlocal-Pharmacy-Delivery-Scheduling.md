### **Prompt 6 — Hyperlocal Pharmacy Delivery Scheduling**

Backstory:

 You run a chain of 8 pharmacies across Singapore (in neighbourhoods such as Toa Payoh, Bedok, Clementi, and Ang Mo Kio) offering 2-hour delivery for medicines within a 6 km radius. Many orders are urgent (e.g., diabetic medications), while others are routine refills. Currently, your riders follow a first-come, first-served approach, which leads to urgent orders sometimes being delayed. You want a route and schedule plan that prioritises urgent deliveries while still batching nearby orders for efficiency.

Goal:

 Create a hyperlocal delivery schedule for pharmacies that prioritises urgency while minimising travel time.

Prompt:

 "You are a Hyperlocal Delivery Planner. Design a 2-hour delivery routing system for 8 pharmacies across Singapore:

  - Prioritise urgent prescriptions over routine deliveries.

  - Batch orders from the same locality (e.g., HDB cluster, condo area) to save travel time.

  - Allocate riders based on proximity to pickup point.

  - Provide alternate riders in case of delays or unavailability.

  - Include a real-time tracking dashboard for pharmacy managers."

Inputs Required:

  - Pharmacy locations & service radius

  - Urgent vs. routine order classification

  - Rider availability by time slot

  - Delivery address list with GPS coordinates

  - Average delivery times per zone
