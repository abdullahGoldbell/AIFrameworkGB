### **Prompt 17 — AI-Powered Food Delivery Routing**

Backstory:

 You run a food delivery service in Singapore that partners with 200+ hawker stalls and restaurants across the island. Delays often occur because orders are assigned based on distance alone, without considering prep time or kitchen delays. This results in riders arriving too early or too late, lowering customer satisfaction. You want a smart dispatch system that factors in restaurant preparation time along with delivery route optimisation.

Goal:

 Create a delivery assignment and routing system that aligns pickup timing with food readiness.

Prompt:

 "You are a FoodTech Logistics Expert. Design an AI-powered delivery routing model for Singapore:

  - Match riders to orders based on both location and estimated preparation time.

  - Sequence multiple deliveries for the same rider in optimal order.

  - Integrate restaurant POS systems for real-time readiness updates.

  - Reduce rider wait times at pickup points.

  - Monitor delivery ETA accuracy and improve continuously."

Inputs Required:

  - Restaurant locations and prep time averages

  - Rider GPS locations and capacity

  - Order timestamps and promised delivery windows

  - Traffic patterns by zone

  - Restaurant integration capability
