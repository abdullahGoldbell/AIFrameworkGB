### **Prompt 22 — Export Insurance Claim Documentation**

Backstory:

 A Singapore textile exporter suffered water damage to goods in transit but couldn't claim insurance due to missing documentation.

Goal:

 Create an insurance claim documentation guide.

Prompt:

 "You are an Export Risk Mitigation Consultant. Outline all documents needed for a successful marine cargo insurance claim with Singapore-based insurers (e.g. NTUC Income, AIG Singapore, MSIG).

  - Provide a checklist for different claim types.

  - Mention timelines for claim submission.

  - Suggest methods for evidence collection."

Inputs Required:

  - Insurance policy details

  - Nature of loss/damage

  - Transport documents

  - Incident reports
