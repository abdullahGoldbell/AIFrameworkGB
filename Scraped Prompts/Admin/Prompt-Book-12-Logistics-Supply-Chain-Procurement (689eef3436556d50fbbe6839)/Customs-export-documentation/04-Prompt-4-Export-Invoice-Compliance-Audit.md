### **Prompt 4 — Export Invoice Compliance Audit**

Backstory:

 A medium-sized lifestyle goods exporter in Singapore was recently penalized for discrepancies between their commercial invoice and packing list. They want a compliance-friendly export invoice template to avoid future issues.

Goal:

 Create a customs-compliant export invoice template.

Prompt:

 "You are a Customs Compliance Officer. Audit an exporter’s existing invoice and prepare a compliant export invoice template for shipments from Singapore to the US.

  - Include all required data fields for customs.

  - Highlight common mistakes and how to avoid them.

  - Suggest formatting that speeds up clearance."

Inputs Required:

  - Current invoice format

  - Product categories and quantities

  - Destination country

  - Buyer details
