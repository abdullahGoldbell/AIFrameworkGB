### **Prompt 3 — Certificate of Origin Preparation**

Backstory:

 A Singapore-based food and beverage exporter shipping to the Middle East has been informed by buyers that they need a Certificate of Origin to qualify for preferential duty rates under existing FTAs. The exporter is unsure of the correct format and issuing authority.

Goal:

 Guide the preparation and application process for a Certificate of Origin.

Prompt:

 "You are a Trade Documentation Advisor. Create step-by-step instructions for obtaining a Certificate of Origin for Singapore F&B exports to the UAE.

  - Specify required documents for the application.

  - Mention recognized issuing bodies in Singapore (e.g. Singapore Customs, SBF, SICC, SCCCI).

  - Provide tips to ensure acceptance by UAE customs."

Inputs Required:

  - Exporter details and registration numbers (UEN)

  - Product details and HS code

  - Destination country

  - Type of trade agreement (if any)
