### **Prompt 13 — Letter of Credit Documentation Checklist**

Backstory:

 A Singapore engineering goods exporter receives payment via letter of credit but often struggles with matching documents to the LC terms, risking non-payment.

Goal:

 Create a documentation checklist for LC compliance.

Prompt:

 "You are a Trade Finance Documentation Expert. Prepare a checklist for aligning export documents with a buyer's LC terms.

  - List common LC document requirements.

  - Provide tips to avoid discrepancies.

  - Suggest a document review timeline before submission."

Inputs Required:

  - Copy of LC terms

  - Product and shipment details

  - Bank details

  - Buyer's documentation preferences
