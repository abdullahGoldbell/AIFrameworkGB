### **Prompt 7 — Digital Customs Filing Process**

Backstory:

 A Singapore exporter is transitioning to electronic filing for customs clearance but finds the online portal confusing and error-prone. They want a detailed guide for smooth digital submissions.

Goal:

 Explain the digital customs filing process for exports.

Prompt:

 "You are a Digital Trade Process Expert. Create a detailed, screenshot-supported guide for filing export declarations online via TradeNet (Singapore Customs).

  - Include step-by-step navigation instructions.

  - Highlight required document uploads.

  - Explain common error messages and fixes."

Inputs Required:

  - Exporter registration ID (UEN / CR account)

  - HS codes and product details

  - Chosen port of export (e.g. PSA, Jurong Port, Changi Airfreight)

  - Mode of transport
