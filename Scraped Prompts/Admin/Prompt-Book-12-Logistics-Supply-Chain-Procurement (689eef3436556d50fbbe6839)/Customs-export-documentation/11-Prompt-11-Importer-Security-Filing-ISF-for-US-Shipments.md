### **Prompt 11 — Importer Security Filing (ISF) for US Shipments**

Backstory:

 A Singapore stone and tile exporter sending goods to the US is confused about the "10+2" ISF requirements. Missing deadlines could result in hefty fines, so they need clear guidance on what to file, when, and how.

Goal:

 Prepare a step-by-step guide for ISF compliance.

Prompt:

 "You are a US Customs Compliance Advisor. Provide a complete guide for preparing and submitting the Importer Security Filing for goods exported from Singapore to the US.

  - Include data elements required.

  - Specify deadlines and penalties for delays.

  - Provide a sample ISF form with explanations."

Inputs Required:

  - Exporter details

  - Product details and HS codes

  - US consignee details

  - Shipment schedule
