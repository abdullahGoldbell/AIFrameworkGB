### **Prompt 23 — Export Compliance SOP for SMEs**

Backstory:

 A small Singapore handicraft and lifestyle goods exporter wants to formalize their export compliance process to train new staff.

Goal:

 Create an export compliance SOP.

Prompt:

 "You are a Trade Compliance Process Designer. Draft a standard operating procedure for export compliance for a Singapore SME.

  - Include step-by-step documentation flow aligned with Singapore Customs and TradeNet.

  - Assign roles and responsibilities.

  - Suggest audit checkpoints."

Inputs Required:

  - Company size and team structure

  - Export product range

  - Key export markets

  - Current compliance challenges
