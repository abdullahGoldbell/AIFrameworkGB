### **Prompt 20 — Country-Specific Export Restrictions Guide**

Backstory:

 A Singapore technology exporter needs to understand export control laws in different countries to avoid shipping restricted products.

Goal:

 List export restrictions for target countries.

Prompt:

 "You are a Global Trade Compliance Analyst. Prepare a guide outlining export restrictions for Singapore technology products in key markets, including obligations under the Singapore Strategic Goods (Control) Act.

  - Include prohibited and restricted items.

  - Mention required permits (e.g. TradeFIRST, Strategic Trade Scheme).

  - Provide official resources for reference (Singapore Customs, destination country regulators)."

Inputs Required:

  - Product category and HS codes

  - Target export markets

  - Export frequency

  - Previous compliance records
