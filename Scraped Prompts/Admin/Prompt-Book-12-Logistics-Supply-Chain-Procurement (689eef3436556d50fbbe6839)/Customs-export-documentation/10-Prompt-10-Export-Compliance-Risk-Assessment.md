### **Prompt 10 — Export Compliance Risk Assessment**

Backstory:

 A Singapore textile exporter has expanded into new markets without fully understanding each country's compliance requirements, risking shipment holds.

Goal:

 Assess and mitigate export compliance risks.

Prompt:

 "You are an International Trade Risk Consultant. Prepare a compliance risk assessment for a Singapore textile exporter.

  - Identify potential customs or documentation risks for each market.

  - Rank risks by likelihood and severity.

  - Suggest preventive measures for top risks."

Inputs Required:

  - Export market list

  - Product descriptions and HS codes

  - Past compliance issues

  - Export volumes per market
