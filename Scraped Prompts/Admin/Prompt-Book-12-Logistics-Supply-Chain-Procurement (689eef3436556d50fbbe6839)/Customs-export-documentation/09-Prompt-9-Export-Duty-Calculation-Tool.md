### **Prompt 9 — Export Duty Calculation Tool**

Backstory:

 A Singapore-based auto parts exporter often underestimates customs duties in quotes, leading to reduced profit margins. They need a reliable method to calculate export duties for various countries.

Goal:

 Provide a duty calculation framework for exporters.

Prompt:

 "You are a Trade Tariff Analyst. Create a simple tool or framework for calculating customs duties for Singapore auto parts exports.

  - Include base duty, surcharges, and preferential rates under Singapore FTAs (e.g. CSFTA, RCEP, CPTPP).

  - Explain how to look up rates in the destination country's tariff database and Singapore Customs TradeNet references.

  - Provide examples for key export markets."

Inputs Required:

  - HS codes for all products

  - Destination country

  - Mode of shipment

  - Trade agreements applicable
