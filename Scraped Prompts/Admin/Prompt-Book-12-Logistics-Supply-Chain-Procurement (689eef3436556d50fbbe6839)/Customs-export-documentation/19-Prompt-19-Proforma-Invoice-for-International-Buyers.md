### **Prompt 19 — Proforma Invoice for International Buyers**

Backstory:

 A Singapore furniture exporter is expanding into Europe and wants to issue professional, customs-friendly proforma invoices for new buyers.

Goal:

 Create a proforma invoice template aligned with customs needs.

Prompt:

 "You are an Export Sales Documentation Expert. Design a proforma invoice template for Singapore exporters selling to EU buyers.

  - Include required trade terms.

  - Suggest clear item descriptions.

  - Provide a sample filled-in version with SGD-denominated values and Incoterms 2020 references."

Inputs Required:

  - Product details

  - Buyer details

  - Chosen Incoterms

  - Payment terms
