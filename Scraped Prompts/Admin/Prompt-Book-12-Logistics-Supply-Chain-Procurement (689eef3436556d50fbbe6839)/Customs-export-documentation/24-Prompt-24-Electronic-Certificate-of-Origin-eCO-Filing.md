### **Prompt 24 — Electronic Certificate of Origin (eCO) Filing**

Backstory:

 A Singapore auto parts exporter wants to switch from paper to electronic certificate of origin filing through TradeNet to save time.

Goal:

 Provide an eCO filing guide.

Prompt:

 "You are an International Trade Digitalisation Specialist. Prepare a guide for filing an Electronic Certificate of Origin from Singapore.

  - List online platforms and authorities (Singapore Customs via TradeNet, Singapore Chamber of Commerce, SICC).

  - Explain step-by-step process.

  - Mention potential rejection reasons and fixes."

Inputs Required:

  - Exporter UEN

  - HS codes

  - Destination country

  - Trade agreement applicability
