### **Prompt 16 — Export Permit Renewal Process**

Backstory:

 A Singapore agro-commodity trader holds a 1-year export permit issued via TradeNet but risks expiry in the middle of peak season. They want to renew well in advance.

Goal:

 Create a step-by-step permit renewal guide.

Prompt:

 "You are a Government Licensing Liaison. Prepare a guide for renewing Singapore Customs export permits via TradeNet without operational downtime.

  - Mention lead times for renewal.

  - Include documents required.

  - Suggest follow-up methods to avoid delays with Singapore Customs and any controlling agencies (e.g. SFA, NEA, HSA)."

Inputs Required:

  - Current permit copy

  - Product categories

  - Export history for past year

  - Destination markets
