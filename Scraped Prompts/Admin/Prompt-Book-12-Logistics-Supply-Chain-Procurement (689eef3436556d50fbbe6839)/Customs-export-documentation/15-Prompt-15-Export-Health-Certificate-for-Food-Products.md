### **Prompt 15 — Export Health Certificate for Food Products**

Backstory:

 A Singapore-based dairy and processed food exporter shipping to Malaysia needs a health certificate issued by Singapore authorities to meet the importing country's food safety standards.

Goal:

 Provide steps for obtaining an export health certificate.

Prompt:

 "You are a Food Export Compliance Advisor. Outline the process to obtain an export health certificate from the Singapore Food Agency (SFA) for dairy products being shipped from Singapore to Malaysia.

  - Include necessary lab testing.

  - List recognized issuing authorities (SFA and accredited labs).

  - Mention validity period and renewal steps."

Inputs Required:

  - Product details

  - Destination country regulations

  - Production facility address

  - Lab testing results
