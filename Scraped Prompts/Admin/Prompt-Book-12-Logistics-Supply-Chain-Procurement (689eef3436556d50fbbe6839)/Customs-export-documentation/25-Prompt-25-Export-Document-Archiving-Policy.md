### **Prompt 25 — Export Document Archiving Policy**

Backstory:

 A large Singapore exporter is facing an internal audit and needs a proper document storage policy to comply with legal retention requirements.

Goal:

 Create an export document archiving policy.

Prompt:

 "You are a Trade Records Management Expert. Develop an archiving policy for export documents in line with Singapore Customs requirements (typically 5 years), IRAS GST record-keeping rules, PDPA, and international regulations.

  - Specify retention periods.

  - Suggest digital archiving tools.

  - Provide security and access guidelines."

Inputs Required:

  - Company size and export volume

  - Document types handled

  - Storage method (digital/physical)

  - Legal requirements in target markets
