### **Prompt 18 — Export Declaration Form Guidance**

Backstory:

 A Singapore jewellery exporter regularly files export declarations via TradeNet but struggles to keep up with changing requirements from Singapore Customs.

Goal:

 Provide updated export declaration guidelines.

Prompt:

 "You are a Customs Declaration Expert. Provide a 2025-updated guide to filing export declarations through TradeNet in Singapore.

  - Include field-by-field explanations for the OUT permit application.

  - Mention required supporting documents.

  - Highlight recent changes in Singapore Customs procedures."

Inputs Required:

  - Exporter UEN and TradeNet account details

  - HS codes

  - Shipment value and currency

  - Mode of transport
