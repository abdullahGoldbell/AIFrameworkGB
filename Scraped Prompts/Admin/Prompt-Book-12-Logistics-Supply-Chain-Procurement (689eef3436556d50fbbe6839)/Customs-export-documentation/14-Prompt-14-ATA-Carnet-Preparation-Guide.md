### **Prompt 14 — ATA Carnet Preparation Guide**

Backstory:

 A Singapore events company is temporarily exporting exhibition goods to Dubai and wants to use an ATA Carnet to simplify customs clearance.

Goal:

 Guide preparation and use of an ATA Carnet.

Prompt:

 "You are a Temporary Export Documentation Consultant. Create a complete ATA Carnet preparation and usage guide for Singapore exporters.

  - Include application steps and authorities (e.g. Singapore International Chamber of Commerce as the issuing body).

  - List goods eligible under ATA Carnet.

  - Provide best practices for re-importation."

Inputs Required:

  - List of goods for temporary export

  - Destination country

  - Event dates

  - Applicant's company details
