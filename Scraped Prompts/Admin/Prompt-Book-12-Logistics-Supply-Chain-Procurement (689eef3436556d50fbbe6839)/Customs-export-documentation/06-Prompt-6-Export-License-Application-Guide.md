### **Prompt 6 — Export License Application Guide**

Backstory:

 A Singapore-based pharmaceutical exporter received a large overseas order but is unsure about the required export licenses and permits for regulated products. Delays in licensing could cost them the deal.

Goal:

 Guide the exporter through the license application process.

Prompt:

 "You are an Export Licensing Consultant. Outline the complete process for obtaining export licenses for pharmaceutical products from Singapore to the UK.

  - Include timelines, costs, and authorities involved (HSA, Singapore Customs, TradeNet).

  - Highlight mandatory compliance checks.

  - Suggest ways to expedite the process."

Inputs Required:

  - Product description and HS code

  - Destination country

  - Company registration (UEN) and licensing history

  - Intended export volume and frequency
