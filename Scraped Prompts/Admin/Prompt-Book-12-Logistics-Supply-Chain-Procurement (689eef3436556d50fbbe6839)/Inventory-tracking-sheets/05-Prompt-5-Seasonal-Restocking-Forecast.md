### **Prompt 5 — Seasonal Restocking Forecast**

Backstory:

 Your sports equipment store in Singapore sees significant seasonal demand changes—badminton and running gear spike during the Standard Chartered Singapore Marathon period, football gear during major tournament windows, and gym equipment in January as part of New Year resolutions. Restocking decisions are currently based on guesswork, leading to excess stock in some categories and shortages in others. You need a forecast-driven restocking plan that aligns with seasonal demand.

Goal:

 Design a seasonal restocking forecast to match demand patterns.

Prompt:

 "You are a Demand Forecasting Specialist. Build a seasonal restocking plan for a sports equipment store.

  - Analyse past 3 years’ monthly sales data per category.

  - Identify seasonal peaks for each product category.

  - Adjust reorder points based on upcoming demand.

  - Include promotional campaigns in demand projections.

  - Provide a quarterly restocking schedule."

Inputs Required:

  - Historical sales data by product category

  - Current stock levels

  - Seasonal demand trends

  - Marketing campaign calendar

  - Supplier lead times
