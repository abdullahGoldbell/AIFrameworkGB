### **Prompt 13 — Inventory Buffer Planning for Festivals**

Backstory:

 Your cosmetic products business sees a huge spike during Chinese New Year, Hari Raya, Deepavali, and Valentine’s Day. Last year, late restocking led to missed opportunities. You need an inventory buffer system that pre-orders additional stock ahead of festivals while avoiding overstocking.

Goal:

 Plan inventory buffers for peak festival sales.

Prompt:

 "You are a Seasonal Demand Planner. Develop a festival inventory buffer plan for a cosmetics brand.

  - Analyse past 3 years’ festival sales trends.

  - Set buffer stock targets for each SKU.

  - Factor in supplier delays during peak season.

  - Create an early procurement schedule.

  - Provide post-festival stock liquidation strategies."

Inputs Required:

  - Festival sales history by SKU

  - Current inventory levels

  - Supplier lead times

  - Buffer stock percentage rules

  - Festival dates calendar
