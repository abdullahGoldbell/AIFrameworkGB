### **Prompt 2 — Inventory Tracking Dashboard for E-commerce**

Backstory:

 You operate a small but growing e-commerce business that sells fashion accessories across Singapore and into Malaysia. You stock products in two warehouses—one in Tuas and one in Johor Bahru just across the Causeway—but inventory updates are often delayed, causing inaccurate stock counts on your website and on Shopee/Lazada listings. This leads to overselling items you no longer have in stock and losing customer trust. You need a live inventory tracking dashboard that updates in real-time and supports multi-location stock management.

Goal:

 Build a live, multi-location inventory tracking dashboard that syncs with the website.

Prompt:

 "You are an Inventory Management Specialist. Create a real-time inventory tracking dashboard for an e-commerce business with two warehouses.

  - Integrate live stock data feeds from both warehouses.

  - Display available, reserved, and incoming stock for each product.

  - Flag low-stock items in red and out-of-stock items in grey.

  - Sync updates instantly to the e-commerce platform.

  - Include a weekly inventory turnover report."

Inputs Required:

  - List of products and SKUs

  - Stock levels per warehouse

  - Reserved stock data

  - Integration details for e-commerce platform

  - Preferred dashboard tool (Excel, Google Data Studio, Power BI, etc.)
