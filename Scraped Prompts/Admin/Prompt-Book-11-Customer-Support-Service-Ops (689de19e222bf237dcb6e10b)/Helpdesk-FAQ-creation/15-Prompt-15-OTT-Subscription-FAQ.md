## **Prompt 15 — OTT Subscription FAQ**

Backstory:

 A 26-year-old customer support lead at a streaming service based in Singapore is facing ticket overload from users about subscription plans, renewal, and download limits.

Goal:

 Create an OTT subscription FAQ.

Prompt:

 "You are an OTT helpdesk content creator. Create an FAQ covering:

  - Subscription plans and pricing

  - Renewal and cancellation

  - Download limits and offline viewing

  - Troubleshooting playback issues"

Inputs Required:

  - Plan details

  - Renewal policies
