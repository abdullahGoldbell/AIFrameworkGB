## **Prompt 18 — College Admission FAQ**

Backstory:

 A 33-year-old admissions officer at a Singapore autonomous university faces endless calls from parents and students about eligibility, application timelines, and document submission.

Goal:

 Create an admission FAQ.

Prompt:

 "You are a higher education communications officer. Create an FAQ covering:

  - Eligibility criteria

  - Application process

  - Document submission

  - Fee payment methods"

Inputs Required:

  - Admission policy

  - Calendar of deadlines

  - Fee structure
