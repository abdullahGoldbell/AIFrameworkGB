## **Prompt 23 — SaaS Software Pricing FAQ**

Backstory:

 A 29-year-old sales engineer at a SaaS startup based in Paya Lebar Quarter is facing repetitive questions about subscription tiers, free trials, and add-on costs.

Goal:

 Create a SaaS pricing FAQ.

Prompt:

 "You are a SaaS product support writer. Develop an FAQ covering:

  - Pricing tiers and inclusions

  - Free trial terms

  - Add-on features and costs

  - Upgrade/downgrade process"

Inputs Required:

  - Pricing sheet

  - Feature list

  - Trial policy
