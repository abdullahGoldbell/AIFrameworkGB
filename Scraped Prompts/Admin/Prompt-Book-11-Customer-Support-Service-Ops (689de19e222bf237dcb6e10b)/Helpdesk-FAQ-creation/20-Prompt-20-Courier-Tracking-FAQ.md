## **Prompt 20 — Courier Tracking FAQ**

Backstory:

 A 25-year-old customer care agent at a logistics company operating out of Tuas with last-mile delivery across Singapore is constantly asked about shipment delays and tracking updates.

Goal:

 Create a shipment tracking FAQ.

Prompt:

 "You are a logistics support content writer. Create an FAQ covering:

  - How to track a shipment

  - Common reasons for delay

  - Delivery attempt policies

  - Contact escalation process"

Inputs Required:

  - Tracking process flow

  - Delay reason codes

  - Escalation SOP
