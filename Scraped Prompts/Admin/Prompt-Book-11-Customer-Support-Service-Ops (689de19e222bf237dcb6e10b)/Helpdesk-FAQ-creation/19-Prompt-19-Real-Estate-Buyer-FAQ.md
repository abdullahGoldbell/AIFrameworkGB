## **Prompt 19 — Real Estate Buyer FAQ**

Backstory:

 A 32-year-old sales manager at a Singapore property developer launching a new condo project in District 19 is flooded with calls about payment plans, possession timelines, and legal clearances.

Goal:

 Create a property purchase FAQ.

Prompt:

 "You are a real estate support writer. Develop an FAQ covering:

  - Payment schedule

  - Possession dates

  - Legal documents provided

  - Loan assistance details"

Inputs Required:

  - Payment plan

  - Project timeline

  - Legal clearance list
