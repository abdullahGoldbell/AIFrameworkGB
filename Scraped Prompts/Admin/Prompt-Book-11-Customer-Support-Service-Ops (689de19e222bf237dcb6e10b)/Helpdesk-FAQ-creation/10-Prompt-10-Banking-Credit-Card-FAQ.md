## **Prompt 10 — Banking Credit Card FAQ**

Backstory:

 A 28-year-old banking call centre manager at a Singapore retail bank in the CBD is getting high call volumes for credit card activation, bill payment, and reward redemption queries.

Goal:

 Prepare a credit card usage FAQ.

Prompt:

 "You are a banking customer education writer. Create an FAQ covering:

  - How to activate the card

  - Payment methods and due dates (including PayNow and GIRO)

  - Reward points earning and redemption

  - Fraud prevention tips"

Inputs Required:

  - Card product features

  - Payment gateway links

  - Rewards catalog
