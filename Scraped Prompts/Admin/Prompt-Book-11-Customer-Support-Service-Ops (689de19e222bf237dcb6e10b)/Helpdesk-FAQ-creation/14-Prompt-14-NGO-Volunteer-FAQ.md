## **Prompt 14 — NGO Volunteer FAQ**

Backstory:

 A 27-year-old volunteer coordinator at a Singapore-based charity registered with the Commissioner of Charities is tired of repeating onboarding steps to new volunteers.

Goal:

 Create a volunteer onboarding FAQ.

Prompt:

 "You are a nonprofit support writer. Prepare an FAQ covering:

  - Volunteer eligibility and roles

  - Registration process

  - Activity schedules

  - Reimbursement policy (if any)"

Inputs Required:

  - Volunteer policy

  - Activity calendar

Registration forms
