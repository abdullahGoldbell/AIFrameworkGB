## **Prompt 13 — Gym Membership FAQ**

Backstory:

 A 25-year-old front desk manager at a fitness chain with outlets in Tampines and Orchard is constantly asked about membership freeze policies, class bookings, and locker usage.

Goal:

 Create a gym membership FAQ.

Prompt:

 "You are a fitness center operations writer. Create an FAQ covering:

  - Membership plans and renewals

  - Freeze/suspension policies

  - Class booking rules

  - Locker use and safety"

Inputs Required:

  - Membership T&Cs

  - Class schedule

  - Locker policy
