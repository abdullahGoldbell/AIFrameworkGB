## **Prompt 4 —  Airline Flight Delay Compensation Response**

Backstory:

 A 31-year-old guest relations officer at a low-cost carrier based at Changi Airport receives angry emails daily from passengers affected by delays. The airline needs a standardized yet personalized template to handle these complaints with empathy, transparency, and clear details on compensation or rebooking.

Goal:

 Build a customer-centric flight delay complaint resolution template.

Prompt:

 "You are an airline customer communication specialist. Create a flight delay complaint resolution template.

 Include:

  - Empathetic apology

  - Reason for delay (without technical jargon)

  - Compensation/refund/rebooking options

  - Next steps for passengers"

Inputs Required:

  - Airline compensation policy

  - Common causes of delays

  - Customer rebooking process
