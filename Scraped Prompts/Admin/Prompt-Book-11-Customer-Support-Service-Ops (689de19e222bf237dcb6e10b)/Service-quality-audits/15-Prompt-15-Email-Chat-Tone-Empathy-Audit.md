## **Prompt 15 — Email/Chat Tone & Empathy Audit**

Backstory:

 Though factual accuracy is decent, a Singapore-based SaaS support team headquartered in one-north gets feedback that replies feel “robotic.” Leaders want empathy without verbosity and consistent brand voice across agents.

Goal:

 Measure tone, empathy, and brand voice adherence; recommend improvements.

Prompt:

 “You are a communications QA specialist. Audit written interactions for:

  - Personalization (name, context acknowledgement).

  - Empathy markers and assurance language.

  - Clarity (short sentences, scannable structure).

  - Brand voice (formal vs. friendly) and inclusive language.

 Deliver a tone rubric, exemplars, and a micro-writing playbook.”

Inputs Required:

  - Sample emails/chats

  - Brand voice guide

  - Prohibited phrases list

  - SLA for reply length/format
