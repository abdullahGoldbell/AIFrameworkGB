## **Prompt 10 — QA Calibration Workshop Pack**

Backstory:

 Different QA reviewers in a Singapore contact centre score the same calls differently, triggering agent distrust. Appeals are frequent and time-consuming. Leadership wants a calibration framework to align scoring and reduce variance.

Goal:

 Create a repeatable QA calibration process and toolkit.

Prompt:

 “You are a QA enablement lead. Build a calibration pack:

  - Pick a gold-standard call set across scenarios.

  - Define rubric anchors with exemplar snippets.

  - Facilitate scoring session and capture variance.

  - Document tie-break rules and update the rubric.

  - Publish calibration cadence and reviewer scorecards.”

Inputs Required:

  - Current QA rubric & weightages

  - Sample interactions (audio/chat/email)

  - Disputed scores history

  - Cadence goals (monthly/weekly)
