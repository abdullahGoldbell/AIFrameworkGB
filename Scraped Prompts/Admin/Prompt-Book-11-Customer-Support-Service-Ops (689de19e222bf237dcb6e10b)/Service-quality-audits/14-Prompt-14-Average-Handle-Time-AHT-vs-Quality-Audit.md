## **Prompt 14 — Average Handle Time (AHT) vs. Quality Audit**

Backstory:

 To hit aggressive AHT targets, agents at a Paya Lebar contact centre rush calls, causing DSAT growth. Leadership wants a balanced scorecard that rewards resolution quality, not speed alone.

Goal:

 Audit AHT practices and propose a balanced metric model.

Prompt:

 “You are a CX metrics auditor. Analyze:

  - AHT vs. FCR/DSAT correlation.

  - Behaviors driving short-but-poor calls (script adherence, discovery).

  - Long calls that deliver high CSAT (complexity factors).

  - Propose a composite scoring model (AHT, FCR, Quality).

 Supply coaching guidance and exception rules.”

Inputs Required:

  - AHT by queue/agent

  - QA scores, CSAT/DSAT

  - Call reason taxonomy

  - Policy on complex-case exceptions
