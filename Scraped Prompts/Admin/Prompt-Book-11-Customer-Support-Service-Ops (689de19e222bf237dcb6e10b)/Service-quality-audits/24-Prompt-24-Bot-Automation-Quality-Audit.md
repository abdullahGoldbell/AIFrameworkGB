## **Prompt 24 — Bot & Automation Quality Audit**

Backstory:

 A Singapore EdTech’s chatbot handles 40% of queries but NPS dips when the bot loops or dead-ends. Handoffs to humans lack context, frustrating customers. Leadership wants a bot QA audit.

Goal:

 Evaluate conversation coverage, accuracy, and handoff quality.

Prompt:

 “You are a conversational AI auditor.

  - Analyze top intents, containment, and fallback rates.

  - Check response accuracy, tone, and KB alignment.

  - Evaluate human handoff (context, transcript, priority).

  - Recommend training data gaps, flows to retire, and handoff SOP.”

Inputs Required:

  - Bot transcripts & intent analytics

  - KB links used by bot

  - Handoff rules & agent tools

  - Success metrics (containment, CSAT)
