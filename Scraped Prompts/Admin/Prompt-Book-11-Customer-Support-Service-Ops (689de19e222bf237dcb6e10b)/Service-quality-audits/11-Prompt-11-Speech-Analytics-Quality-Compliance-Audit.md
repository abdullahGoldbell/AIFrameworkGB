## **Prompt 11 — Speech Analytics Quality & Compliance Audit**

Backstory:

 A Singapore contact centre installed speech analytics but insights haven’t improved QA outcomes. Agents don’t trust keyword flags; compliance misses are still discovered manually. Management needs an audit to tighten model accuracy and governance.

Goal:

 Evaluate speech analytics performance vs. QA/compliance objectives.

Prompt:

 “You are a speech analytics auditor. Review:

  - Keyword/phrase precision & recall (vs. human QA).

  - False positives/negatives on critical intents (cancellation, threat, legal).

  - Consent/PII detection reliability (PDPA-aligned).

  - Tuning plan (dictionaries, acoustic models).

  - Governance: model change control, audits, drift checks.”

Inputs Required:

  - Model config (keywords, thresholds)

  - Ground truth QA samples

  - Compliance rules (PDPA consent, PII)

  - Current alert workflows
