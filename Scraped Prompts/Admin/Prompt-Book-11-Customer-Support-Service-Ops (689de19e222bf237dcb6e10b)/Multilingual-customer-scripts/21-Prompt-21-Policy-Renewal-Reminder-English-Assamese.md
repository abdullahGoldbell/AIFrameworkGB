## **Prompt 21 — Policy Renewal Reminder – English & Malay**

Backstory:

 A Singapore-based insurance company serves a sizeable Malay-speaking customer base across HDB heartlands. Without a bilingual renewal notice, customers risk policy lapse.

Goal:

 Create a renewal reminder that is polite, urgent, and clear.

Prompt:

 "You are an insurance retention specialist. Write a bilingual (English & Malay) policy renewal reminder that includes:

  - Policyholder name in greeting

  - Policy type and expiry date

  - Renewal premium amount

  - Easy payment steps (PayNow / GIRO / online portal)

  - Customer helpline"

Inputs Required:

  - Customer name

  - Policy type

  - Expiry date

  - Premium amount

  - Payment instructions
