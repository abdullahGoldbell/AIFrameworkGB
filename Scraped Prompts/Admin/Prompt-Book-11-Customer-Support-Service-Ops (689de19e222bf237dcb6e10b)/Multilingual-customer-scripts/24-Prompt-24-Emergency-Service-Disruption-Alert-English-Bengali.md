## **Prompt 24 — Emergency Service Disruption Alert – English & Mandarin**

Backstory:

 A Singapore utility provider needs to inform residents about urgent water service cuts due to repairs. Mandarin-speaking residents, especially seniors in HDB estates, must get the same clear instructions as English speakers.

Goal:

 Create an emergency disruption alert that is concise, clear, and reassuring.

Prompt:

 "You are a utility service communication officer. Write a bilingual (English & Mandarin) emergency water supply disruption alert that includes:

  - HDB block / estate / district affected

  - Start and expected restoration time

  - Reason for disruption (brief)

  - Alternative arrangements (if any)

  - Helpline number"

Inputs Required:

  - Affected area / block(s)

  - Start time

  - Expected restoration time

  - Reason for disruption

  - Helpline
