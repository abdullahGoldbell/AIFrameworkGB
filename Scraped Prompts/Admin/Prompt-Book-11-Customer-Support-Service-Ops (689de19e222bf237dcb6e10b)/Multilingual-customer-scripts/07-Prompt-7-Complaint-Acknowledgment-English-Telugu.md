## **Prompt 7 — Complaint Acknowledgment – English & Telugu**

Backstory:

 A Singapore-based telecom service provider serving a growing South Asian customer base wants to ensure every complaint is acknowledged in both English and Telugu to build trust and prevent escalation. Currently, some agents skip important empathy phrases.

Goal:

 Create a bilingual complaint acknowledgment script that reassures the customer their issue is being addressed.

Prompt:

 "You are a customer complaint handling expert. Create a bilingual (English & Telugu) complaint acknowledgment script that includes:

  - Warm and empathetic opening

  - Confirmation of complaint details (summarized)

  - Assurance of action and expected resolution time

  - Contact method for updates"

Inputs Required:

  - Customer name

  - Complaint ID

  - Issue summary

  - Resolution timeline
