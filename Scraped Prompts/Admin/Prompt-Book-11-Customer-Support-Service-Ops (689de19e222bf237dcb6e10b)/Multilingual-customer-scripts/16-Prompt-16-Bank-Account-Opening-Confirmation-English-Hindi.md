## **Prompt 16 — Bank Account Opening Confirmation – English & Hindi**

Backstory:

 A Singapore retail bank with branches across Little India, Jurong, and the CBD receives high walk-in traffic from customers in the Hindi-speaking community, including expatriate professionals and migrant workers. Many customers are uncertain if their account is active after document submission. The bank wants a standard bilingual confirmation to assure customers and provide next steps.

Goal:

 Design a clear bilingual account opening confirmation for SMS and printed receipt.

Prompt:

 "You are a banking communication advisor. Create a bilingual (English & Hindi) account opening confirmation that covers:

  - Warm welcome message with customer name

  - Confirmation of account number and activation date

  - Information on how to collect debit card and chequebook

  - Customer care contact details"

Inputs Required:

  - Customer name

  - Account number

  - Activation date

  - Collection process for debit card & chequebook
