## **Prompt 19 — Credit Card Bill Reminder – English & Punjabi**

Backstory:

 A Singapore-based private bank serves many Punjabi-speaking SME owners and professionals who occasionally miss credit card due dates due to unclear reminders. A formal yet friendly bilingual message will improve on-time payments.

Goal:

 Create a credit card bill reminder script in English and Punjabi for SMS and calls.

Prompt:

 "You are a financial communication expert. Write a bilingual (English & Punjabi) credit card bill reminder that includes:

  - Customer name in greeting

  - Outstanding bill amount and due date

  - Payment methods (PayNow, GIRO, internet banking, branch)

  - Late fee policy (brief)

  - Customer care number"

Inputs Required:

  - Customer name

  - Bill amount (S$)

  - Due date

  - Payment methods

  - Late fee details
