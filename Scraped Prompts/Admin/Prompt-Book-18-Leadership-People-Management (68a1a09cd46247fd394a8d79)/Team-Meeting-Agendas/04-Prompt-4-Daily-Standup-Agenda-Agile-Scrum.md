### Prompt 4 – Daily Standup Agenda (Agile/Scrum)

Backstory:

 A software development team based in Singapore's Paya Lebar tech cluster follows Agile methodology. They hold daily 15-minute standups to report on progress, plan for the day, and surface blockers. The Scrum Master wants a standardised agenda to keep the standups sharp and on-track.

Goal:

 Design a daily standup meeting agenda.

Prompt:

 *"You are an Agile coach. Create a daily standup agenda that includes:

  - What did you complete yesterday?

  - What will you work on today?

  - Any blockers or dependencies?

 Keep responses concise and limit each member to 2 minutes."*

Inputs:

  - Team size

  - Sprint duration

Time zone differences
