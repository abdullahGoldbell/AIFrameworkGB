### Prompt 6 – All-Hands Company Meeting Agenda

Backstory:

 A fast-growing healthtech startup based in Singapore's Biopolis precinct wants to hold monthly all-hands meetings to keep employees informed and engaged. These sessions should cover company performance, departmental highlights, and employee recognition.

Goal:

 Design an all-hands meeting agenda.

Prompt:

 *"You are an internal communications strategist. Create a monthly all-hands agenda that includes:

  - CEO/founder update on company progress

  - Department highlights and success stories

  - Financial health snapshot

  - Employee recognition and awards

  - Q&A segment

 Ensure agenda keeps energy high and fosters transparency."*

Inputs:

  - Company size

  - Key metrics to share

  - Recognition categories
