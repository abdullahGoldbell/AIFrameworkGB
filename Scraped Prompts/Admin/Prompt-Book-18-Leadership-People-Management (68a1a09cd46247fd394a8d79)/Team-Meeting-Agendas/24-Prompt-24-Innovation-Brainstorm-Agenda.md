### Prompt 24 – Innovation Brainstorm Agenda

Backstory:

 An edtech startup headquartered in Singapore wants to hold quarterly innovation sessions to crowdsource ideas from employees for new products and process improvements. Previous attempts at brainstorming have resulted in unfocused discussions and few actionable outcomes.

Goal:

 Create an innovation brainstorming agenda that channels creativity into practical, testable ideas.

Prompt:

 *"You are an innovation workshop facilitator. Draft a 2-hour brainstorming meeting agenda including:

  - Inspiration Segment – Share industry trends, customer insights, and competitor moves.

  - Idea Generation Round 1 – Individual silent brainstorming.

  - Idea Sharing & Clustering – Group similar ideas and identify themes.

  - Idea Prioritization – Vote on top ideas based on impact and feasibility.

  - Action Planning – Assign small teams to develop top 3 ideas into prototypes.

  - Closing & Next Steps – Schedule follow-up to review progress.

 Use creative exercises like 'How Might We' framing to focus thinking."*

Inputs:

  - Recent market research

  - Company strategic priorities

  - Workshop facilitation tools

  - Budget for prototyping
