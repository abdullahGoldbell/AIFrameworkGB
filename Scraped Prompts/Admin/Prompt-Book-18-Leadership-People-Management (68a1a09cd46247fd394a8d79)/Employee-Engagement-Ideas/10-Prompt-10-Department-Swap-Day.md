### Prompt 10 — Department Swap Day

Backstory:

 Adrian, 29, works at a large logistics company in Jurong, Singapore. Employees often don’t understand what other departments do, leading to miscommunication. Adrian wants to introduce a “Department Swap Day” where employees experience a different role for a few hours.

Goal:

 Encourage cross-functional understanding through a fun, role-swapping activity.

Prompt:

 "You are a team culture facilitator. Create a ‘Department Swap Day’ plan for 50 employees across 5 departments. The plan should:

  - Match employees with complementary roles.

  - Include a guided observation period and a task simulation.

  - Allow a post-activity discussion to share learnings.

  - Provide a participation certificate for everyone.

 Ensure no critical operations are disrupted."

Inputs:

  - Number of employees

  - Departments involved

  - Duration of swap

  - Type of roles
