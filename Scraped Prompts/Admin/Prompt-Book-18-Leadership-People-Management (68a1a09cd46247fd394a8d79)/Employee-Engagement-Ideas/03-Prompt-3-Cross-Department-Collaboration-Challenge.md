### Prompt 3 — Cross-Department Collaboration Challenge

Backstory:

 Mei Ling, 27, recently joined as an engagement coordinator at a mid-sized FMCG company in Singapore. She noticed that employees mostly interact within their own departments, which limits idea exchange and innovation. She wants to design a fun “Collaboration Challenge” that encourages employees from different teams to work together.

Goal:

 Develop a one-week cross-department collaboration program that fosters idea-sharing, creativity, and healthy competition.

Prompt:

 "You are a corporate engagement specialist. Create a ‘Collaboration Challenge’ for 80 employees across 4 departments. The program should:

  - Assign mixed-department teams.

  - Give them fun, innovation-based tasks (e.g., design a mock product, pitch a marketing campaign).

  - Include a scoring system and panel of judges.

  - End with a showcase and prize ceremony.

 Ensure the challenge builds long-term interdepartmental relationships."

Inputs:

  - Number of participants

  - Departments involved

  - Duration (days)

  - Type of tasks (innovation, creative, social impact)
