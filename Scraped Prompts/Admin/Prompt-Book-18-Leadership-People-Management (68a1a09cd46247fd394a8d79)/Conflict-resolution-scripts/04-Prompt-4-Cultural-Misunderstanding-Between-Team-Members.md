### Prompt 4 – Cultural Misunderstanding Between Team Members

Backstory:

 In a multinational IT firm in Singapore's Changi Business Park, a 27-year-old local team lead and a 31-year-old expat colleague have had recurring misunderstandings during project discussions. The expat team member feels the lead’s communication style is too direct and abrupt, while the lead finds the colleague’s indirectness frustrating. The tension is starting to impact project delivery timelines.

Goal:

 Create a culturally sensitive conflict resolution script to clear misunderstandings and improve collaboration.

Prompt:

 *"You are an intercultural workplace coach. Draft a conflict resolution script for addressing a cultural communication misunderstanding between two colleagues. The script should include:

  - Neutral Opening – Acknowledge cultural diversity as a strength.

  - Clarification of Concerns – Each person explains their interpretation of specific interactions.

  - Cultural Awareness Briefing – Briefly highlight how cultural norms influence communication styles.

  - Mutual Understanding – Facilitate empathy by having each person reframe the other’s intent.

  - Communication Agreement – Define shared communication norms for the team.

  - Ongoing Support Plan – Suggest periodic check-ins to ensure improved collaboration.

 Use language that prevents stereotyping while promoting respect."*

Inputs:

  - Examples of past misunderstandings

  - Cultural background of both parties

  - Company diversity and inclusion policy

  - Project timeline and urgency
