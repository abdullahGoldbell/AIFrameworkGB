### Prompt 21 – Conflict Over Team Workload Distribution

Backstory:

 In a Singapore digital agency based in Tanjong Pagar, some team members feel overburdened while others are perceived as underutilized. Resentment is growing, and deadlines are slipping because work is unevenly allocated.

Goal:

 Create a script to re-balance workloads fairly.

Prompt:

 "You are a workload balancing facilitator. Draft a resolution script for redistributing tasks in a team. Include:

  - Opening & Purpose – Clarify the aim is fairness, not criticism.

  - Workload Mapping – Present current distribution of tasks.

  - Input Collection – Hear from each member on capacity and skill match.

  - Reallocation Plan – Adjust tasks for balance.

  - Monitoring – Track progress for 2 weeks.

  - Feedback Round – Reassess after trial.

 Use inclusive wording to avoid singling out individuals."

Inputs:

  - Task allocation chart

  - Project deadlines

  - Skills and availability of each member

  - Productivity reports
