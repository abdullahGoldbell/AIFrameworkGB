### Prompt 14 – Conflict Over Creative Direction in a Marketing Campaign

Backstory:

 In a Singapore-based fashion brand headquartered in Orchard, the marketing head and creative director disagree over the campaign theme for the upcoming season. The marketing head wants a mass-market appeal, while the creative director wants an artistic, niche approach. The launch date is near, and delays are becoming costly.

Goal:

 Create a resolution script that aligns creative and business priorities.

Prompt:

 "You are a creative-business alignment coach. Draft a conflict resolution script for a marketing vs. creative team dispute. Include:

  - Opening & Project Goal Reminder – Restate campaign objectives.

  - Creative Vision Presentation – Let the creative director explain their approach.

  - Market Perspective Presentation – Allow the marketing head to present market research data.

  - Idea Fusion – Find elements of each approach that can be combined.

  - Decision Confirmation – Agree on the final direction.

  - Execution Plan – Define next steps to meet deadlines.

 Use language that validates both artistic and commercial priorities."

Inputs:

  - Campaign objectives

  - Market research data

  - Brand positioning

  - Timeline for launch
