### Prompt 19 – Creative Role Performance Review

Backstory:

 A film and content production house based in Singapore (servicing brands across Southeast Asia) wants to evaluate scriptwriters, editors, and designers based on creativity, originality, and alignment with brand storytelling. They need a review format that captures qualitative aspects alongside deadlines and project impact.

Goal:

 Create a performance review template for creative roles.

Prompt:

 *"You are a creative industry HR consultant. Design a performance review form for creative professionals that measures:

  - Originality of ideas

  - Brand tone and vision alignment

  - Timeliness of deliverables

  - Team collaboration in creative process

  - Audience reception/impact of creative output

 Include open-ended questions for creative feedback."*

Inputs:

  - Project type (film, digital ad, etc.)

  - Review period

Audience feedback availability
