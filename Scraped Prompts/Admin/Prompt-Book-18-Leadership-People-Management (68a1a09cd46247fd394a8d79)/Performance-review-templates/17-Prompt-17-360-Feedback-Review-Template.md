### Prompt 17 – 360° Feedback Review Template

Backstory:

 A fintech company based in Singapore’s CBD wants a full-circle evaluation method involving self-assessment, peer review, manager assessment, and subordinate feedback. They believe 360° feedback offers richer, multi-perspective insights compared to traditional top-down reviews.

Goal:

 Create a 360° performance review template.

Prompt:

 *"You are an HR strategy consultant. Create a 360° feedback performance review form that includes:

  - Self-assessment section

  - Peer evaluation questions

  - Manager feedback criteria

  - Subordinate feedback (for managers)

  - Summary and development plan

 Include confidentiality guidelines for honest responses."*

Inputs:

  - Role level

  - Review frequency

  - Competency categories
