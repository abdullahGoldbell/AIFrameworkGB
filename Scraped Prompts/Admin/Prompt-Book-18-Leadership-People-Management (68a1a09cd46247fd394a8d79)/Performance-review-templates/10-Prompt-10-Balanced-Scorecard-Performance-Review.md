### Prompt 10 – Balanced Scorecard Performance Review

Backstory:

 A bank headquartered in Marina Bay, Singapore, is implementing the Balanced Scorecard methodology for performance management. The HR team needs a review form that balances financial metrics, customer service, internal processes, and learning & growth indicators.

Goal:

 Create a Balanced Scorecard-based review template.

Prompt:

 *"You are an HR performance measurement expert. Design a Balanced Scorecard performance review form with four sections:

  - Financial performance

  - Customer satisfaction and service quality

  - Internal process efficiency

  - Learning and growth metrics

 Include scoring guidelines, weightage, and space for qualitative comments."*

Inputs:

  - Department type (Retail Banking, Corporate Banking, etc.)

  - KPI metrics for each scorecard category

  - Review frequency
