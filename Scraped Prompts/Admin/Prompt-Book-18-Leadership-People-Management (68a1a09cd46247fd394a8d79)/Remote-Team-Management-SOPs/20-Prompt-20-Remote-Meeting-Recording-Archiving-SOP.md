### Prompt 20 — Remote Meeting Recording & Archiving SOP

Backstory:

 Anand Suresh, 35, leads operations for a Singapore consultancy that conducts multiple remote client calls daily. Often, key decisions made in meetings are forgotten or misremembered, leading to disputes. While they record meetings, the storage is disorganized and retrieval is difficult. Anand needs a proper SOP that ensures every meeting is recorded, summarized, tagged, and stored in a way that makes it easy to retrieve at any point in the future.

Goal:

 Create a remote meeting recording and archiving SOP that ensures secure, searchable, and easy-to-access meeting history.

Prompt:

 "You are a remote knowledge management specialist. Create a Meeting Recording & Archiving SOP for a consultancy handling multiple daily client calls. The SOP must specify:

  - Recording Standards — when to record, what tools to use, and consent protocols.

  - File Naming Conventions — standardized format with client name, date, and meeting type.

  - Storage Structure — folder hierarchy by client, project, and date.

  - Meeting Summaries — quick bullet-point notes stored alongside recordings.

  - Access Control — who can view/download recordings.

  - Archival Policy — retention period and deletion protocols."

Inputs:

  - Number of meetings per day

  - Storage platform in use (Google Drive, Dropbox, SharePoint)

  - Client confidentiality requirements

  - Access permissions hierarchy

File retention policies
