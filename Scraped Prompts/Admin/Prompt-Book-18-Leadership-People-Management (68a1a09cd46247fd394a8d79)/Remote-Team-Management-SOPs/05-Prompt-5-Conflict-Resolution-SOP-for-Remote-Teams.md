### Prompt 5 — Conflict Resolution SOP for Remote Teams

Backstory:

 Michelle Lee, 30, manages a hybrid marketing team where half the staff work remotely from various neighbourhoods around Singapore (Tampines, Jurong, Woodlands) and a couple of regional members in Kuala Lumpur and Bangkok. She notices conflicts often get escalated because tone is misinterpreted over text, and there’s no standard way to resolve disagreements. She needs an SOP that helps managers identify, address, and resolve remote team conflicts quickly and respectfully.

Goal:

 Create a conflict resolution SOP specifically for remote and hybrid teams to maintain trust and team cohesion.

Prompt:

 "You are a workplace conflict management specialist. Write a conflict resolution SOP for a hybrid marketing team of 20 members. Include:

  - A system for flagging conflicts early.

  - Steps for private one-on-one discussions before group escalation.

  - Video call etiquette for conflict resolution.

  - Guidelines for involving HR when needed.

  - A conflict log template for tracking issues and outcomes."

Inputs:

  - Team size

  - Nature of work (collaborative/individual)

  - Preferred conflict resolution style

  - HR escalation process
