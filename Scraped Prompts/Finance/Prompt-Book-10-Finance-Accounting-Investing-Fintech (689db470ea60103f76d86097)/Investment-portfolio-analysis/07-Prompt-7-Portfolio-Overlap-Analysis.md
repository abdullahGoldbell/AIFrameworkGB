### **Prompt 7 — Portfolio Overlap Analysis**

Backstory:

 A 25-year-old content creator based in Singapore has invested in 6 unit trusts and suspects they might be holding the same stocks. This could lead to over-concentration in certain companies and reduce diversification benefits. She wants to know the degree of portfolio overlap.

Goal:

 Identify duplicate stock holdings across multiple funds.

Prompt:

 "You are a unit trust data analyst. Perform an overlap analysis across all holdings.

 Tasks:

  - List all unique stocks held across funds

  - Calculate % of portfolio invested in each stock

  - Highlight any stock with >10% cumulative exposure

  - Recommend alternative funds to reduce duplication"

Inputs Required:

  - List of unit trusts in portfolio

  - Latest fund factsheets or holdings data
