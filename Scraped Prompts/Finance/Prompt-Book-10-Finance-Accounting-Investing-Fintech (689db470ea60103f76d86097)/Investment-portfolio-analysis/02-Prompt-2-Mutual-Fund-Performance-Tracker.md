### **Prompt 2 — Mutual Fund Performance Tracker**

Backstory:

 You are working with a 32-year-old marketing professional from Singapore who has invested in 8 different unit trusts over the past 3 years through her bank's wealth platform. She regularly adds monthly RSP (Regular Savings Plan) contributions but has never compared their performance against benchmarks or peers. With inflation rising, she wants to know which funds are underperforming and if she should stop or switch her RSPs.

Goal:

 Analyze mutual fund performance and recommend corrective actions.

Prompt:

 "You are a unit trust analyst. Review the investor's fund portfolio.

 For each fund:

  - Compare 1-year, 3-year, and 5-year CAGR against benchmark indices

  - Check consistency of returns (rolling returns analysis)

  - Flag underperforming funds

  - Recommend whether to hold, switch, or stop RSPs"

Inputs Required:

  - Fund names and codes

  - Number of units and current NAV

  - Monthly RSP amount and start date for each fund

  - Benchmark index for each fund
