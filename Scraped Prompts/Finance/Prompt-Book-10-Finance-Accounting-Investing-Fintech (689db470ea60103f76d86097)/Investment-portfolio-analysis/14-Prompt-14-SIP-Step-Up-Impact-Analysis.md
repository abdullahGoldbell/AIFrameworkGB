### **Prompt 14 — RSP Step-Up Impact Analysis**

Backstory:

 A 27-year-old banker in Singapore invests S$500/month through a Regular Savings Plan (RSP) into ETFs and unit trusts, and plans to increase contributions by 10% each year as his salary grows. He wants to know the impact of this step-up strategy over 20 years compared to keeping RSP amounts fixed.

Goal:

 Project future value of step-up RSP strategy.

Prompt:

 "You are a unit trust planner. Compare corpus from a fixed RSP vs. 10% annual step-up RSP over 20 years.

 Show:

  - Year-wise contribution and total corpus for both methods

  - Additional returns generated by step-up strategy

  - Chart comparing growth curves"

Inputs Required:

  - Base RSP amount

  - Step-up percentage per year

  - Investment duration

  - Expected return rate
