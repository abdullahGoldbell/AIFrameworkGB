### **Prompt 22 — Retirement Corpus Projection**

Backstory:

 A 29-year-old architect in Singapore wants to retire at 55. She invests in unit trusts and makes voluntary CPF contributions, but isn’t sure if her current pace will meet her desired lifestyle.

Goal:

 Project retirement corpus and identify gaps.

Prompt:

 "You are a retirement planner. Project corpus at retirement age based on current portfolio and contributions.

 Show:

  - Total projected corpus

  - Required corpus for desired lifestyle

  - Additional investment needed"

Inputs Required:

  - Current portfolio value

  - Monthly investment amount

  - Expected annual return

  - Inflation rate
