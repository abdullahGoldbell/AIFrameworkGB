### **Prompt 25 — Annual Portfolio Review Report**

Backstory:

 A 33-year-old finance professional in Singapore makes ad-hoc investments but has never done a full portfolio review. He wants an annual summary that covers performance, asset allocation, risks, and recommendations.

Goal:

 Create a comprehensive annual portfolio review.

Prompt:

 "You are a portfolio manager. Prepare a full-year review of the portfolio.

 Include:

  - Annual returns vs benchmark (e.g. STI ETF, MSCI World)

  - Asset allocation chart

  - Major risks and recommendations

  - Action plan for next year"

Inputs Required:

  - Portfolio holdings with purchase data

  - Benchmark indices

  - Annual transaction history
