### **Prompt 23 — Reinvestment Strategy for Maturity Proceeds**

Backstory:

 A 31-year-old dentist in Singapore has a fixed deposit maturing next month and wants to reinvest the amount to improve returns without taking excessive risk.

Goal:

 Suggest reinvestment options based on risk profile.

Prompt:

 "You are a personal investment consultant. Recommend reinvestment options for maturity proceeds.

 Include:

  - At least 3 options with risk/return profile

  - Tax implications

  - Liquidity considerations"

Inputs Required:

  - Maturity amount

  - Risk tolerance

  - Investment horizon
