### **Prompt 13 — Portfolio Liquidity Assessment**

Backstory:

 A 25-year-old MBA student in Singapore holds a mix of CPF Special Account, CPF Investment Scheme, SRS contributions, and equity unit trusts. She plans to start her own business in 3 years and needs to know how quickly she can liquidate her investments if required.

Goal:

 Evaluate liquidity of each investment in the portfolio.

Prompt:

 "You are a wealth planner. Assess liquidity for each portfolio component.

 For each holding:

  - Indicate lock-in period (if any)

  - Estimate time to convert to cash

  - Highlight any penalties for early withdrawal"

Inputs Required:

  - Full list of investments with start dates

  - Applicable lock-in rules

  - Withdrawal penalties or restrictions
