### **Prompt 18 — Portfolio Alignment with Goals**

Backstory:

 A 32-year-old lawyer in Singapore has 3 major goals — buying a condo in 5 years, child’s university education in 15 years, and retirement in 30 years. She wants to know if her current portfolio allocation supports these timelines.

Goal:

 Match investments to specific life goals.

Prompt:

 "You are a goal-based financial planner. Align portfolio with stated financial goals.

 For each goal:

  - Estimate required corpus

  - Map suitable investments from current portfolio

  - Identify shortfall and suggest new investments"

Inputs Required:

  - Current portfolio details

  - Goal timelines and estimated costs

  - Inflation assumption
