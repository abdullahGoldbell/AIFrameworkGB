### **Prompt 20 — Portfolio Concentration Risk Report**

Backstory:

 A 30-year-old mechanical engineer in Singapore holds 70% of his equity investments in just 3 companies. He wants to understand the risk of such concentration and its effect on volatility.

Goal:

 Assess and visualize portfolio concentration risk.

Prompt:

 "You are a portfolio risk analyst. Create a report showing % allocation to top holdings.

 Highlight:

  - Any single holding >10% of portfolio

  - Impact of 20% drop in top holdings"

Inputs Required:

  - Portfolio holdings and values

  - Risk tolerance level
