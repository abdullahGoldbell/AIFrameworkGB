### **Prompt 9 — Asset Allocation Rebalancing Plan**

Backstory:

 A 29-year-old finance manager in Singapore targets 70% equity, 20% bonds, and 10% gold allocation. After 2 years of market rallies, his equity allocation has grown to 82%, creating excess exposure to market volatility. He wants a plan to rebalance without triggering large tax liabilities.

Goal:

 Create a tax-efficient rebalancing plan to restore target allocation.

Prompt:

 "You are a portfolio rebalancing advisor. Suggest steps to move from current allocation to target 70/20/10 mix.

 Include:

  - Amounts to be shifted between asset classes

  - Priority order for selling assets to minimize tax impact

  - Suggest regular savings plan (RSP) or systematic withdrawal options if gradual rebalancing is better"

Inputs Required:

  - Current portfolio value by asset class

  - Target allocation percentages

  - Tax holding period status for each asset
