### **Prompt 24 — Goal-Based SIP Allocation**

Backstory:

 A 25-year-old fresh graduate working in Singapore wants to start 3 regular savings plans (RSPs) for different goals — a motorbike or COE-supported car downpayment in 3 years, a master's degree in 5 years, and an HDB BTO downpayment in 10 years.

Goal:

 Allocate monthly RSP amounts to each goal for optimal growth.

Prompt:

 "You are an RSP allocation planner. Suggest fund types and amounts for each goal.

 For each goal:

  - Recommended asset class/fund category

  - Monthly contribution required

  - Expected return and maturity value"

Inputs Required:

  - Goal timelines and cost estimates

  - Risk appetite

  - Total monthly budget for RSPs
