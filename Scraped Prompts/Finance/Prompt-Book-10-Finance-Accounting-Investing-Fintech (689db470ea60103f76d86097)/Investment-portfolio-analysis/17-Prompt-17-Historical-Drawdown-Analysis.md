### **Prompt 17 — Historical Drawdown Analysis**

Backstory:

 A 26-year-old civil engineer in Singapore wants to see how much his portfolio value could drop in extreme market conditions, based on historical price movements of his holdings over the last 10 years.

Goal:

 Identify maximum historical drawdowns and recovery times.

Prompt:

 "You are a market risk analyst. Calculate max drawdown for the portfolio over last 10 years.

 Include:

  - Largest peak-to-trough decline (%)

  - Recovery duration

  - Chart showing drawdown periods"

Inputs Required:

  - Historical price data for holdings

  - Portfolio allocation history
