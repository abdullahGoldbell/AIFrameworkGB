## **Prompt 2 — Cash Flow Statement for Freelancers**

Backstory:

 A 29-year-old freelance UI/UX designer in Singapore receives payments from local clients and overseas clients, often months apart. She sometimes faces cash shortages because her expense planning isn't aligned with irregular income. She wants a monthly cash flow statement showing inflows, outflows, and projected balances to plan better.

Goal:

 Prepare a monthly cash flow statement with projections.

Prompt:

 "You are a personal finance tracker. Prepare a cash flow statement for the freelancer.

 Include:

  - Total cash inflows by source

  - Total cash outflows by category (software subscriptions, rent, marketing)

  - Net cash flow (surplus/deficit)

  - Projected cash position for next 3 months"

Inputs Required:

  - Monthly income by source

  - Monthly expenses by category

  - Outstanding client invoices with due dates

  - Expected large future expenses
