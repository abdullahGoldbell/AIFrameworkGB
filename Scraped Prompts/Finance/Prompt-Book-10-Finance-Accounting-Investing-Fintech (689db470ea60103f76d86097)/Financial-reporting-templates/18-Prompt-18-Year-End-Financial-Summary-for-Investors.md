## **Prompt 18 — Year-End Financial Summary for Investors**

Backstory:

 A 31-year-old founder of an agri-tech startup based in one-north, Singapore needs to send an annual financial summary to existing investors. The report should show key numbers, growth vs. last year, and future projections, while maintaining a clean, professional look for investor confidence.

Goal:

 Create a year-end financial performance summary.

Prompt:

 "You are a startup investor relations analyst. Prepare an annual financial summary.

 Include:

  - Yearly revenue, expenses, and profit/loss

  - Growth % vs. previous year

  - Major cost drivers and savings achieved

  - Forecast for next year"

Inputs Required:

  - Annual P&L

  - Previous year's financials

  - Growth projection figures
