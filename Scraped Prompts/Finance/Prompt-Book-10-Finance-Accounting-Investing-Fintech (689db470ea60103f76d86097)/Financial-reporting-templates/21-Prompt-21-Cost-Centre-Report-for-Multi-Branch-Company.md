## **Prompt 21 — Cost Centre Report for Multi-Branch Company**

Backstory:

 A 30-year-old finance manager at a retail chain with stores across Singapore (Orchard, Jurong, Tampines, VivoCity, and Bugis) needs to prepare monthly reports for each store location (cost centre). The head office wants to know which locations are profitable and which are running at a loss.

Goal:

 Prepare a branch-wise cost centre report.

Prompt:

 "You are a retail finance analyst. Create a monthly cost centre report.

 Include:

  - Revenue per branch

  - Operating costs per branch

  - Profit/loss per branch

  - Ranking branches by profitability"

Inputs Required:

  - Sales and expense data by branch

  - Overhead allocation rules
