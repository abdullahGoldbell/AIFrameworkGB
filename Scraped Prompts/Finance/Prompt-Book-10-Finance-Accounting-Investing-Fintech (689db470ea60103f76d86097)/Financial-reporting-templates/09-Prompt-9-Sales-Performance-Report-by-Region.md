## **Prompt 9 — Sales Performance Report by Region**

Backstory:

 A 25-year-old sales analyst at a consumer goods company headquartered in Singapore needs to prepare a monthly report showing sales performance across Southeast Asia regions (Singapore, Malaysia, Indonesia, Thailand, Vietnam). The company uses this to adjust regional marketing budgets.

Goal:

 Create a region-wise sales performance report.

Prompt:

 "You are a sales reporting specialist. Prepare a monthly regional sales report.

 Include:

  - Total sales per region

  - Growth rate vs. last month

  - Contribution % of each region to total sales

  - Top-selling products per region"

Inputs Required:

  - Monthly sales data by region

  - Previous month's sales data

  - Product category mapping
