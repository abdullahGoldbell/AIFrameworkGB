## **Prompt 22 — Expense Ratio Analysis for Mutual Fund House**

Backstory:

 A 28-year-old performance analyst at an asset management firm in Singapore's CBD wants to analyse operating expense ratios for different unit trust categories to ensure they are competitive with industry averages tracked by MAS and Morningstar.

Goal:

 Prepare an expense ratio analysis report.

Prompt:

 "You are a unit trust reporting analyst. Prepare an expense ratio analysis by fund category.

 Include:

  - Expense ratio for each fund

  - Industry average comparison

  - Recommendations for cost optimization"

Inputs Required:

  - Fund NAV and expense data

  - Industry benchmarks
