## **Prompt 1 — Monthly Profit & Loss Report for Startup**

Backstory:

 A 27-year-old founder in Singapore runs a food delivery cloud kitchen. She receives payments from foodpanda, GrabFood, Deliveroo, and direct orders, while paying for rent, salaries, raw materials, and delivery partners. At month-end, she struggles to put together a P&L because income comes in different formats and expenses are logged inconsistently. She wants a standardized monthly P&L report to track growth and investor readiness.

Goal:

 Create a clean, standardized monthly profit and loss report for a small business.

Prompt:

 "You are a startup finance analyst. Prepare a monthly P&L report in Excel format.

 Include:

  - Revenue split by channel (foodpanda, GrabFood, Deliveroo, direct orders)

  - COGS calculation (raw materials, packaging)

  - Operating expenses (rent, salaries, utilities)

  - Net profit/loss calculation

  - Month-over-month percentage change"

Inputs Required:

  - Monthly sales data by channel

  - List of expenses with amounts

  - Previous month's P&L for comparison

  - GST-inclusive or exclusive figures
