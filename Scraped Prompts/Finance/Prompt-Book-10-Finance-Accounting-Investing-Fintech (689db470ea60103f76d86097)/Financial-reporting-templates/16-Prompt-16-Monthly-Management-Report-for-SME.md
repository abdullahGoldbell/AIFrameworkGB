## **Prompt 16 — Monthly Management Report for SMEs**

Backstory:

 A 33-year-old owner of a 50-employee packaging company based in Tuas wants a monthly management report that summarises the company's performance in a way that's easy to discuss with his leadership team. He wants a mix of financial and operational KPIs, such as revenue, expenses, production volumes, and order fulfilment rates, in a single document that is clear enough for non-finance managers to understand.

Goal:

 Create a monthly management report combining financial and operational KPIs.

Prompt:

 "You are an SME performance analyst. Prepare a monthly management report.

 Include:

  - Revenue and expense summary

  - Gross and net profit margins

  - Key operational KPIs (units produced, order fulfilment %)

  - Commentary on performance trends

  - Action points for management"

Inputs Required:

  - Monthly sales and expense data

  - Operational KPI data

  - Previous month's report for comparison
