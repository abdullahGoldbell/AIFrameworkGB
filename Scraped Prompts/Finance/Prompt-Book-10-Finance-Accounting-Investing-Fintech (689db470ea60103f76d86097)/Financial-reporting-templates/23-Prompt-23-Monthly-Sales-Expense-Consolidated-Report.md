## **Prompt 23 — Monthly Sales & Expense Consolidated Report**

Backstory:

 A 27-year-old entrepreneur in Singapore runs two separate businesses — a café in Tiong Bahru and an online bakery delivering across the island. She wants a consolidated monthly report combining sales and expenses for both ventures to get a single profitability view.

Goal:

 Prepare a consolidated monthly sales and expense report.

Prompt:

 "You are a multi-business finance analyst. Prepare a monthly consolidated report.

 Include:

  - Sales total for each business and combined total

  - Expense total for each business and combined total

  - Net profit/loss for each business and combined"

Inputs Required:

  - Monthly sales and expense data for both businesses

  - Allocation of shared expenses
