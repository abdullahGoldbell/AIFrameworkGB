## **Prompt 11 — Balance Sheet Template for SMEs**

Backstory:

 A 32-year-old owner of a small textile manufacturing unit in Tuas, Singapore needs a professional balance sheet format for bank loan applications. His current records are handwritten and lack standard accounting presentation.

Goal:

 Provide a clean, SME-friendly balance sheet template.

Prompt:

 "You are an accounting documentation expert. Create a balance sheet template for SMEs.

 Include:

  - Assets (current, non-current)

  - Liabilities (short-term, long-term)

  - Equity section

  - Automated total calculation formulas"

Inputs Required:

  - Asset and liability lists with values

  - Preferred reporting currency

  - Reporting date
