## **Prompt 7 — GST Collection & Payment Summary**

Backstory:

 A 28-year-old owner of a small electronics store in Singapore wants a monthly GST report summarizing total GST collected from customers and GST paid to suppliers, along with net payable or refundable amounts. This helps him prepare for IRAS GST return filing without last-minute stress.

Goal:

 Create a GST collection and payment summary for the month.

Prompt:

 "You are a GST accountant. Prepare a monthly GST summary report.

 Include:

  - Total GST collected on sales (output tax)

  - Total GST paid on purchases (input tax)

  - Net GST payable or refundable

  - Invoice count for sales and purchases"

Inputs Required:

  - Sales invoice list with GST amounts

  - Purchase invoice list with GST amounts

  - GST rate applicable (Singapore GST 9%)
