## **Prompt 19 — Liquidation Value Estimation**

Backstory:

 A 33-year-old manufacturing business owner in Singapore needs to shut down operations at her Jurong facility and sell assets. She wants to know the liquidation value of machinery, inventory, and other assets for settlement planning.

Goal:

 Estimate liquidation value of assets.

Prompt:

 *"You are an asset valuation consultant. Calculate:

  - Current market resale value for each asset

  - Total liquidation value after disposal costs"*

Inputs Required:

  - Asset list with purchase details

  - Current market price estimates

  - Disposal costs
