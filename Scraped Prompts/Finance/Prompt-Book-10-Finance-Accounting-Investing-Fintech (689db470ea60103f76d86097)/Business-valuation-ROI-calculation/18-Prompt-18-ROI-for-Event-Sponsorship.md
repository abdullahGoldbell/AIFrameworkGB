## **Prompt 18 — ROI for Event Sponsorship**

Backstory:

 A 28-year-old beverage brand owner in Singapore sponsored a major sports event at the Singapore Sports Hub for S$70,000. She wants to calculate ROI in terms of increased sales and brand awareness.

Goal:

 Evaluate event sponsorship ROI.

Prompt:

 *"You are a brand ROI analyst. Calculate:

  - Incremental sales revenue post-event

  - ROI %

  - Intangible benefits summary"*

Inputs Required:

  - Sponsorship cost

  - Sales before and after event

  - Brand awareness survey data
