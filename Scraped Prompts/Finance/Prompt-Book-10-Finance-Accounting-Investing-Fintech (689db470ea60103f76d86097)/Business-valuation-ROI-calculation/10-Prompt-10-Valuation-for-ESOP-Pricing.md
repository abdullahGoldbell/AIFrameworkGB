## **Prompt 10 — Valuation for ESOP Pricing**

Backstory:

 A 31-year-old CFO at a fintech startup based in Singapore's CBD needs to determine the fair market value (FMV) of shares for issuing ESOPs to employees, in compliance with Singapore tax laws and IRAS guidelines.

Goal:

 Calculate FMV for ESOP allotment.

Prompt:

 *"You are a corporate finance advisor. Calculate FMV using:

  - DCF method

  - Net asset value method

  - Choose most appropriate based on company stage"*

Inputs Required:

  - Company financial statements

  - Future cash flow projections

  - Industry multiples
