## **Prompt 22 — ROI of Employee Wellness Program**

Backstory:

 A 32-year-old HR head at a Singapore company implemented a wellness program costing S$150,000. She wants to evaluate its ROI through reduced sick days and improved productivity.

Goal:

 Calculate ROI for wellness programs.

Prompt:

 *"You are an HR ROI analyst. Calculate:

  - Tangible savings from reduced absenteeism

  - Productivity gains in S$ terms

  - ROI %"*

Inputs Required:

  - Program cost

  - Sick leave data before and after program

  - Productivity measurement metrics
