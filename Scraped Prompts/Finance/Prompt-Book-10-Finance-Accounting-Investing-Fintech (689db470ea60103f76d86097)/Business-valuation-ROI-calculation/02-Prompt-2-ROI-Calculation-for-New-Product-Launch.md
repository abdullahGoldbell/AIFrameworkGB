## **Prompt 2 — ROI Calculation for New Product Launch**

Backstory:

 A 31-year-old FMCG entrepreneur in Singapore plans to launch a new flavoured beverage line for the Southeast Asia market. She has projected development and marketing costs of S$600,000 and expects to sell 200,000 units in the first year. She wants to know the ROI for year one and over a three-year horizon, factoring in unit price, expected margins, and marketing expenses.

Goal:

 Calculate ROI for a product launch over multiple time frames.

Prompt:

 *"You are a product finance analyst. Calculate ROI for the product launch:

  - Year 1, Year 2, and Year 3 ROI %

  - Break-even point in units and revenue

  - Payback period in months"*

Inputs Required:

  - Development, production, and marketing costs

  - Unit selling price and cost per unit

  - Sales projections for 3 years
