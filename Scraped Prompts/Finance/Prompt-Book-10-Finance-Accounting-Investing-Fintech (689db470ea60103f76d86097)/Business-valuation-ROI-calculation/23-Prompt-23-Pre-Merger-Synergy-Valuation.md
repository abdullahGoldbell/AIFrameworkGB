## **Prompt 23 — Pre-Merger Synergy Valuation**

Backstory:

 A 34-year-old founder based in one-north Singapore is merging with a competitor. She needs to estimate the additional value generated from synergies like shared distribution and reduced marketing costs.

Goal:

 Calculate synergy value for a merger.

Prompt:

 *"You are an M&A consultant. Calculate:

  - Cost savings from synergy

  - Incremental revenue potential

  - Total synergy valuation"*

Inputs Required:

  - Current cost structures of both companies

  - Expected revenue increase

  - Industry synergy benchmarks
