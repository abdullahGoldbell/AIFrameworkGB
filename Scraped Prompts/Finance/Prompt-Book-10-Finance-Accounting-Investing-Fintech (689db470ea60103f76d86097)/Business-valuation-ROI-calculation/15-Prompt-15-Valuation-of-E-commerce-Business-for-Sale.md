## **Prompt 15 — Valuation of E-commerce Business for Sale**

Backstory:

 A 32-year-old e-commerce entrepreneur in Singapore runs a Shopee and Lazada store with S$2 million in annual revenue and S$320,000 profit. She wants to sell the business and needs a valuation based on profit multiples and market comparables.

Goal:

 Calculate e-commerce business valuation.

Prompt:

 *"You are an e-commerce business broker. Calculate valuation using:

  - Seller's Discretionary Earnings (SDE) multiple

  - Comparable online store sales data"*

Inputs Required:

  - Annual revenue and profit

  - SDE adjustments

  - Comparable sales data
