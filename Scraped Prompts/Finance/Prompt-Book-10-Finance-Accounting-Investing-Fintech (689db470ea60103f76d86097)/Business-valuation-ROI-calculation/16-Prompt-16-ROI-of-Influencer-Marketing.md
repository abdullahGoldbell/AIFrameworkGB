## **Prompt 16 — ROI of Influencer Marketing**

Backstory:

 A 26-year-old fashion startup founder in Singapore spent S$40,000 on influencer campaigns with local Instagram and TikTok creators. She wants to measure the ROI by comparing sales generated directly from influencer links vs. the cost.

Goal:

 Calculate influencer marketing ROI.

Prompt:

 *"You are a marketing analytics consultant. Calculate:

  - Total revenue from influencer-driven sales

  - ROI %

  - CAC vs. LTV of customers acquired"*

Inputs Required:

  - Campaign cost

  - Sales from tracked influencer links

  - Average customer lifetime value
