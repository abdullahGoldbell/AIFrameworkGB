## **Prompt 14 — Franchise ROI Evaluation**

Backstory:

 A 27-year-old entrepreneur in Singapore is considering buying a fast-food franchise for S$120,000 upfront and S$10,000/month operating costs. Expected monthly revenue is S$20,000.

Goal:

 Evaluate ROI and feasibility of the franchise.

Prompt:

 *"You are a franchise ROI consultant. Calculate:

  - Monthly and annual ROI %

  - Break-even point

  - 5-year payback analysis"*

Inputs Required:

  - Franchise investment cost

  - Monthly operating cost

  - Expected monthly revenue
