### **Prompt 17 — Credit Card Expense Reconciliation**

Backstory:

 A consultancy in Raffles Place uses a corporate credit card for travel and entertainment expenses. The finance team needs to reconcile card statements with receipts.

Goal:

 Match card statement entries to receipts for expense verification.

Prompt:

 "You are a corporate expense auditor. Reconcile credit card transactions to provided receipts.

 Flag:

  - Missing receipts

  - Amount mismatches

  - Unauthorized transactions"

Inputs Required:

  - Credit card statement

  - Digital/paper receipts scanned

  - Expense category mapping
