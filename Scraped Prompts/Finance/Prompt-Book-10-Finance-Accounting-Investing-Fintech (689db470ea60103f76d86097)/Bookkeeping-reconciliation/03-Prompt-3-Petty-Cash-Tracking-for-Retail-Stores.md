### **Prompt 3 — Petty Cash Tracking for Retail Stores**

Backstory:

 A family-owned stationery shop in a Tampines HDB neighbourhood uses petty cash for small daily expenses — coffee for staff, packaging materials, and urgent stock buys. The store owner often forgets to record cash withdrawals and wants a transparent petty cash ledger to monitor leakage.

Goal:

 Create a daily petty cash log with balances updated after every transaction.

Prompt:

 "You are a retail accounting specialist. Create a petty cash log for one month.

 Include:

  - Date of transaction

  - Description of expense or deposit

  - Amount withdrawn or added

  - Running balance after each entry

  - Weekly summary of total spent vs replenished cash"

Inputs Required:

  - Starting petty cash amount

  - List of daily petty cash transactions

  - Cash replenishment dates and amounts

  - Expense categories (e.g., supplies, refreshments, maintenance)
