### **Prompt 18 — Advance Payment Settlement**

Backstory:

 A wedding planner in Singapore takes deposit payments from clients and vendors but struggles to match them to final invoices.

Goal:

 Track advance payments and settlement status.

Prompt:

 "You are a project finance tracker. Create a log for advances taken and settled.

 Include:

  - Client/vendor name

  - Advance amount and date

  - Final invoice amount

  - Settlement balance"

Inputs Required:

  - List of all advances taken

  - Final invoices issued

  - Payment history
