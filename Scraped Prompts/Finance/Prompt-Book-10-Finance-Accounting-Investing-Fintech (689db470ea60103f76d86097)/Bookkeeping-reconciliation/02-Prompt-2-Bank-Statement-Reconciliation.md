### **Prompt 2 — Bank Statement Reconciliation**

Backstory:

 A 27-year-old freelancer based in Singapore earns from multiple sources — Upwork, Fiverr, and direct clients. Payments come in via PayPal, bank transfers, and PayNow. At month-end, they need to reconcile their bank statement with project invoices to ensure all payments are received and recorded.

Goal:

 Generate a reconciled statement showing matched and unmatched transactions.

Prompt:

 "You are a certified bookkeeper. Reconcile the given bank statement with the provided invoice list.

 Steps:

  - Match each payment in the bank statement to a specific invoice.

  - Highlight unmatched entries and flag them for follow-up.

  - Identify duplicate or erroneous transactions.

  - Provide a summary of total matched, pending, and disputed amounts."

Inputs Required:

  - Bank statement (CSV, PDF, or Excel)

  - Invoice list with invoice numbers, amounts, and client names

  - Payment dates tolerance range (e.g., ±3 days)

  - Currency conversion rate if foreign payments exist
