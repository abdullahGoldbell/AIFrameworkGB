### **Prompt 13 — Expense Approval Workflow**

Backstory:

 A digital marketing agency in one-north reimburses employee expenses for client work. The accounts team wants a simple approval tracker to avoid delays.

Goal:

 Track expense approval status for each claim.

Prompt:

 "You are a process documentation specialist. Create an expense claim tracker.

 Fields:

  - Employee name and ID

  - Claim date and amount

  - Approver name and status

  - Payment date if approved"

Inputs Required:

  - List of claims

  - Approval rules

  - Employee department list
