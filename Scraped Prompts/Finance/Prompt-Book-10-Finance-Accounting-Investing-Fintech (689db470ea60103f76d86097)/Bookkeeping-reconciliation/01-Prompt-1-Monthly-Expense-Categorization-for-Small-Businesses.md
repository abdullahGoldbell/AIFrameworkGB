### **Prompt 1 — Monthly Expense Categorization for Small Businesses**

Backstory:

 You have a 31-year-old entrepreneur running a boutique in Singapore who uses PayNow, bank transfers, and occasional cash payments for business transactions. They struggle to track and categorize monthly expenses, especially distinguishing between personal and business spending. They want a clean breakdown for GST filing and internal tracking.

Goal:

 Generate a categorized monthly expense report ready for accounting and compliance.

Prompt:

 "You are a professional accountant. Create a categorized monthly expense report for a small business in Singapore.

 For each transaction:

  - Categorize it as operational, marketing, utilities, payroll, or personal.

  - Add payment method (cash, PayNow, bank transfer, cheque).

  - Flag potential tax-deductible expenses.

  - Summarize total spend per category in SGD.

 Output should be a clean, table-formatted report ready for GST filing."

Inputs Required:

  - List of all transactions with date, amount, and payment method

  - Business nature and industry

  - GST applicability (yes/no)

  - List of known recurring expenses
