### **Prompt 5 — Annual Ledger Cleanup**

Backstory:

 A 35-year-old manufacturing SME based in Tuas has an annual ledger cluttered with duplicate entries, misspelled vendor names, and inconsistent expense categories. Before the annual audit, they need the ledger cleaned and standardized.

Goal:

 Prepare a clean, consistent ledger for the fiscal year.

Prompt:

 "You are an accounting data cleanup specialist. Review and clean the provided ledger for FY 2024–25.

 Tasks:

  - Remove duplicate entries.

  - Standardize vendor and client names.

  - Correct inconsistent category labels.

  - Flag unusual or suspicious transactions.

  - Provide a before-and-after summary showing corrections made."
