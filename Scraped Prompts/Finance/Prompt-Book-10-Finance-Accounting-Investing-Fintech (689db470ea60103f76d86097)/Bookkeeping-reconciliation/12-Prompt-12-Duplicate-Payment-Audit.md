### **Prompt 12 — Duplicate Payment Audit**

Backstory:

 An NGO based in Singapore suspects that a few suppliers have been accidentally paid twice for the same invoice. They want a review of last year's transactions to confirm.

Goal:

 Identify and flag duplicate payments.

Prompt:

 "You are an audit specialist. Review all supplier payments for possible duplicates.

 Criteria:

  - Same vendor name and invoice number

  - Same amount within ±S$1

  - Payment dates within 15 days"

Inputs Required:

  - Supplier payment register

  - Vendor master list

  - Duplicate detection criteria
