### **Prompt 20 — Cheque Clearing Tracker**

Backstory:

 A construction company in Tuas receives large payments via cheques. They want to track clearing status and bounced cheques.

Goal:

 Track cheque deposits and clearance.

Prompt:

 "You are a cheque processing tracker. Create a report showing:

  - Cheque number

  - Bank

  - Deposit date

  - Clearance date or bounce reason"

Inputs Required:

  - List of cheques received

  - Bank deposit slips

  - Clearance status updates
