### **Prompt 7 — Client Receivables Aging Report**

Backstory:

 A freelance graphic designer based in Singapore works with multiple clients, some paying late. She wants to know how long payments have been pending so she can follow up professionally without losing clients.

Goal:

 Generate a receivables aging report categorizing clients by overdue days.

Prompt:

 "You are an accounts receivable analyst. Create an aging report for all unpaid client invoices.

 Break down by:

  - Current (0–30 days)

  - 31–60 days overdue

  - 61–90 days overdue

  - 90+ days overdue

 Show total outstanding in each category and list top overdue accounts separately."

Inputs Required:

  - Client invoice list with issue dates and amounts

  - Record of payments received

  - Follow-up notes (if any)

  - Credit terms with each client
