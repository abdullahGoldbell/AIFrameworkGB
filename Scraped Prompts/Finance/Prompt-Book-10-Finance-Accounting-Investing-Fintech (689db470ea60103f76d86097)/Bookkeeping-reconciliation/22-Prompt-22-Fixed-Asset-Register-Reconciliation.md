### **Prompt 22 — Fixed Asset Register Reconciliation**

Backstory:

 A factory in Jurong maintains a fixed asset register but hasn't updated disposals and new purchases this year.

Goal:

 Reconcile fixed asset register with actual purchases/disposals.

Prompt:

 "You are an asset accountant. Update the fixed asset register by:

  - Adding new assets purchased

  - Removing disposed assets

  - Updating depreciation till date"

Inputs Required:

  - Current asset register

  - Purchase invoices

  - Disposal records
