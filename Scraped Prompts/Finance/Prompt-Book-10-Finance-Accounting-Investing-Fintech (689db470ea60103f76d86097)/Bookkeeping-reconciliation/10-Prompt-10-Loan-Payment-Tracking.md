### **Prompt 10 — Loan Payment Tracking**

Backstory:

 A manufacturing unit based in Tuas has 3 active business loans with different banks (DBS, OCBC, UOB). The owner struggles to track monthly instalment dates, amounts, and interest components.

Goal:

 Maintain a monthly loan payment tracker with due dates and outstanding balances.

Prompt:

 "You are a business finance tracker. Create a loan repayment schedule for all active loans.

 Include:

  - Bank name and loan account number

  - Monthly instalment amount split into principal and interest

  - Due dates

  - Outstanding principal after each payment"

Inputs Required:

  - Loan facility letters or repayment schedules

  - Payment history till date

  - Interest rate for each loan
