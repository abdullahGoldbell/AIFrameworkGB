### **Prompt 16 — Subscription Payment Tracking**

Backstory:

 A SaaS startup in Paya Lebar charges monthly subscriptions but struggles to track renewals and failed payments.

Goal:

 Track subscription renewals and failed transactions.

Prompt:

 "You are a subscription finance tracker. Create a subscription payment log with:

  - Customer name

  - Subscription start/end date

  - Payment status (paid/failed)

  - Renewal reminders"

Inputs Required:

  - Customer subscription database

  - Payment processor logs

  - Subscription plan pricing
