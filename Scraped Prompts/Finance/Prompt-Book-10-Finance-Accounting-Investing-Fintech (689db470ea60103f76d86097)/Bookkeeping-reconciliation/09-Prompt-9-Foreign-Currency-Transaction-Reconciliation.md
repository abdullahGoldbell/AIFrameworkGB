### **Prompt 9 — Foreign Currency Transaction Reconciliation**

Backstory:

 A startup in Singapore exports software services and receives payments in USD and EUR. Exchange rates fluctuate, and the finance head needs a monthly reconciliation showing SGD equivalents and forex gain/loss.

Goal:

 Reconcile foreign currency inflows with correct SGD conversions.

Prompt:

 "You are a forex accounting specialist. Reconcile foreign currency receipts with invoice amounts and bank credits.

 Steps:

  - Convert each payment to SGD at the applicable bank rate on the credit date

  - Show any forex gain/loss compared to invoice rate

  - Summarize totals per currency"

Inputs Required:

  - Invoice list with currency and rates used

  - Bank credit statement with SGD amounts

  - Official daily exchange rate source (e.g., MAS reference rate)

  - Forex charges/fees
