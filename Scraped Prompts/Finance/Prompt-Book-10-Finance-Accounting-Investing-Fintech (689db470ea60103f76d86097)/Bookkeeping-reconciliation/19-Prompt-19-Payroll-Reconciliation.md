### **Prompt 19 — Payroll Reconciliation**

Backstory:

 A mid-sized international school in Bukit Timah processes monthly salaries but finds mismatches between payroll register and bank transfers.

Goal:

 Reconcile payroll payouts to bank credits.

Prompt:

 "You are a payroll reconciliation specialist. Match payroll register entries with bank transfer statements.

 Flag:

  - Missing payments

  - Extra payments

  - Amount mismatches"

Inputs Required:

  - Payroll register

  - Bank transfer statement

  - Pay period
