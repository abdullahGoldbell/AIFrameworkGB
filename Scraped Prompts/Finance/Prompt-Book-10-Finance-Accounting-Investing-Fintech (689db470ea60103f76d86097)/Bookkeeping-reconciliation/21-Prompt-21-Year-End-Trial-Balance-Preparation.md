### **Prompt 21 — Year-End Trial Balance Preparation**

Backstory:

 A Singapore-registered NGO needs a trial balance ready for audit but their accounts are scattered across bank statements, receipts, and Excel sheets.

Goal:

 Prepare a complete trial balance.

Prompt:

 "You are an accountant. Prepare a trial balance from provided data.

 Include:

  - Ledger accounts with debit/credit balances

  - Totals matching

  - Highlight accounts needing clarification"

Inputs Required:

  - Ledger data

  - Journal entries

  - Bank reconciliations
