### **Prompt 10 — Tax Provisioning & Reminder System**

Backstory:

 A 34-year-old self-employed interior designer in Singapore often forgets to set aside funds for her annual income tax and GIRO instalments, leading to cashflow strain when the IRAS Notice of Assessment arrives. She wants a system that sends her reminders and pre-calculates each set-aside based on updated income estimates.

Goal:

 Set up an automated tax provisioning, GIRO instalment, and reminder plan.

Prompt:

 "You are a financial automation consultant. Design a reminder system for income tax provisioning and GIRO instalment payments in Singapore.

 Include:

  - Key IRAS dates (ECI, Form B/B1 filing, NOA, GIRO instalment schedule)

  - Estimated set-aside amounts per month

  - Method to adjust for income fluctuations"

Inputs Required:

  - Estimated annual income pattern

  - Previous year's tax paid

  - Current quarter's income data
