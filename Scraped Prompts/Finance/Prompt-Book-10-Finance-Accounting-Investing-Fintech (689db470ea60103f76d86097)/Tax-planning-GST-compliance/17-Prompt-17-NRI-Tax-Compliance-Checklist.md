### **Prompt 17 — Non-Resident Tax Compliance Checklist**

Backstory:

 A 33-year-old software engineer who is a Singapore citizen is currently working on assignment in the United Kingdom but still has investments back home — a CPF Investment Scheme portfolio, Singapore-listed REITs and unit trusts, and a private condo in Bishan that she rents out. She files her UK taxes from abroad but often misses details about the Singapore-UK Double Taxation Agreement (DTA), repatriation of rental proceeds, and rental income reporting to IRAS. She needs a consolidated annual checklist to ensure she meets all her cross-border tax obligations without penalties.

Goal:

 Create a comprehensive cross-border tax compliance checklist for a Singapore tax resident / non-resident with Singapore income sources.

Prompt:

 "You are a Singapore cross-border taxation consultant. Prepare a checklist for annual tax compliance for a non-resident or overseas-posted individual with Singapore income and assets.

 Include:

  - Determining tax residency status under IRAS rules and applying the relevant DTA

  - Reporting and paying tax on Singapore-source rental income to IRAS

  - Capital gains treatment on disposal of Singapore property and shares (and any seller's stamp duty / ABSD considerations)

  - Repatriation of funds and MAS / bank reporting requirements"

Inputs Required:

  - Country of current residence and posting period

  - List of Singapore income sources (rental, dividends, interest, employment income)

  - Details of Singapore property ownership and tenancy

  - Any recent asset sales in Singapore
