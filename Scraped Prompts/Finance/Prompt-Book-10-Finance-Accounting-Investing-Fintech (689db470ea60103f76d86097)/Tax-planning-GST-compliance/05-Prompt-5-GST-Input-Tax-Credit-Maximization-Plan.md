### **Prompt 5 — GST Input Tax Maximisation Plan**

Backstory:

 A 28-year-old e-commerce seller in Singapore is GST registered but claims less input tax than possible because many supplier tax invoices are missing or do not meet IRAS' tax invoice requirements. She wants a system to track and maximise input tax claims every quarter.

Goal:

 Create a process to ensure maximum GST input tax claims under IRAS rules.

Prompt:

 "You are a GST specialist. Create a quarterly input tax maximisation plan for a Singapore GST-registered business.

 Include:

  - Checking that suppliers are GST registered and that tax invoices meet IRAS requirements

  - Matching purchase listings with supplier tax invoices and import GST permits

  - Following up on missing or non-compliant invoices

  - Avoiding disallowed input tax categories (e.g. club subscriptions, private cars, family benefits)"

Inputs Required:

  - Supplier list with GST registration numbers

  - Purchase register and import permits

  - Current input tax claimed vs. eligible input tax
