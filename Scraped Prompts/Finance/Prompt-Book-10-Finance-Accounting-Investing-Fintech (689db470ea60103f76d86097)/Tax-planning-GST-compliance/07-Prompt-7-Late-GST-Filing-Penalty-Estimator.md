### **Prompt 7 — Late GST Filing Penalty Estimator**

Backstory:

 A 33-year-old small business owner in Singapore missed filing her GST F5 return for two consecutive quarters due to personal emergencies. She wants to know the exact late submission penalty, 5% surcharge, and additional interest payable before submitting.

Goal:

 Calculate GST late submission penalty, surcharge, and interest charges per IRAS rules.

Prompt:

 "You are a GST compliance calculator. Estimate late submission penalty, 5% surcharge, and additional 2% per month penalty for delayed GST F5 filing in Singapore.

 Include:

  - Late submission penalty (S$200 immediate, capped at S$10,000 per outstanding return)

  - 5% penalty on unpaid tax + additional 2% per month (capped at 50%)

  - Total payable amount"

Inputs Required:

  - Filing frequency (quarterly/monthly)

  - Number of months delayed

  - Net GST payable for each period
