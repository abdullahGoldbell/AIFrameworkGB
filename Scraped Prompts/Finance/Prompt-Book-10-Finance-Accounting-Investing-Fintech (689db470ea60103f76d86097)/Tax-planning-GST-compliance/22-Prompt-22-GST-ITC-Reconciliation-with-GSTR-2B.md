### **Prompt 22 — GST Input Tax Reconciliation Workflow**

Backstory:

 A 29-year-old electronics retailer in Singapore files GST F5 returns quarterly with IRAS but often faces input tax mismatches because suppliers issue tax invoices late, send credit notes, or fail to comply with InvoiceNow / e-invoicing requirements. She wants an automated process to reconcile input tax claimed in her accounting books with supplier tax invoices and the InvoiceNow / Peppol records before she submits each F5.

Goal:

 Create a quarterly GST input tax reconciliation workflow.

Prompt:

 "You are a Singapore GST reconciliation expert. Develop a quarterly process to match input tax in the purchase register against supplier tax invoices and InvoiceNow / Peppol data.

 Include:

  - Identifying mismatched or missing tax invoices and credit notes

  - Communicating with suppliers for corrections before the F5 due date

  - Updating input tax claims and adjustments before submitting GST F5"

Inputs Required:

  - Purchase register for the quarter

  - Supplier tax invoices (PDF / InvoiceNow / Peppol records)

  - Supplier list with UEN and contact details
