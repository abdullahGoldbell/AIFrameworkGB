### **Prompt 12 — Annual GST Reconciliation & Year-End Preparation Plan**

Backstory:

 A 32-year-old GST-registered trader in Singapore needs to perform his first proper year-end GST reconciliation across all four F5 quarters and prepare supporting documentation in case of an IRAS GST audit or ASK self-review. He wants a clear plan to gather all documents and reconcile data before year-end close.

Goal:

 Create a year-end GST reconciliation and Assisted Self-help Kit (ASK) review checklist.

Prompt:

 "You are a GST return expert. Create a step-by-step year-end GST reconciliation checklist for a Singapore GST-registered business.

 Include:

  - Reconciling F5 returns with books and management accounts

  - Checking input tax claimed vs. eligible (per IRAS rules)

  - Reviewing standard-rated, zero-rated, and exempt supplies summary

  - ASK self-review readiness checklist"

Inputs Required:

  - GST F5 returns filed during the financial year

  - Purchase and sales registers

  - Input tax ledger and import GST permits
