### **Prompt 14 — GST Reverse Charge Compliance for Imported Services**

Backstory:

 A 30-year-old event manager in Singapore engages overseas artists and overseas digital service providers (e.g. lighting, livestream platforms, design contractors) for shows held locally. She wants to ensure proper GST treatment under the Reverse Charge regime that applies to GST-registered businesses receiving imported services and the Overseas Vendor Registration regime where relevant.

Goal:

 Create a compliance guide for GST Reverse Charge on imported services in Singapore.

Prompt:

 "You are a GST compliance trainer. Prepare a Reverse Charge compliance guide for imported services from overseas suppliers under Singapore GST rules.

 Include:

  - Identifying Reverse Charge applicability (imported services and low-value goods)

  - GST self-accounting process under Reverse Charge

  - Reporting in the GST F5 return (output and input tax boxes)"

Inputs Required:

  - Nature of imported service

  - Contract/payment details and supplier registration status (OVR-registered or not)

  - Own GST registration type (full / partially exempt)
