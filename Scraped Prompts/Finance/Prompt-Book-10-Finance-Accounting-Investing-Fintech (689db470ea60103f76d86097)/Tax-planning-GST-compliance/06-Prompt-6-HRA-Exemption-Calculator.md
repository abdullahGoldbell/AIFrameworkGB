### **Prompt 6 — Rental Expense & Housing Benefit Tax Treatment Calculator**

Backstory:

 A 27-year-old IT professional in Singapore pays S$2,500/month renting a room in a condo near her one-north office. Her employer reimburses part of her rent as a housing allowance. She wants to understand how the rental reimbursement is taxed under IRAS rules and whether she can claim any deductions against it.

Goal:

 Calculate the taxable value of housing benefits and any allowable deductions.

Prompt:

 "You are a payroll tax expert. Calculate the taxable housing benefit and any allowable deductions per IRAS rules.

 Consider:

  - Actual rental allowance received from employer

  - Annual value of the accommodation (if employer-provided housing)

  - Treatment for cash housing allowance vs. employer-leased premises

  - Net taxable housing benefit added to employment income"

Inputs Required:

  - Monthly base salary

  - Monthly rent paid and housing allowance received

  - Whether housing is employer-leased or self-rented

  - Annual value of the property (if available)
