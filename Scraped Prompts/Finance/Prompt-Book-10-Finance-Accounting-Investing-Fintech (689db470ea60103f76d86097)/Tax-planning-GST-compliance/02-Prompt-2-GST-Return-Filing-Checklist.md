### **Prompt 2 — GST Return Filing Checklist**

Backstory:

 A 32-year-old small business owner in Singapore runs a boutique selling clothes both at a Tampines shopfront and through Instagram. She is GST registered and files quarterly F5 returns but often misses claiming input tax because supplier tax invoices aren't properly tracked. She needs a clear pre-filing checklist to avoid errors.

Goal:

 Create a step-by-step GST F5 return filing checklist.

Prompt:

 "You are a GST compliance expert. Create a checklist for accurate quarterly GST F5 filing in Singapore.

 Include:

  - Verifying sales tax invoices and customer GST registration numbers

  - Matching purchase tax invoices for input tax claims

  - Reconciling output and input tax with books of accounts

  - Reviewing late submission penalties and 5% surcharge applicability"

Inputs Required:

  - GST registration number of the business

  - Sales and purchase invoice lists

  - GST filing frequency (quarterly/monthly)

  - Accounting software used (e.g. Xero, Quickbooks, Financio)
